# FTS5 query syntax: "phrases", AND/OR/NOT, (grouping)
# Lines within a group are concatenated; --- separates groups (score = max across groups)
#
# Column filters: prefix a term with title: or description: to restrict
# matching to that column. Useful for:
#
#   Role titles — scope to title to avoid matching body text mentions:
#     title:"software engineer"    matches "Software Engineer, Platform"
#     title:"research scientist"   matches "Senior Research Scientist"
#
#   Exclusions — title-scoped NOTs are more precise than full-text NOTs:
#     NOT title:manager            excludes "Manager, Applied AI Engineering"
#     NOT title:intern             excludes intern roles without filtering
#                                  jobs that mention "mentoring interns"
#     NOT title:frontend           excludes all frontend title variants
#
#   Domain/technology terms should stay unscoped (match anywhere):
#     "distributed systems"        often in descriptions, not titles
#     CUDA OR TPU                  technology terms in body text

# Group 1: Backend / Platform
(title:"software engineer" OR title:"backend engineer" OR title:"platform engineer")
NOT title:intern NOT title:internship NOT title:manager NOT title:director
---
# Group 2: Infrastructure
("distributed systems" OR microservices OR "API design")
AND ("cloud infrastructure" OR Kubernetes OR "site reliability")
NOT title:intern NOT title:internship NOT title:manager NOT title:director
---
# Group 3: Languages
(Python OR Go OR Java OR PostgreSQL OR Redis OR Kafka)
NOT title:intern NOT title:internship NOT title:manager NOT title:director
---
# Group 4: ML/AI
("machine learning" OR "ML engineering" OR "model deployment" OR PyTorch)
NOT title:intern NOT title:internship NOT title:manager NOT title:director
