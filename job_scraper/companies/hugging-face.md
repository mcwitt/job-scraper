# Hugging Face

## Overview

Open-source AI platform and community hub for sharing, discovering,
and deploying machine learning models, datasets, and applications.
Originally focused on NLP, now spans vision, audio, multimodal, and
robotics. Operates a freemium model with hosted inference, enterprise
subscriptions, and API access.

## Technical Focus

- Open-source model hosting and collaboration (Hub with 1M+ models)
- Transformers library and ecosystem (Diffusers, Tokenizers, etc.)
- LeRobot: open-source robotics (models, datasets, affordable hardware)
- Inference infrastructure (Inference Endpoints, Spaces)
- Open model training and fine-tuning tooling

## Scale & Stage

Private, Series D. Raised $235M Series D in August 2023 at $4.5B
valuation ($396M total). Investors include Google, Amazon, Nvidia,
Intel, AMD, Salesforce, and IBM. ~665 employees. Revenue ~$130M in
2024, up from $70M in 2023.

## Hiring

Engineering-heavy organization spanning ML research, infrastructure,
open-source tooling, and developer experience. Hiring across model
training, inference optimization, robotics (LeRobot), and platform
engineering. Remote-friendly with distributed team.

Strong open-source culture -- many hires are active contributors to
the Hugging Face ecosystem. Emphasis on community engagement and
making ML accessible rather than proprietary research.

## Recent Context

Acquired Pollen Robotics and launched LeRobot, an open-source
robotics initiative including affordable hardware ($100 robotic arm,
$300 robot, ~$3K humanoid). Released SmolVLA robotics model trained
on 10M frames from community datasets. Partnered with Nvidia on
robotics R&D. Small layoff (4%, ~10 people) in February 2025.
