# Airbnb

## Overview

Online marketplace for short-term rentals, experiences, and
travel accommodations. Founded in 2008 by Brian Chesky, Nathan
Blecharczyk, and Joe Gebbia. Headquartered in San Francisco.

## Technical Focus

- Search ranking and recommendation systems
- Machine learning for pricing, fraud detection, and trust
- Generative AI for customer support and conversational search
- Large-scale data infrastructure and real-time systems
- Mobile and web platform engineering (React, Kotlin, Swift)
- Payments, identity verification, and marketplace systems

## Scale & Stage

Public (ABNB). ~$12B trailing revenue (2025). Market cap ~$81B.
~8,200 employees globally. Profitable and cash-flow positive.

## Hiring

Engineering roles span ML, infrastructure, product, and
platform teams. Strong emphasis on applied ML — search ranking,
personalization, and NLP. Systems roles require experience with
distributed systems at scale. Culture values craftsmanship and
cross-functional collaboration. Remote-friendly with hubs in
San Francisco, Seattle, and international offices.

## Recent Context

Investing heavily in AI-native features: automated ~1/3 of
customer support, rolling out AI voice agents globally, and
piloting conversational search. Appointed former Meta AI
executive as CTO. Expanding research presence at top ML
conferences (KDD, CIKM, COLING, VLDB).
