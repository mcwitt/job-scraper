# Black Forest Labs

## Overview

Generative AI company focused on image and video generation
models. Founded by researchers behind Stable Diffusion (ex-
Stability AI, ex-university of Freiburg). Creators of the FLUX
model family. Offices in Freiburg, Germany and San Francisco.

## Technical Focus

- Diffusion and flow-matching models for image generation
- Text-to-image and text-to-video synthesis
- Model architecture research (transformers for generation)
- Inference optimization for generative models
- API and developer platform for media generation

## Scale & Stage

Early-stage startup. Venture-backed (Series A). Small team,
likely <100 employees. Revenue via API access and model
licensing.

## Hiring

Research-heavy team. Looking for ML researchers with
generative modeling experience and engineers who can optimize
large diffusion model training and serving. Academic
publications in computer vision or generative models are valued.
Small team size means broad responsibilities.

## Recent Context

FLUX models competing with Midjourney, DALL-E, and Stable
Diffusion successors. Positioned as the research-driven
alternative in image generation, with open-weight model
releases alongside commercial API offerings.
