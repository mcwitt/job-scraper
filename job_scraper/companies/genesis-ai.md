# Genesis AI

## Overview

Physical AI research lab building universal robotics
foundation models. Founded December 2024 by Zhou Xian
(Carnegie Mellon PhD) and Théophile Gervet (ex-Mistral).
HQ in Austin, TX with offices in Bay Area, Paris, and
London.

## Technical Focus

- World models for robotics and physical AI
- Proprietary simulation engine (alternative to Nvidia)
- Multi-domain robotics foundation models
- Rendering, inference, and compiler infrastructure
- Robot learning and data operations

## Scale & Stage

Private. $105M seed round (July 2025) co-led by Eclipse
and Khosla Ventures, with participation from Bpifrance,
HSG, Eric Schmidt, and Xavier Niel. ~45 employees,
targeting 100 by end of 2025.

## Hiring

Rapidly scaling across research, engineering, robotics,
and operations. Roles include MTS positions in
foundations, robot learning, rendering, inference, and
UI/research tooling. Multi-site hiring across Bay Area,
Paris, and London.

## Recent Context

Emerged from stealth July 2025 with $105M seed — one of
the largest seed rounds for a robotics AI company. Plans
to release its first multi-domain robotics model to the
community. Competes with Figure, Physical Intelligence,
and others in the physical AI space.
