# Bitnomial

## Overview

CFTC-regulated cryptocurrency derivatives exchange and
clearinghouse. Founded 2014 by Luke Hoersten in Chicago.
Offers perpetual futures, options, and futures on crypto
assets with institutional-grade infrastructure.

## Technical Focus

- Haskell backend (matching engine, all core services)
- Low-latency trading systems with type safety
- Functional programming for financial correctness
- Infrastructure as code (Terraform, Nomad)
- Crypto custody and margin systems

## Scale & Stage

Private. Raised ~$49M total from investors including
Ripple, Belvedere Strategic Capital, Collaborative Fund,
and Collab+Currency. Team of ~20-30 employees across
Chicago, SF, NYC, and remote US.

## Hiring

Actively hires Haskell engineers — one of the largest
production Haskell codebases in fintech. Main repo is
66% Haskell, 11% TypeScript, 9% HCL. Strong preference
for functional programming expertise and type-system
fluency. Remote-friendly within the US.

## Recent Context

In late 2025, gained CFTC approval to launch prediction
markets and became the first US regulated exchange to
accept digital assets as native margin collateral. Also
launched a leveraged retail spot crypto exchange in
December 2025, expanding beyond derivatives into spot
trading — significant product surface growth.
