# OpenTeams

## Overview

Open source services and consulting company connecting organizations
with open source maintainers and developers. Founded by Travis
Oliphant (creator of NumPy, SciPy, Numba; founder of Anaconda and
NumFOCUS). Focuses on sustainable funding for open source projects and
providing enterprise support, consulting, and custom development
around open source AI and data science tools.

## Technical Focus

- Open source AI and ML consulting and integration
- Enterprise support for 680+ open source technologies
- Custom development on open source data science stacks
- Open source sustainability and maintainer funding models
- Strategy, migration, and 24/7 support services

## Scale & Stage

Private, early stage. Raised ~$10M total. Acquired Quansight's AI
consulting division in May 2025, including its core engineering team
(Dharhas Pothina joined as CTO). Small team. Based in Austin, Texas.

## Hiring

Small organization built around deep open source expertise. Hiring
open source architects, engineers, and consultants with backgrounds in
the PyData ecosystem and related tools. Strong emphasis on upstream
open source contributions. Expect candidates to have meaningful
involvement in open source communities.

## Recent Context

Acquired Quansight's consulting business in May 2025, significantly
expanding engineering capacity and AI consulting capabilities. Travis
Oliphant continues to lead with a mission of making open source
development financially sustainable for maintainers while providing
enterprise-grade support to organizations.
