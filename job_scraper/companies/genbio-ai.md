# GenBio AI

## Overview

AI company building multiscale foundation models to
decode and simulate human biology. Founded in 2024 by
Fred Hu (CEO), Eric Xing, David Baker, Le Song, Ziv
Bar-Joseph, and Emma Lundberg. Headquartered in Palo
Alto, CA with satellite labs in Paris and Abu Dhabi.

## Technical Focus

- AI-Driven Digital Organism (AIDO) platform
- Multiscale foundation models for DNA, RNA, protein
- Single-cell expression and perturbation modeling
- Protein structure prediction and design
- Drug discovery and personalized medicine

## Scale & Stage

Private, early stage. Funding details not publicly
disclosed. Backed by notable co-founders spanning AI
(Eric Xing, Le Song), structural biology (David Baker,
Nobel laureate), and cell biology (Emma Lundberg).

## Hiring

Research-focused team working across AI and biology.
Global presence (Palo Alto, Paris, Abu Dhabi) enables
access to diverse talent pools. Roles likely span ML
research, computational biology, and bioinformatics.
Strong academic pedigree among founders suggests a
research-intensive culture.

## Recent Context

Released Phase 1 of AIDO (late 2024), the world's
first AI-driven Digital Organism, featuring six models
for DNA, RNA, protein, protein structure, single-cell
expression, and evolutionary information. Presented
five papers at ICML 2025 across multiple workshops.
Appointed Ziv Bar-Joseph as CSO and Emma Lundberg as
Chief Scientific Advisor in 2025.
