# Block

## Overview

Fintech company formerly known as Square, building economic
empowerment tools for individuals and businesses. Ecosystem
includes Square (seller tools), Cash App (consumer finance),
Afterpay (BNPL), TIDAL (music streaming), Bitkey (Bitcoin
wallet), and Proto (Bitcoin mining hardware). Founded in 2009
by Jack Dorsey and Jim McKelvey. HQ in San Francisco.

## Technical Focus

- Payment processing and point-of-sale hardware (Square)
- Consumer finance and peer-to-peer payments (Cash App)
- Buy-now-pay-later and near real-time underwriting
- Bitcoin mining hardware and custom chip design (Proto)
- AI-powered commerce insights (Square AI)
- Large-scale distributed systems and data infrastructure

## Scale & Stage

Public (NYSE: XYZ, formerly SQ). ~6,000 employees after
a major restructuring in February 2026 that cut ~4,000
roles. 2025 gross profit $10.36B (+17% YoY). 2026 guidance:
$12.2B gross profit, $3.2B adjusted operating income (+54%).
58 million Cash App monthly actives. Over $200B in customer
credit issued across Cash App Borrow, Afterpay, and
Square Loans.

## Hiring

Shifted to a leaner, AI-augmented workforce in 2026.
Actively recruiting senior AI engineering talent and
investing in AI infrastructure. Functional org structure
(no traditional GM layer) with engineering, design, and
marketing teams working across the full ecosystem. Known
for a learning-oriented, egoless engineering culture.

## Recent Context

Cut ~40% of staff in February 2026 while posting record
profitability, replacing many roles with AI. Launched
second-gen Square Register (Feb 2026) with faster
performance and IP54 rating. Rolled out Square AI in the
UK as a free conversational assistant for merchants. CEO
Jack Dorsey retook hands-on leadership as "Block Head,"
centering 2026 strategy on AI integration and Bitcoin.
