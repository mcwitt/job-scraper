# SandboxAQ

## Overview

Enterprise AI and quantum technology company spun out of Alphabet in 2022.
Builds large quantitative models (LQMs) that combine AI with advanced
mathematics, simulation, and quantum computing for applications in
cybersecurity, drug discovery, and materials science. Named to TIME100 Most
Influential Companies 2025.

## Technical Focus

- Post-quantum cryptography (AQtive Guard: cryptographic discovery and migration)
- AI-driven drug discovery (AQBioSim: molecular simulation)
- Large quantitative models (LQMs) for scientific computing
- Quantum sensing and navigation systems

## Scale & Stage

Private, Series E. Closed $450M Series E with investors including Google,
Nvidia, Ray Dalio, and BNP Paribas. Valued at $5.75B. Total funding ~$950M.
~350 employees. Government and enterprise customer base including DoD
contracts.

## Hiring

Interdisciplinary team spanning quantum physics, cryptography, computational
chemistry, and ML engineering. Hires researchers and engineers with
backgrounds in post-quantum cryptography, molecular simulation, applied
mathematics, or scientific computing. Government security clearances relevant
for defense-related roles.

The broad technical surface area (crypto, pharma, materials, quantum) means
diverse hiring needs. Strong publication record or domain expertise in one of
their verticals is valued alongside software engineering fundamentals.

## Recent Context

Google and Nvidia invested $150M to expand the Series E to $450M at $5.75B
valuation. Secured a five-year DoD contract for AQtive Guard post-quantum
cryptography migration. AQBioSim landed a potential $200M pharma partnership
with MapLight Therapeutics for CNS disorder treatments (December 2025).
