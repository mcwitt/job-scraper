# Stitch Fix

## Overview

Online personal styling service combining data science
and human stylists to deliver personalized clothing.
Founded in 2011 by Katrina Lake. Headquartered in
San Francisco, CA.

## Technical Focus

- Recommendation and styling algorithms at scale
- Machine learning for inventory, demand forecasting,
  and merchandising
- LLM integration alongside traditional ML pipelines
- Computer vision for style and fit matching
- Full-stack data science (end-to-end model ownership)
- Logistics and fulfillment platform engineering

## Scale & Stage

Public (SFIX). ~$1.3B annual revenue. ~4,300 employees.
IPO in 2017. Operates warehouses across the US and UK.

## Hiring

Engineering and data science are core functions. "Full-
stack data scientist" model — scientists own problems
end-to-end from research to production. Roles span ML,
platform, fulfillment, and product engineering. Remote-
friendly with offices in San Francisco, Austin, and
other US locations.

## Recent Context

Pioneered the full-stack data science role; algorithms
touch nearly every business function. Published
research via Multithreaded tech blog and algorithms
tour. Continuing to integrate generative AI and LLMs
into styling and recommendation workflows.
