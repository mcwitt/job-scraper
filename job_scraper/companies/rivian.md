# Rivian

## Overview

Electric vehicle manufacturer building adventure-oriented EVs and
commercial delivery vans. Public company (NASDAQ: RIVN). Produces the
R1T pickup truck, R1S SUV, and Amazon delivery vans. Headquartered in
Irvine, CA with manufacturing in Normal, IL and a major joint venture
with Volkswagen Group for software-defined vehicle technology.

## Technical Focus

- Electric vehicle platform and powertrain engineering
- Software-defined vehicle architecture (Rivian-VW joint venture)
- Autonomous driving and ADAS (zonal controllers, cloud connectivity)
- In-vehicle OS, UI/UX, and connected services
- Cloud infrastructure and distributed systems for fleet management

## Scale & Stage

Public (NASDAQ: RIVN). Raised $10.5B+ total including IPO. VW
investing up to $5B through 2026 via joint venture for electrical
architecture and software. ~15,200 employees. $6.1B cash on hand at
end of 2025. New Atlanta headquarters opening with 500 employees
planned by end of 2026.

## Hiring

Large engineering organization spanning hardware and software. Hiring
across embedded systems, cloud platforms, mobile development, ADAS,
cybersecurity, and vehicle controls. The Rivian-VW joint venture is a
separate hiring entity focused on software-defined vehicle platforms.

Palo Alto office focuses on software stack and vehicle electronics.
Expect a mix of automotive and tech culture. Software roles pay
$120K-$220K for engineers.

## Recent Context

Rivian-VW Group Technology joint venture is scaling up, addressing
shared challenges in software-defined vehicles including operating
systems, zonal controllers, and cloud connectivity. Spun out Mind
Robotics in early 2026, which raised $500M Series A for industrial
AI-powered robots. Production volumes growing but the company remains
unprofitable, with significant cash runway from VW partnership.
