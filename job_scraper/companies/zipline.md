# Zipline

## Overview

Drone delivery company operating the world's largest autonomous
delivery network. Founded in 2014, initially focused on medical supply
delivery in Rwanda and Ghana, now expanding into commercial last-mile
delivery in the US. Designs, manufactures, and operates its own drone
fleet and logistics infrastructure end-to-end.

## Technical Focus

- Autonomous fixed-wing drone design and manufacturing
- Platform 2: VTOL drones for last-mile delivery (70 mph, 8 lb payload,
  10 mi radius) with precision wire-lowering delivery system
- Logistics software for autonomous fleet management and routing
- Regulatory compliance and airspace integration at scale
- Distribution hub design and autonomous recharging infrastructure

## Scale & Stage

Private, late-stage. Raised $600M in January 2026 at $7.6B valuation
from Tiger Global, Fidelity, Baillie Gifford, and Valor Equity. Total
funding exceeds $1B. ~1,475 employees. Headquartered in South San
Francisco. Operations in 10+ countries.

## Hiring

Engineering-heavy organization spanning aerospace, robotics, software,
and operations. Hiring across embedded systems, flight controls,
perception, logistics optimization, and hardware engineering. Also
hiring for ground operations as US delivery footprint grows.

Unique culture combining aerospace precision with startup speed.
Hardware-software integration is central -- engineers work across the
full stack from airframe design to fleet management software.

## Recent Context

Surpassed 2 million commercial drone deliveries in January 2026. US
deliveries growing ~15% week-over-week for seven months. Launched
Platform 2 deliveries for Walmart in Dallas-Fort Worth (April 2025).
Expanding to Houston and Phoenix in 2026. Secured $150M US State
Department agreement for drone hub construction in Africa. Ranked on
CNBC Disruptor 50 in 2025.
