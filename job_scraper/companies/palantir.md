# Palantir Technologies

## Overview

Data analytics and AI platform company building software for
defense, intelligence, and commercial sectors. Founded in 2003
by Peter Thiel, Alex Karp (CEO), Stephen Cohen, Joe Lonsdale,
and Nathan Gettings. Originally based in Palo Alto; relocated
HQ to Denver in 2020, then to Miami in early 2026.

## Technical Focus

- Data fusion platforms: Gotham (defense/intel), Foundry
  (enterprise data integration), Apollo (continuous delivery)
- Artificial Intelligence Platform (AIP) for operationalizing
  LLMs and AI on enterprise and government data
- Ontology-based data modeling and knowledge graphs
- Secure multi-tenant deployment across cloud, on-prem, and
  edge environments
- Real-time decision intelligence and workflow automation

## Scale & Stage

Public (NYSE: PLTR). ~4,400 employees. FY 2025 revenue
$4.48B, up 56% YoY. Q4 2025 revenue $1.41B, up 70% YoY
with U.S. commercial revenue surging 137%. Guiding FY 2026
revenue of ~$7.2B (~61% YoY growth). 954 customers as of
Q4 2025.

## Hiring

Highly selective (~1-2% acceptance rate for new grad roles).
Interview process averages ~28 days with technical and
case-style assessments. Mission-driven culture emphasizing
intellectual rigor, ownership, and autonomy. Primarily
in-person with some hybrid flexibility. Engineering roles
span data infrastructure, platform, and forward-deployed
(embedded with customers) positions.

## Recent Context

Secured $448M U.S. Navy "ShipOS" contract for shipbuilding
supply chain modernization (late 2025). Partnered with
Rackspace, Databricks, Cognizant, and HD Hyundai to expand
AIP and Foundry adoption. AIP Bootcamp program (five-day
workshops) driving ~75% customer conversion rate. Showcased
cross-industry AI deployments at AIPCon 9 (March 2026).
