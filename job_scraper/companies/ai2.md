# AI2 (Allen Institute for AI)

## Overview

Non-profit AI research institute founded by Paul Allen in 2014.
Focused on high-impact AI research for the common good. Known
for open-source contributions to NLP and language modeling.
Based in Seattle.

## Technical Focus

- Open language models (OLMo, OLMo 2)
- Semantic Scholar (academic search engine)
- NLP research and tooling (AllenNLP legacy)
- Open datasets and benchmarks
- Responsible AI development

## Scale & Stage

Non-profit research institute. ~300 employees. Funded by the
Paul G. Allen estate and grants. Operates more like an academic
lab than a startup — publishes openly and releases models and
data under permissive licenses.

## Hiring

Research-oriented hiring. Strong publication record valued for
research scientist roles. Engineering roles support research
infrastructure — model training pipelines, data processing,
web platforms (Semantic Scholar). Compensation competitive for
non-profit but below Big Tech and well-funded startups.

Good fit for researchers who want to publish freely and work
on open-source AI without commercial pressure.

## Recent Context

Major push on open language models with OLMo series, providing
fully open training data, code, and weights. Positioned as the
leading open-science alternative to closed frontier labs.
