# Opendoor

## Overview

Online platform for buying and selling residential
real estate with instant cash offers. Founded in 2014
by Eric Wu. Headquartered in San Francisco / Tempe, AZ.

## Technical Focus

- AI/ML for home pricing and valuation models
- Agentic AI and automation for operations
- Full-stack product engineering (Go, SQL, TypeScript)
- Data infrastructure and analytics platforms
- Mortgage and fintech systems
- Computer vision for property assessment

## Scale & Stage

Public (OPEN). ~$4.4B trailing revenue. ~1,400
employees. Went public via SPAC in 2020. Operates
in major US metro markets.

## Hiring

Engineering roles span AI/ML, platform, pricing,
and product. Strong focus on agentic AI and
automation — roles like "Agentic Analytics Engineer"
and "AI Solutions Engineer." Data science central to
pricing engine. Offices in San Francisco, Tempe,
Seattle, Miami, and Toronto.

## Recent Context

Pursuing "Opendoor 2.0" strategy focused on reaching
adjusted net income profitability by end of 2026.
Investing heavily in AI-powered marketing and
operations to achieve 2-3x productivity gains.
Q4 2025 earnings signaled strategic shift toward
leaner, AI-driven operations.
