# Schrodinger

## Overview

Physics-based computational platform for molecular discovery and design,
serving both life sciences (drug discovery) and materials science. Combines
quantum mechanics simulations with machine learning to predict molecular
properties and accelerate the design of new compounds.

## Technical Focus

- Physics-based molecular simulation (FEP+, Glide, WaterMap)
- Machine learning force fields (MPNICE)
- Structure-based and ligand-based drug design
- Materials science modeling and discovery
- Predictive toxicology solutions

## Scale & Stage

Public (NASDAQ: SDGR). ~900 employees across 12+ global offices,
headquartered in New York. FY2025 revenue of $256M (up 23%), with $200M
from software licensing and $56M from drug discovery collaborations.

## Hiring

Strong preference for computational chemistry, physics, and ML
backgrounds. Roles span platform engineering, scientific research, and
drug discovery. Known for deep technical interviews testing understanding
of molecular simulation methods and statistical mechanics.

Collaborative research culture with academic-style publication record
(20+ peer-reviewed papers in 2025). Active partnerships with Lilly,
Otsuka, and Ajax Therapeutics provide exposure to real drug discovery
programs.

## Recent Context

Revenue jumped 54% in Q3 2025 driven by expanded pharma collaborations.
Plans to present Phase 1 data for SGR-3515 in solid tumors in H1 2026
and is seeking development partners for clinical programs. Launching a
