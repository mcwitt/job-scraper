# Persefoni

## Overview

Persefoni provides an AI-driven carbon accounting and
climate management platform that helps enterprises
measure, manage, and report their carbon footprint.
Founded in 2020 by CEO Kentaro Kawamori, the company is
headquartered in Tempe, AZ. Persefoni supports
enterprise-grade carbon accounting as well as a free
self-serve tier (Persefoni Pro) for SMBs.

## Technical Focus

- AI-powered carbon footprint measurement and reporting
- Enterprise GHG accounting (Scope 1, 2, 3)
- Product carbon footprint / life cycle assessment
- Audit-ready controls and compliance workflows
- Self-serve analytics and reporting builder

## Scale & Stage

Private, Series C. Raised $179M total including a $23M
Series C extension (Apr 2025) with TPG Rise, Clearvision
Ventures, and NGP Energy Technology Partners. Previous
$50M round in Aug 2023. Approximately 200-300 employees.
Persefoni Pro has 6,000+ organic sign-ups since launch.
Founded 2020.

## Hiring

Persefoni hires across software engineering, data
science, product management, and sustainability domain
expertise. The platform requires knowledge of carbon
accounting standards (GHG Protocol, CSRD, SEC climate
rules) combined with strong technical skills. The
company is targeting profitability and emphasizes
efficient scaling.

## Recent Context

Persefoni raised $23M in April 2025 to enhance its
carbon accounting software and drive toward profitability
by late 2025. The company launched product carbon
footprint and LCA capabilities and expanded its
analytics tooling. With over 6,000 self-serve sign-ups,
Persefoni is building a land-and-expand motion from
SMBs to enterprise accounts.
