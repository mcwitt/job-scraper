# Eikon Therapeutics

## Overview

Clinical-stage biopharma using Nobel Prize-winning
super-resolution microscopy to discover novel cancer
treatments. Founded 2019 by Eric Betzig (2014 Nobel in
Chemistry), Robert Tjian, Luke Lavis, and Xavier Darzacq.
CEO: Roger M. Perlmutter, M.D., Ph.D. HQ in
Hayward/Millbrae, CA with offices in Jersey City, NJ.

## Technical Focus

- Super-resolution microscopy and live-cell protein dynamics
- High-performance computing for drug discovery
- TLR7/8 co-agonists (EIK1001, lead program, Phase 2/3)
- Selective PARP1 inhibitors and WRN helicase inhibitors
- Oncology pipeline: melanoma, NSCLC, breast, ovarian,
  prostate, pancreatic cancers

## Scale & Stage

Public (NASDAQ: EIKN). IPO February 2026 raising ~$381M
at $18/share. Previously raised $1.1B+ privately including
$350.7M Series D (Feb 2025). ~384 employees. Market cap
~$743M.

## Hiring

Roles span clinical operations, research, engineering
(hardware, software, data science), legal, finance, and
regulatory. Clinical trials across 28 countries on 5
continents. Strong computational biology and microscopy
engineering culture.

## Recent Context

IPO in Feb 2026 was one of the largest biotech IPOs since
2024. Phase 2/3 trial of EIK1001 + Keytruda in melanoma
underway, first interim analysis expected H2 2026. NSCLC
trial dosing expected H2 2026.
