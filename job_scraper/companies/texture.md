# Texture

## Overview
Texture is an AI-native platform for coordinating energy
operations across distributed energy resources (DERs) —
often described as "Plaid for energy." Founded August 2023
by Sanjiv Sanghavi (co-founder of ClassPass), Victor Quinn,
and Nicholas Alan Brown. HQ in New York, NY.

## Technical Focus
- Standardizes energy data into shared models across DERs
  (solar, wind, batteries, EV chargers, thermostats)
- Real-time data aggregation and API integrations with
  multiple device manufacturers
- Applies logic continuously and routes outputs across
  systems and devices
- Compliance reporting, preventative maintenance, batch
  device control
- Supports ~85% of distributed energy resources on market
