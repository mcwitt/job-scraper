# Eos Energy

## Overview

Eos Energy Enterprises designs and manufactures zinc-based battery energy
storage systems for grid-scale and commercial applications. The company's
non-lithium chemistry offers long-duration storage (4-16+ hours) with
non-flammable, recyclable components, positioning it as an alternative to
lithium-ion for stationary storage.

## Technical Focus

- Zinc-based aqueous battery chemistry (Z3 platform)
- Indensity modular battery architecture (1 GWh/acre density)
- Long-duration energy storage (4-16+ hours)
- Non-flammable, recyclable battery systems
- Manufacturing scale-up and process engineering

## Scale & Stage

Public (NASDAQ: EOSE). Q4 2025 revenue of $58M (90% QoQ increase), 7x
YoY revenue growth. FY2026 revenue guidance of $300M-$400M. Expanding
manufacturing in Pennsylvania to 8 GWh annual capacity. Backed by a
$398.6M DOE loan guarantee for production line construction.

## Hiring

Eos hires across battery engineering, manufacturing, and software roles.
Technical positions focus on electrochemistry, battery management systems,
manufacturing process engineering, and controls. The company is scaling
rapidly from pilot to high-volume production, creating demand for
manufacturing engineers and operations talent.

Given the move to Pennsylvania and the manufacturing ramp, candidates
with experience in battery or process manufacturing scale-up are
particularly relevant. The company also hires software engineers for
battery management and monitoring systems. Data center applications are
an emerging focus area.

## Recent Context

In January 2026, Eos launched Indensity, a modular zinc battery system
achieving 4x the energy density per acre of competing technologies,
targeting data centers and urban infrastructure. The company is investing
$352.9M to expand manufacturing and relocate its headquarters from New
Jersey to Pittsburgh, PA, with DOE loan guarantee support for 8 GWh
annual production capacity.
