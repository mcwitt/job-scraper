# Commonwealth Fusion Systems

## Overview

Fusion energy company using high-field superconducting
magnets to build compact tokamak reactors. Founded in
2018 as an MIT spinout. CEO Bob Mumgaard. Headquartered
in Devens, Massachusetts.

## Technical Focus

- High-temperature superconducting (HTS) magnet design
- Compact tokamak fusion reactor (SPARC / ARC)
- Plasma physics and confinement optimization
- AI-powered digital twins (with Siemens and NVIDIA)
- Fusion power plant engineering and scale-up

## Scale & Stage

Private. Nearly $3B total raised, including $863M Series
B2 (Aug 2025) with NVIDIA participation. Valued over $10B.
Hundreds of employees across Devens campus. Pre-revenue;
SPARC demonstration reactor under construction.

## Hiring

Large and rapidly growing team spanning superconducting
magnet engineering, plasma physics, mechanical and
electrical engineering, and software. Culture rooted in
MIT research with commercial urgency. High technical bar.

## Recent Context

Installed first 24-ton D-shaped toroidal field magnet in
SPARC (Jan 2026). All 18 magnets to be installed by
summer 2026. First plasma expected 2027. Announced
Siemens + NVIDIA partnership for AI digital twin of SPARC
at CES 2026. First commercial plant (ARC, 400 MW) planned
for Richmond, VA in early 2030s.
