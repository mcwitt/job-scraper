# Skild AI

## Overview

Robotics AI company building the first unified foundation model for
robots, called the Skild Brain. The model is "omni-bodied" — it can
control any robot form factor (humanoids, quadrupeds, arms, mobile
manipulators) without prior knowledge of specific body configurations.

## Technical Focus

- Unified robotics foundation model (Skild Brain)
- Omni-bodied robot control across form factors
- Pre-training on internet video and physics simulations
- Sim-to-real transfer learning
- Deployment across security, delivery, warehouse, manufacturing, construction

## Scale & Stage

Private. Series C of $1.4B (Jan 2026) led by SoftBank at $14B+
valuation, up from $4.5B seven months prior. Total funding well over
$2B. Investors include Nvidia NVentures, Jeff Bezos, Sequoia, Coatue,
Lightspeed, Samsung, LG, Salesforce Ventures. Pittsburgh-based. Revenue
grew from zero to ~$30M in a few months in 2025.
