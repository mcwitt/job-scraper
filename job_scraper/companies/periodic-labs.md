# Periodic Labs

## Overview

AI research lab building autonomous "AI scientists" that can conduct
physical experiments, collect data, and iterate to discover new materials.
Founded by Liam Fedus (former VP of Research at OpenAI, helped create
ChatGPT) and Ekin Dogus Cubuk (former Google Brain/DeepMind materials
and chemistry lead, creator of GNoME).

## Technical Focus

- Foundation models for scientific discovery
- Autonomous materials synthesis labs (robotic powder synthesis)
- AI-driven materials design and optimization
- Superconductor discovery (initial focus area)
- Synthetic data scaling for scientific AI

## Scale & Stage

Private, seed stage. Raised $300M in a record seed round (September 2025)
led by Andreessen Horowitz, with Felicis, DST Global, Accel, and NVIDIA
NVentures. Individual backers include Jeff Bezos, Eric Schmidt, Jeff
Dean, and Elad Gil. Early-stage team with exceptional pedigree.

## Hiring

Extremely high bar given founder caliber (OpenAI VP of Research,
DeepMind materials lead). Roles span ML research, robotics/automation,
materials science, and infrastructure engineering. Expect candidates
with top-tier research backgrounds or exceptional engineering skills.

Culture is research-lab meets startup, focused on long-horizon
scientific breakthroughs (Moore's Law, fusion, space travel). The
massive seed round provides runway to hire aggressively and build
both software and physical lab infrastructure.

## Recent Context

Emerged from stealth in September 2025 with a record-breaking $300M
seed round. Initial focus on discovering new superconductors that
outperform existing materials. Building autonomous powder synthesis
labs where robots conduct physical experiments guided by AI models
that learn and improve iteratively.
