# Together AI

## Overview

AI cloud platform company providing infrastructure for training,
fine-tuning, and serving open-source models. Combines a research lab
building open models with a cloud platform optimized for fast,
cost-efficient inference. Founded by Stanford researchers.

## Technical Focus

- GPU cloud infrastructure for model training and inference
- Open-source model development and optimization
- Inference optimization and serving at scale
- Data transformation and structuring (via Refuel.ai acquisition)
- Custom data center operations (Maryland, Memphis)

## Scale & Stage

Private, Series B ($305M in February 2025). Valued at $3.3B. Total
funding ~$534M. ~310 employees. Annualized revenue ~$300M as of
September 2025, up from $130M at end of 2024. Investors include General
Catalyst, Salesforce Ventures, NVIDIA, Kleiner Perkins.

## Hiring

Growing engineering team focused on systems, infrastructure, and ML
optimization. Values candidates with experience in GPU programming,
distributed systems, and ML serving infrastructure. Research roles
require strong open-source contributions or publications.

Lean team relative to revenue, suggesting high individual productivity
expectations. San Francisco headquartered. Acquired Refuel.ai in May
2025, indicating interest in data engineering and structured data
extraction talent.

## Recent Context

Raised $305M Series B in early 2025, more than doubling valuation to
$3.3B. Revenue grew rapidly to ~$300M annualized by September 2025.
Expanding own data center footprint with facilities in Maryland (live
July 2025) and Memphis, and acquired Refuel.ai for data capabilities.
