# Magic

## Overview

AI coding company building frontier-scale code models aimed at creating
an AI software engineering coworker, not just a copilot. Pursuing a path
to AGI through automating AI research and code generation. Based in San
Francisco, founded in 2022 by Sebastian De Ro and Eric Steinberger.

## Technical Focus

- Ultra-long context models (up to 100M tokens at inference)
- Frontier-scale pre-training for code understanding
- Domain-specific reinforcement learning for coding tasks
- Inference-time compute scaling
- Repository-scale code completion and generation

## Scale & Stage

Private, Series C. Total funding ~$466M, including a $320M round in
2024 backed by Eric Schmidt, Alphabet's CapitalG, Atlassian, Jane
Street, Sequoia, Nat Friedman, and Daniel Gross. ~100 employees.
Competing with Anysphere (Cursor), Cognition (Devin), Codeium, Poolside,
and Augment in the AI coding space.

## Hiring

Small, research-heavy team (~100 people) focused on pushing the
frontier of code AI. Likely prioritizes candidates with deep expertise
in large-scale model training, long-context architectures, and
reinforcement learning. The team is lean, so expect high ownership and
breadth of responsibility.

Given the AGI-oriented mission and research intensity, strong
publications or demonstrated expertise in relevant ML areas are likely
valued. Systems engineering for training and inference at scale is also
critical given the 100M-token context ambitions.

## Recent Context

Raised $320M in 2024 from prominent investors including Eric Schmidt
and Sequoia. Competing in the rapidly growing AI coding market with a
