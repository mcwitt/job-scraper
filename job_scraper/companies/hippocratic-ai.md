# Hippocratic AI

## Overview

Healthcare AI company building clinically safe
patient-facing AI agents powered by LLMs. Founded 2023
by Munjal Shah. HQ in Palo Alto, CA.

## Technical Focus

- Large language models fine-tuned for healthcare
- Non-diagnostic patient-facing clinical AI agents
- Speech technologies and conversational AI
- Clinical safety and compliance frameworks
- Healthcare system integrations (EHR, pharma)

## Scale & Stage

Private. $126M Series C (Nov 2025) at $3.5B valuation
led by Avenir Growth. Total funding ~$404M across seed,
Series A ($53M), Series B ($141M, Kleiner Perkins), and
Series C. Investors include a16z, General Catalyst,
CapitalG, and major health systems.

## Hiring

Onsite 5 days/week in Palo Alto. Roles span engineering,
applied research, speech technologies, SRE/cloud infra,
medical affairs, regulatory, and sales. Strong emphasis
on clinical safety — agents cannot prescribe or diagnose.

## Recent Context

Over 50 health system, payor, and pharma partnerships
across 6 countries. Completed 115M+ clinical patient
interactions with no safety incidents. Rapid valuation
growth from $500M (Series A) to $3.5B (Series C) in
under two years.
