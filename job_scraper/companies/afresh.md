# Afresh

## Overview

AI-powered fresh food management platform for grocery
retailers, optimizing ordering, forecasting, and
inventory to reduce food waste. Founded in 2017 by
Matt Schwartz, Volodymyr Kuleshov, Nathan Fenner, and
Sha Sebastian. Headquartered in San Francisco, CA.

## Technical Focus

- ML demand forecasting for perishable goods at
  item level
- Optimization algorithms for store ordering and
  inventory management
- iPad-based store operations applications
- Data infrastructure ingesting millions of grocery
  data points daily
- Applied science research (published at ICML)
- Full-stack web and mobile engineering

## Scale & Stage

Private, Series B ($199M total funding). Investors
include Spark Capital, Innovation Endeavors, and
Threshold. ~100-200 employees. Deployed in thousands
of grocery stores across the US.

## Hiring

Engineering roles span ML, applied science,
infrastructure, and product. Strong applied ML focus
— forecasting, optimization, and operations research.
Product roles tied to iPad ordering and inventory
tools. Remote-friendly across many US states and
Ontario, Canada.

## Recent Context

Prevented over 44M pounds of food waste through
grocer partnerships. Stores using Afresh reduce food
waste by 25%+ on average. Continuing expansion across
regional and national grocery chains.
