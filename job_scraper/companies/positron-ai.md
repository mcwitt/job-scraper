# Positron AI

## Overview

AI inference hardware startup building purpose-built
silicon to challenge Nvidia. Co-founded 2023 by Thomas
Sohmers (CTO, ex-Groq) and Edward Kmett (Chief
Scientist). CEO Mitesh Agrawal (ex-Lambda COO) joined
Feb 2025. Based in the US.

## Technical Focus

- Custom inference-optimized silicon (Atlas, Asimov)
- Energy-efficient AI inference at scale
- 3.5x better perf-per-dollar vs. Nvidia H100
- 66% lower power consumption than H100
- Chip design, verification, and ML optimization

## Scale & Stage

Private, Series B. Raised ~$300M total: $12.5M seed,
$51.6M Series A (Jul 2025), $230M Series B (Feb 2026)
at $1B+ valuation. Investors include Valor Equity, Jump
Trading, Arm, and Qatar Investment Authority. Team has
taped out 100+ chips collectively.

## Hiring

Interdisciplinary team spanning chip design, ML systems,
functional programming, and silicon manufacturing. High
bar — CTO built multiple chips before age 30, chief
scientist is a well-known Haskell contributor. Likely
scaling aggressively with $300M in capital for silicon
engineering, software, and ML roles.

## Recent Context

Shipping Atlas, its first-gen inference product, built
in 18 months on just $12.5M seed funding. Next-gen
custom silicon (Asimov) targets tape-out late 2026 with
production early 2027. Raised $230M Series B in Feb 2026
at unicorn valuation, positioning as a serious Nvidia
inference competitor.
