# Omnidian

## Overview

Omnidian provides solar and battery storage asset
performance management, offering monitoring, maintenance
coordination, and performance guarantees for residential
and commercial systems. Founded in 2016 by CEO Mark
Liffmann, the company is headquartered in Seattle, WA,
with international operations in Australia.

## Technical Focus

- Proprietary analytics for solar system monitoring
- Performance assurance (95% energy guarantee)
- Automated issue detection and remediation workflows
- Residential and commercial solar O&M coordination
- Battery storage performance management

## Scale & Stage

Private, Series C. Raised $172M total including an $87M
Series C (Apr 2025, led by B Capital). Manages 3+ GW
of solar and battery storage systems. Approximately
$46M annual revenue. Also received investment from
Marunouchi Innovation Partners (Apr 2025). Founded 2016.

## Hiring

Omnidian hires across software engineering, data
science, solar operations, and customer support. The
platform requires expertise in energy analytics, IoT
monitoring systems, and field service coordination.
The company's performance guarantee model means
engineering decisions directly impact financial
outcomes. Growing team with a mission-driven culture.

## Recent Context

Omnidian raised an $87M Series C in April 2025 to
fuel growth. In July 2025, the company acquired
Sunnova Energy's residential solar installation
business. In February 2026, Omnidian was selected by
Renewbrook Energy to manage solar systems acquired
from PosiGen. The company continues to scale as a
leading third-party solar asset management platform
with 3+ GW under management.
