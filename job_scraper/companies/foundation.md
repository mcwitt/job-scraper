# Foundation

## Overview

Humanoid robotics startup building the Phantom
general-purpose humanoid robot for industrial and defense
applications. Founded in May 2023 by Sankaet Pathak
(ex-Synapse CEO). Headquartered in San Francisco with an
AI research office in Munich, Germany.

## Technical Focus

- Full-stack humanoid robot design (hardware + software)
- Proprietary rolling contact gearbox actuators (90-95%
  energy efficiency vs 50-60% for harmonic drives)
- Hybrid AI: state-based models + imitation learning
- Autonomous locomotion, manipulation, and navigation
- Robot control and physics-aware ML (Munich AI team)

## Scale & Stage

Private. Raised ~$21M total including $11M pre-seed (Aug
2024, led by Tribe Capital). Reportedly seeking $500M at
a $3B valuation as of early 2026. Holds $24M in US
military research contracts (Army, Navy, Air Force).
Plans to ship 40 robots in 2025, 10k in 2026, and 40k+
in 2027.

## Hiring

Hiring across robotics engineering, ML/AI research, and
operations in San Francisco and Munich. Munich AI team
led by Prof. Patrick van der Smagt (ex-TU Munich).
Technical bar is high -- team draws from IHMC and TU
Munich robotics groups. Roles span mechanical
engineering, embedded systems, ML for robot control,
and perception. Hardcore execution culture emphasized.

## Recent Context

Sent two Phantom MK-1 units to Ukraine in Feb 2026 for
frontline reconnaissance testing. Pursuing expanded US
military contracts including Marine Corps breaching
operations. Phantom MK-1 stands 5'11", weighs ~180 lbs,
and is camera-based (no LIDAR).
