# Rippling

## Overview

Workforce management platform unifying HR, IT, and finance
on a single source of truth for employee data. Integrates
with 500+ third-party apps. Products span payroll, benefits,
device management, identity/access, expense management,
corporate cards, headcount planning, and global EOR/contractor
services. Founded in 2016 by Parker Conrad (CEO) and Prasanna
Sankar. Headquartered in San Francisco, CA.

## Technical Focus

- Full-stack web platform (Python, Django, React, MongoDB)
- Backend services in Golang for performance-critical paths
- Analytics infrastructure (Trino, Apache Pinot)
- Global payroll engine across 50+ countries
- Identity and access management / MDM for IT automation
- AI-powered features (Talent Signal for performance
  analysis, intelligent workflow automation)

## Scale & Stage

Private, Series G ($450M at $16.8B valuation, May 2025).
Total funding ~$1.85B across 10 rounds. Investors include
Kleiner Perkins, Founders Fund, Sequoia, and Greenoaks.
Estimated ~$570M ARR as of early 2025, growing 30%+ YoY.
Approximately 7,000 employees. Offices in San Francisco,
New York, Dublin (European HQ), Sydney, Bangalore, and
London.

## Hiring

Engineering-led culture where managers are expected to be
strong individual contributors. Engineers own products end
to end with high autonomy. Hybrid model with 3+ days/week
in office. Stack emphasizes full-stack generalists across
Python/Django and React. Known for fast pace and a bias
toward shipping. Named to Forbes best startup employers.

## Recent Context

Launched Rippling Travel (corporate travel booking, Jul
2025) and employee scheduling (fastest product to $1M in
sales). Introduced Contractor of Record services in 48
countries. Named to CNBC Disruptor 50 list (Jun 2025).
Embroiled in high-profile trade-secret lawsuit against
competitor Deel; DOJ opened a criminal investigation into
the allegations. Federal court cleared case to proceed
toward trial in Feb 2026.
