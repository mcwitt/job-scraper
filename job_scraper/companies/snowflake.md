# Snowflake

## Overview

Cloud data platform providing data warehousing, data lakes, data
engineering, and data sharing as a unified service. Originally known
for separating storage and compute in cloud analytics, now expanding
aggressively into AI with Cortex (LLM services, vector search, AI
agents) and positioning as the platform where enterprises build
AI-native applications on top of governed data.

## Technical Focus

- Cloud data warehouse with separated storage and compute
- Cortex AI: embedded LLM services, vector search, model deployment
- Cortex Code: AI coding agent for data engineering workflows
- Snowflake Intelligence: AI-native analytics and workflows
- Apache Iceberg open table format support
- Cross-cloud data sharing and marketplace

## Scale & Stage

Public (NYSE: SNOW). ~8,200 employees. Trailing 12-month revenue of
~$4.7B (FY ending Jan 2026). Product revenue growing ~30% YoY. Major
enterprise customer base with expanding AI workloads. Guiding FY2027
product revenue at ~$5.7B.

## Hiring

Engineering-heavy culture with high expectations and fast pace. Active
hiring in AI/ML engineering, data platform, and infrastructure roles.
Expanded partnerships with Anthropic, OpenAI ($200M expansion), and
Google Cloud, signaling heavy investment in AI capabilities. Expect
strong data engineering and systems backgrounds.

## Recent Context

Acquired Observe (AI-powered observability) for ~$1B in January 2026,
its largest acquisition. Also acquired Crunchy Data (PostgreSQL) for
~$250M and TensorStax (AI data pipelines) in 2025-2026. Launched
Cortex Code in February 2026. Competing directly with Databricks for
AI-on-data workloads, with both companies aggressively acquiring
AI-focused assets.
