# Elicit

## Overview

AI-powered research assistant that helps researchers search, summarize, and
extract data from scientific literature. Covers 125M+ papers and is used by
400,000+ researchers monthly at institutions like Harvard, MIT, Stanford, and
companies like Genentech and Novartis. Public benefit corporation spun out
of the non-profit Ought.

## Technical Focus

- Large-scale scientific literature search and retrieval
- AI-powered paper summarization and data extraction
- Evidence-based reasoning and synthesis across papers
- LLM application engineering for research workflows

## Scale & Stage

Private, Series A. Raised $22M Series A in February 2026 at $100M valuation
(led by Spark Capital and Footwork). ~30-45 employees. Public benefit
corporation. ~$18M revenue in 2025. 2M+ total users.

## Hiring

Small, mission-driven team focused on applying AI to improve scientific
reasoning. Values experience with LLM applications, information retrieval,
NLP, and product engineering. The PBC structure attracts candidates motivated
by research impact and epistemic quality over pure growth metrics.

Engineering roles span ML/AI, full-stack product development, and data
infrastructure. Expanding beyond academia into enterprise and industry
research use cases with the Series A funding.
