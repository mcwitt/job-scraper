# Inflection AI

## Overview

Enterprise AI company building empathetic, fine-tunable LLMs for
Fortune 500 customers. Originally built Pi, a consumer chatbot
known for emotional intelligence. After Microsoft acqui-hired most
of the team (including co-founders Mustafa Suleyman and Karen
Simonyan) in March 2024, the remaining company pivoted to
enterprise under new CEO Sean White.

## Technical Focus

- Inflection 3.0 models (Pi for conversational AI, Productivity
  for enterprise workflows)
- On-premises and hybrid LLM deployment ("Own Your Intelligence")
- Bespoke model fine-tuning on customer infrastructure
- Emotionally intelligent conversational AI
- Enterprise data integration and privacy controls

## Scale & Stage

Private. Raised $1.52B total ($225M Series D in 2022, $1.3B
Series E in 2023 from Nvidia, Reid Hoffman, Bill Gates). ~69
employees post-acqui-hire. Investors include Cascade Investment,
Nvidia, and Microsoft (pre-acqui-hire).

Unusual situation: raised massive funding as a consumer AI play,
lost most of the team to Microsoft, and pivoted to enterprise
with a skeleton crew. Viability as an independent company is
uncertain.

## Hiring

Very small team (~69 people) rebuilding after the Microsoft
acqui-hire. Likely hiring across engineering and enterprise sales
to support the pivot. Acquired BoostKPI and Jelled.ai in late
2024 to add data analytics and communications capabilities.

## Recent Context

Pivoted fully to enterprise under CEO Sean White. Licenses
Inflection 3.0 models to businesses for on-premises deployment.
Pi consumer chatbot remains active but is no longer the focus.
Acquired BoostKPI and Jelled.ai in November 2024 to strengthen
enterprise platform. Company's long-term trajectory remains
unclear given the dramatic team loss.
