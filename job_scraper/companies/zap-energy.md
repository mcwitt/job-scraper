# Zap Energy

## Overview

Fusion energy startup using sheared-flow-stabilized Z-pinch
technology — compact fusion without large magnets. Founded
in 2017 by Benj Conway (CEO), Brian Nelson (CTO), and
Uri Shumlak (CSO). Headquartered in Everett, Washington.

## Technical Focus

- Sheared-flow-stabilized Z-pinch plasma confinement
- Compact fusion without external magnetic coils
- Pulsed power systems engineering
- Liquid metal blanket technology
- Plasma diagnostics and computational modeling

## Scale & Stage

Private. Over $330M total raised, including $130M Series D
(Oct 2025) led by Soros Fund Management. ~50-100 employees.
Pre-revenue; building engineering demonstration platform.

## Hiring

Growing team of plasma physicists, pulsed power engineers,
mechanical engineers, and computational scientists. Startup
pace with deep physics focus. University of Washington
spinout culture. Looking for builders comfortable with
experimental hardware.

## Recent Context

FuZE-3 device achieved 1.6 GPa total plasma pressure
(Nov 2025) — highest ever for sheared-flow Z-pinch.
Century platform (liquid metal + pulsed power integration)
under construction for full power plant subsystem demo.
Series D funding expands engineering demonstration work
alongside continued plasma R&D.
