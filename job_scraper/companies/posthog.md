# PostHog

## Overview

PostHog is an open-source, all-in-one product analytics
platform that bundles event tracking, session replays,
feature flags, A/B testing, and user surveys. Founded in
2020 by James Hawkins (CEO) and Tim Glaser (CTO), PostHog
is a fully remote company with no central office.

## Technical Focus

- Product analytics and event capture (open source)
- Session replay and heatmaps
- Feature flags and experimentation (A/B testing)
- User surveys and feedback collection
- Data warehouse and SQL querying
- Usage-based pricing on events and data volume

## Scale & Stage

Private, valued at $1.4B (Sep 2025 Series E). Raised $194M
total over 7 rounds. Series E ($75M) led by Peak XV;
Series D ($70M) led by Stripe. Approximately 170 employees
as of early 2026. Achieved unicorn status in 2025.

## Hiring

PostHog is all-remote and hires engineers, product managers,
and developer advocates. The company has a strong open-source
and transparency culture — its handbook, strategy, and
finances are public. Engineering roles emphasize full-stack
ownership, and the team is small relative to revenue,
meaning high individual impact.

## Recent Context

PostHog raised two rounds in 2025: a $70M Series D led by
Stripe (Jun 2025) and a $75M Series E at $1.4B (Sep 2025).
The company grew from startup to unicorn in five years,
driven by the developer-first open-source model and
all-in-one analytics positioning.
