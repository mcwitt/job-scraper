# WeaveGrid

## Overview

Software company connecting electric vehicles and
distributed energy resources to the electric grid. Founded
in 2018 by Apoorv Bhargava (CEO) and John Taggart (CTO).
Headquartered in San Francisco. Uses machine learning and
optimization to help utilities manage EV charging load and
integrate renewable energy.

## Technical Focus

- EV-grid integration and managed charging (DISCO platform)
- Distribution system optimization for transformers, feeders
- Machine learning for load forecasting and scheduling
- Utility-scale demand response for EVs and home batteries
- Renewable energy integration and grid flexibility

## Scale & Stage

Private, raised ~$78M total including a $35M Series B
(November 2022). ~85 employees. Supports utilities serving
over 40% of U.S. electric vehicles. Major utility customers
include Xcel Energy, Southern California Edison, DTE Energy,
and Southern Company subsidiaries. Founded 2018.

## Hiring

Hires software engineers, data scientists, and energy domain
experts. Engineering roles focus on ML, optimization, and
distributed systems for grid management. Values candidates
who combine software depth with interest in clean energy
and grid modernization. Startup culture with utility-sector
domain knowledge.

## Recent Context

Partnered with PowerFlex to advance grid flexibility through
commercial managed charging (March 2026). Launched EV
initiatives with Alabama Power and Georgia Power (Southern
Company subsidiaries). Partnered with American Center for
Mobility to test EV-grid integration software. Operating
the largest distribution optimization programs in the U.S.
