# Plaid

## Overview

Financial infrastructure company providing API-first data
connectivity between consumer apps and financial institutions.
Connects to 16,000+ banks and powers 8,000+ apps including
Venmo, Chime, and Robinhood. Founded 2012 by Zach Perret and
William Hockey. Headquartered in San Francisco. Visa attempted
a $5.3B acquisition in 2020; deal was blocked by the DOJ.

## Technical Focus

- Unified banking API abstracting legacy bank systems
- Account linking, balance checks, transaction data
- Identity verification and anti-fraud products
- Payments infrastructure (ACH, instant transfers)
- Credit reporting and income verification APIs
- Layer — instant onboarding via tokenized auth
- OAuth 2.0 auth flows across 16,000+ institutions

## Scale & Stage

Private. Raised $575M Series D at $6.1B valuation (April 2025,
led by Franklin Templeton, Fidelity, BlackRock, NEA, Ribbit).
Reached $8B valuation in February 2026 employee tender offer.
Total funding ~$1.3B. Approximately 1,300 employees across
the US, Canada, UK, and EU.

## Hiring

Engineering-heavy org hiring across infrastructure, platform,
and product teams. Investing in AI tooling for engineering
productivity (new Intelligent Tooling team in 2026). Interview
process averages ~22 days. Known for strong early-career talent
and fast promotions. Hybrid work model with SF headquarters.

## Recent Context

Completed $8B tender offer in February 2026 giving employees
liquidity. Partnered with Backbase to tackle bank data
fragmentation with AI-powered onboarding. MANTL became the
first fintech to resell Plaid Layer for community banks.
Named new CFO Seun Sodipo (October 2025) signaling potential
future IPO. Revenue growing as open banking adoption expands.
