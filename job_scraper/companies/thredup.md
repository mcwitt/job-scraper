# thredUP

## Overview

World's largest online resale platform for apparel,
shoes, and accessories. Founded in 2009 by James
Reinhart. Headquartered in Oakland, CA.

## Technical Focus

- Proprietary item-processing platform (~100k unique
  items/day)
- ML models for pricing, routing, and inventory
  optimization across millions of unique secondhand items
- Computer vision for item identification and grading
- Resale-as-a-Service (RaaS) white-label platform for
  major brands
- Data infrastructure on AWS and Databricks
- iOS and web marketplace engineering

## Scale & Stage

Public (TDUP). ~$300M annual revenue (growing ~34%
YoY). ~1,500 employees. IPO in 2021. Operates
distribution centers across the US.

## Hiring

Engineering roles span ML, platform, product, and
operations technology. Data science is central to the
business — pricing, demand forecasting, and logistics
optimization. Mobile and web product engineering for
the consumer marketplace. Oakland HQ with distributed
US workforce.

## Recent Context

Record Q3 2025 revenue of $82.2M (+34% YoY). Active
buyers grew to 1.57M (+26%). RaaS platform powering
resale programs for ~50 brand partners. Published
annual Resale Report showing secondhand growing 15x
faster than traditional retail.
