# Kraken Tech

## Overview
Kraken Tech (Kraken Technologies Limited) builds an AI-powered
operating system for energy utilities, automating much of the
energy supply chain to enable efficient service during the
transition to decentralized, decarbonized energy. Founded as
part of Octopus Energy Group (launched 2016), headquartered in
London, UK. Registered in England and Wales.

## Technical Focus
- End-to-end customer management: billing, meter data, CRM,
  and communications for residential and commercial customers
- Distributed energy resource (DER) optimization: EVs, heat
  pumps, solar, batteries, smart thermostats
- Field operations and workforce management
- Generation and storage asset optimization
- Expanding into water utilities and telecoms

## Scale & Stage
- Part of Octopus Energy Group (private, Series C+)
- 70 million+ customer accounts on the platform
- Operational in 30+ countries; 40+ large-scale migrations
- 400,000+ connected devices
- Notable clients: EDF, Tokyo Gas, Origin Energy, National
  Grid, Severn Trent, Portsmouth Water
