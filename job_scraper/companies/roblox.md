# Roblox

## Overview

Immersive platform where users create, share, and play millions of
3D experiences. Operates both the runtime engine and creator tools
(Roblox Studio) that power user-generated content at massive scale.
85+ million daily active users, primarily younger demographics but
expanding. Revenue comes from in-experience purchases (Robux
currency) with a creator economy sharing model.

## Technical Focus

- Real-time 3D engine and physics simulation at global scale
- Generative AI for 3D content creation, geometry synthesis, and
  animation
- Computer vision and multimodal AI (foundation models for 3D)
- Large-scale distributed infrastructure (custom orchestration,
  observability platform)
- Real-time communication and networking
- Trust and safety ML (content moderation at scale)

## Scale & Stage

Public (NYSE: RBLX). ~3,100 employees. 2025 revenue of ~$4.9B,
growing ~36% YoY. 85.3M daily active users. Still operating at a
net loss (~$1.1B in 2025) due to heavy investment in platform
growth. Guiding 2026 revenue of ~$6.0-6.3B.

## Hiring

Engineering-focused culture headquartered in San Mateo. Actively
hiring in AI/ML (particularly 3D generative models, computer
vision, and graphics), infrastructure, and platform engineering.
PhD-level roles available in multimodal AI and 3D foundation
models. Over 1,000 engineers using internal developer tooling.
Community apprenticeship programs for Lua developers.

## Recent Context

Investing heavily in AI-driven creator tools to make 3D content
creation more accessible. Hiring for applied research in generative
3D, geometry synthesis, and real-time simulation with ML. Revenue
growth accelerating (~36% in 2025 vs prior years). Expanding
engineering tooling internally with microservice lifecycle platform
and AI-assisted code review.
