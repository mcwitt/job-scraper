# Aurora Solar

## Overview

Aurora Solar develops cloud-based software for solar
and storage system design, sales, and project management.
Founded in 2013 by Christopher Hopper and Samuel Adeyemo,
the company is headquartered in San Francisco, CA. Over
7,000 organizations use Aurora, and more than 10 million
solar projects have been designed on its platform globally.

## Technical Focus

- Solar PV system design and engineering simulation
- LIDAR and aerial-imagery-based roof modeling
- Sales proposal generation and financing tools
- Shading analysis and energy-production forecasting
- Workflow management for solar installers

## Scale & Stage

Private, Series D ($200M in Feb 2022) led by Coatue and
Energize Ventures, with Lux Capital, Fifth Wall, ICONIQ,
and Emerson Collective. Total raised ~$524M at a $4B
valuation. Approximately 300 employees as of early 2026.
Founded 2013.

## Hiring

Aurora hires across software engineering, product, data
science, and go-to-market roles. The engineering team
builds browser-based 3D design tools, optimization
algorithms, and integrations with solar financing
platforms. Remote-friendly with an SF headquarters.

## Recent Context

Aurora continues to expand its platform capabilities
for storage design alongside solar, responding to growing
residential and commercial battery attach rates. The
company has focused on deepening integrations with
permitting and utility interconnection workflows as the
U.S. solar market matures.
