# Terawatt Infrastructure

## Overview

Terawatt Infrastructure builds, finances, owns, and operates
large-scale EV and autonomous vehicle fleet charging
infrastructure across the United States. Founded in 2020 by
Scott Mercer, the company is headquartered in San Francisco,
CA. Terawatt develops turnkey charging solutions — urban
mobility hubs and multi-fleet highway/logistics corridor
hubs — for companies deploying commercial EV and AV fleets
at scale.

## Technical Focus

- Large-scale fleet EV charging hub development and
  operations
- Site acquisition, permitting, and real estate for
  charging infrastructure
- Utility interconnection and electrical engineering for
  high-power charging installations
- Software for site monitoring and operations management
- Financing and ownership structures for charging assets
- Autonomous vehicle fleet charging logistics

## Scale & Stage

Private. Raised $1B+ in equity and debt financing, including
a $150M Series A led by Energy Impact Partners and backing
from investors including Aligned Climate Capital. Team of
roughly 80–120 employees. Hiring across real estate,
utilities, construction, finance, and software.

## Hiring

Terawatt hires across real estate acquisitions, utility
coordination, construction management, site development,
finance (project finance/investment operations), policy,
and software/DevOps. Roles require comfort operating in a
fast-moving infrastructure startup; many positions involve
hands-on ownership of complex multi-stakeholder projects
spanning real estate, utilities, and permitting. Culture
emphasizes urgency and humility.

## Recent Context

Terawatt is actively expanding its U.S. portfolio of
charging hubs along key logistics corridors and in urban
markets. The company targets fleet operators including
autonomous trucking and ride-hail companies. As AV
deployment accelerates in 2025–2026, Terawatt is scaling
development capacity with hires in construction, real
estate, and utilities to meet growing customer demand.
