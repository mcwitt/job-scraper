# Mercury

## Overview

Fintech company providing banking services designed for startups
and tech companies. Offers business checking, savings, treasury,
and credit products through a modern software interface. YC-
backed. Based in San Francisco with a remote-friendly culture.

## Technical Focus

- Banking platform and financial services infrastructure
- Payments, ledger systems, and transaction processing
- Compliance and regulatory technology
- Web application development (strong frontend culture)
- API and developer-facing financial tools
- Security and fraud detection

## Scale & Stage

Private. Series C. ~800 employees. Strong revenue growth from
banking fees and treasury products. Large customer base among
startups and SMBs. Not AI-focused but engineering-driven.

## Hiring

Hiring across full-stack engineering, backend infrastructure,
compliance, and product. Engineering culture values craft and
code quality. Tech stack includes Haskell on the backend, which
is unusual and attracts a certain type of engineer. No finance
background required.

Accessible to strong generalist engineers. Good option for
those who want high-quality engineering work in fintech without
the AI hype cycle.

## Recent Context

Expanding product suite beyond basic banking into credit,
treasury management, and financial workflows. Growing customer
base and headcount. Positioned as the default banking choice
for startups in the YC ecosystem.
