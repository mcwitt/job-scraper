# Imbue

## Overview

AI research company building AI agents that can reason and write
code. Previously known as Generally Intelligent. Small, research-
focused team in San Francisco. Emphasis on understanding and
improving AI reasoning capabilities.

## Technical Focus

- AI agents for coding and reasoning tasks
- Large language model training and evaluation
- Code generation and program synthesis
- AI reasoning and planning research
- Custom training infrastructure

## Scale & Stage

Private. Venture-backed (~$200M+ raised). ~30 employees. Very
small, research-intensive team. Pre-product, focused on
foundational research toward capable AI agents.

## Hiring

Extremely small team, so hires are rare and selective. Looking
for strong ML researchers or systems engineers comfortable
working on open-ended research problems. Publication record or
demonstrated research ability expected. Startup pace with
research lab ambiguity.

## Recent Context

Training own models focused on reasoning and code. Operating
in the AI agents space alongside competitors like Cognition
(Devin) and OpenAI. Taking a more research-first approach
compared to product-focused agent startups.
