# BentoML

## Overview

Open-source model serving framework and managed inference
platform (BentoCloud). Founded 2019 by Chaoyu Yang
(ex-Databricks). Headquartered in San Francisco. Acquired
by Modular (Chris Lattner's company) in February 2025.

## Technical Focus

- Open-source model serving framework (Apache 2.0)
- BentoCloud managed inference platform
- LLM, embedding, and agentic pipeline deployment
- Multi-cloud and hybrid inference infrastructure
- Production ML model packaging and deployment

## Scale & Stage

Acquired by Modular in Feb 2025. Previously raised ~$9M
seed from DCM Ventures and Bow Capital. Small team
(under 10 employees pre-acquisition). Over 10,000 orgs
use BentoML OSS, including 50+ Fortune 500 companies.
Open-source project continues under Apache 2.0.

## Hiring

Post-acquisition, hiring is through Modular. Previously
a small, engineering-focused team. Roles centered on
ML infrastructure, model serving, cloud platform, and
developer experience. Strong open-source community
engagement valued.

## Recent Context

Acquired by Modular in February 2025, combining
BentoML's serving framework with Modular's MAX/Mojo
hardware optimization stack. Goal: end-to-end inference
without hardware lock-in. BentoML OSS remains Apache
2.0 with continued community support. Modular is
integrating BentoCloud into its platform offering.
