# Osmo

## Overview

Digital scent design company using AI to digitize the sense
of smell. Founded 2023 by Alex Wiltschko, an olfactory
neuroscientist (Harvard PhD) and former Google Brain
researcher. HQ split between Cambridge, MA (R&D) and
New York (commercial), with manufacturing in Elizabeth, NJ.

## Technical Focus

- Olfactory Intelligence (OI) platform mapping molecular
  structure to predicted scent
- AI-driven molecule discovery and fragrance design
- Proprietary fragrance molecules (Glossine, Fractaline,
  Quasarine)
- Sensor hardware for digitizing real-world scent
- Robotic fragrance manufacturing

## Scale & Stage

Private, Series B ($70M in Feb 2026, led by Two Sigma
Ventures). Total funding ~$133.5M. Earlier $60M Series A
led by Lux Capital and Google Ventures. ~70-100 employees.
Three master perfumers on staff.

## Hiring

Interdisciplinary team of ML researchers, chemists,
hardware engineers, and manufacturing/ops. Most roles in
Elizabeth, NJ (manufacturing, lab, quality). High bar for
cross-domain expertise in ML + chemistry/scent science.

## Recent Context

Closed $70M Series B (Feb 2026). Launched "Generation by
Osmo" fragrance house for B2B AI-assisted development.
Demonstrated scent teleportation technology (Mar 2025).
Published breakthrough Science paper on AI predicting odor
from molecular structure.
