# World Labs

## Overview

Spatial intelligence company building AI that understands
and generates 3D worlds. Founded September 2024 by
Fei-Fei Li (Stanford, ImageNet pioneer), Justin Johnson,
Christoph Lassner, and Ben Mildenhall. Based in San
Francisco.

## Technical Focus

- 3D generative world models (Marble)
- Spatial intelligence and 3D reasoning
- Text/image/video to persistent 3D environments
- AI-native 3D editing tools (Chisel editor)
- Applications in gaming, VFX, VR, and robotics

## Scale & Stage

Private. Raised ~$1.23B total: $230M initial round from
a16z, NEA, and Radical Ventures, plus $1B in Feb 2026
from AMD, Autodesk, Nvidia, Fidelity, and others.
Rapidly scaling team. One of the best-funded AI startups
globally.

## Hiring

Research-heavy team of ML, computer vision, and graphics
experts. Cofounders are world-class researchers in their
fields. Expect a high bar for 3D vision, generative
modeling, and systems engineering roles. Academic pedigree
valued given the founders' backgrounds.

## Recent Context

Launched Marble in November 2025 — its first commercial
generative world model with free and paid tiers. Raised
$1B in February 2026 with strategic backing from Autodesk
($200M) to integrate world models into 3D workflows.
Targeting gaming, VFX, VR, and robotics verticals.
