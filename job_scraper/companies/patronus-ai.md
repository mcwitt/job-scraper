# Patronus AI

## Overview

AI evaluation and safety company building infrastructure
to test, evaluate, and improve large language models.
Founded in 2023 by Anand Kannappan (Meta Reality Labs)
and Rebecca Qian (Meta AI responsible NLP research).
Headquartered in San Francisco. Describes itself as a
frontier lab developing simulation research for
human-aligned AGI.

## Technical Focus

- LLM evaluation and automated testing platforms
- Generative simulation environments for AI agents
- Hallucination detection and factuality scoring
- Multimodal LLM-as-a-Judge evaluation systems
- AI safety benchmarks (FinanceBench, Lynx,
  SimpleSafetyTests, CopyrightCatcher)
- Reinforcement learning environments for alignment

## Scale & Stage

Private, Series A. Raised ~$40M total: $3M seed (2023,
Lightspeed), $17M Series A (May 2024, Notable Capital
with Lightspeed, Datadog). ~35 employees. Backed by
Lightspeed Venture Partners, Notable Capital, and
Stanford-affiliated investors.

## Hiring

Research-oriented team with backgrounds from Meta AI,
Amazon AGI, Google, and top academic labs. Roles span
ML research, applied AI engineering, and platform
infrastructure. High bar for evaluation and safety
expertise. Small, fast-growing team with strong research
publication culture. SOC 2, TISAX, and HIPAA certified.

## Recent Context

Launched Generative Simulators (Dec 2025) -- adaptive
"practice worlds" that generate scenarios for evaluating
autonomous AI agents. Released multimodal
LLM-as-a-Judge (Mar 2025) powered by Google Gemini.
Shipped Percival (May 2025) for automatically detecting
AI malfunctions. Co-created Humanity's Last Exam
benchmark.
