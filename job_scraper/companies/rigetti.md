# Rigetti Computing

## Overview

Full-stack quantum computing company that designs, manufactures, and
deploys superconducting qubit processors. Operates Fab-1, the industry's
first dedicated quantum device manufacturing facility. Builds both
hardware (multi-chip quantum processors) and cloud software (Quil
programming language, Forest SDK) for quantum computation.

## Technical Focus

- Superconducting qubit design and fabrication
- Multi-chip quantum processor architecture (chiplet-based scaling)
- Quantum error correction and gate fidelity optimization
- Hybrid classical-quantum cloud computing (QCS platform)
- Cryogenic systems engineering

## Scale & Stage

Public (NASDAQ: RGTI). ~175 employees. Full-year 2025 revenue of $7.1M
with ~$600M cash on hand. Revenue is primarily from QPU system sales
and quantum cloud services. Pre-profitability; operating at a loss as
the industry matures toward practical quantum advantage.

## Hiring

Interdisciplinary team spanning quantum physics, electrical engineering,
cryogenics, and software. Roles typically require advanced degrees in
physics, EE, or CS. Strong emphasis on fabrication expertise (in-house
chip manufacturing) and quantum algorithm development. Small headcount
means individual contributors have outsized impact.

## Recent Context

Roadmap targets 100+ qubit chiplet system with 99.5% two-qubit gate
fidelity by end of 2025, 150+ qubits at 99.7% by 2026, and 1,000+
qubits at 99.8% by 2027. Secured $35M investment from Quanta Computer
in April 2025. Won $8.4M purchase order from India's C-DAC and $5.7M
in orders for 9-qubit Novera QPU systems.
