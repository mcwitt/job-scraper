# ClimateAi

## Overview

ClimateAi builds an AI-powered climate resilience
platform (ClimateLens) that helps enterprises
climate-proof their supply chains through hyper-local
risk forecasting and adaptation planning. Founded in
2017 by Himanshu Gupta and Max Evans, the company is
headquartered in San Francisco and operates across 35
countries.

## Technical Focus

- Hyper-local climate risk forecasting (1km resolution)
- Seasonal weather and extreme event prediction
- Supply chain vulnerability assessment and adaptation
- Patented AI models applied to climate and weather data
- Food, agriculture, and water system resilience

## Scale & Stage

Private, Series B. Raised $38M total from investors
including Radical Ventures and Four Rivers Group.
30+ enterprise customers across 35 countries. Named
TIME Best Invention in Sustainability (2022). Holds
5 patents. Founded 2017.

## Hiring

ClimateAi hires across machine learning, climate
science, product, and enterprise sales. The platform
demands expertise in atmospheric modeling, geospatial
data, and supply chain operations. The team bridges
climate science and business strategy, so
cross-functional skills are valued. Relatively small
team with high individual impact.

## Recent Context

Hitachi integrated ClimateAi's API to build a global
supply chain risk model, using seasonal weather
forecasts and extreme weather predictions for
weather-resilient inventory management. The company
continues expanding its enterprise customer base and
deepening its focus on food, agriculture, and water
supply chain resilience.
