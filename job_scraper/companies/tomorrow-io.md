# Tomorrow.io

## Overview

Tomorrow.io (formerly ClimaCell) is a weather and
climate intelligence platform that combines proprietary
satellite data, AI models, and ground-based sensors to
deliver decision-grade weather forecasting. Founded in
2016 by Shimon Elkabetz, Rei Goffer, and Itai Zlotnik,
the company is headquartered in Boston, MA.

## Technical Focus

- DeepSky AI-native weather satellite constellation
  (13 satellites deployed, 60-min global revisit)
- AI-driven weather forecasting and nowcasting
- Climate adaptation and early warning systems
- Supply chain and logistics weather optimization
- Aviation, energy, and insurance intelligence APIs

## Scale & Stage

Private, raised $333M+ total including $175M equity
financing (Feb 2026, led by Stonecourt Capital and
HarbourVest) at $1B+ valuation. Approximately 215
employees. Serves ~250 customers including over half
of the top 10 Fortune 500 companies. Founded 2016.

## Hiring

Tomorrow.io hires across satellite engineering, ML/AI,
atmospheric science, software engineering, and
enterprise sales. The company's dual hardware+software
model (satellites + AI platform) creates demand for
both space systems and data science talent. Israeli
founding team with global operations; fast-paced
environment with a strong R&D culture.

## Recent Context

Tomorrow.io raised $175M in February 2026 to accelerate
deployment of DeepSky, the world's first AI-native
weather satellite constellation. The company completed
deployment of 13 satellites achieving 60-minute global
revisit capability and is expanding its intelligence
platform across aviation, logistics, energy, and
insurance verticals.
