# LevelTen Energy

## Overview

LevelTen Energy provides transaction infrastructure for
the clean energy transition, operating the world's largest
online marketplace for power purchase agreements (PPAs),
renewable energy credits, and clean energy assets. Founded
in 2016 by Bryce Smith. Headquartered in Seattle,
Washington, with operations across North America, Europe,
and Africa.

## Technical Focus

- PPA marketplace and transaction platform
- Clean energy data analytics and price indices
- Renewable asset valuation and M&A tools
- Capacity+ platform for data center power procurement
- Granular certificate and carbon credit trading
- Software automation for energy transactions

## Scale & Stage

Private, Series D. Raised $137M total, including a $65M
Series D in July 2024 led by B Capital with participation
from Google and Microsoft Climate Innovation Fund. Other
investors include Aster Capital, E8 Ventures, and Altius
Ventures. Approximately 176 employees across three
continents. Publishes quarterly PPA Price Index reports
that are industry benchmarks.

## Hiring

LevelTen hires across software engineering, data science,
energy market analysis, and transaction advisory. The
company values mission-driven candidates passionate about
decarbonization. Culture emphasizes work-life balance,
calculated risk-taking, and autonomy. Named one of
Seattle's "50 Best Places to Work." Runs the Energy
Scholars Mentorship Program for diverse talent pipelines.

## Recent Context

In July 2025, LevelTen launched Capacity+, a new platform
to help data center operators, manufacturers, and utilities
identify and secure carbon-free capacity resources to meet
surging AI-driven power demand. Q4 2025 PPA Price Index
showed North American solar prices rising 3.2%, driven by
policy uncertainty around IRA tax credit phase-outs after
the July 2025 spending bill. European solar PPA prices
declined ~8% year-over-year through Q4 2025.
