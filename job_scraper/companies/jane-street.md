# Jane Street

## Overview

Quantitative trading firm that trades a wide range of financial
instruments including equities, bonds, options, ETFs, and
commodities. Known for heavy use of OCaml and functional
programming. Offices in New York, London, and Hong Kong.

## Technical Focus

- Quantitative trading strategies and market making
- OCaml ecosystem (largest industrial OCaml user)
- Low-latency systems and networking
- Real-time risk management and monitoring
- Data analysis and statistical modeling
- FPGA and hardware-accelerated trading systems

## Scale & Stage

Private partnership. ~2500 employees. Extremely profitable —
trades ~$20B+ daily. Self-funded, no outside investors. One of
the most technically sophisticated trading firms.

## Hiring

Very selective. Hiring process emphasizes problem-solving,
quantitative reasoning, and clear thinking over specific domain
experience. Traders, developers, and researchers all go through
rigorous interview processes with math and programming
components. No prior finance experience required.

Compensation is among the highest in tech/finance. Strong new
grad pipeline — actively recruits from top CS and math programs.
OCaml experience not required; they train on the job.

## Recent Context

Expanding trading operations globally. Growing technology teams
to support increasing trade volume and new asset classes.
Investing in ML/AI for trading signals while maintaining core
quantitative approach. Significant open-source contributions to
OCaml ecosystem.
