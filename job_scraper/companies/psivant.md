# Psivant

## Overview

Computational chemistry-driven drug discovery company (rebranded to PsiThera
in December 2025) that models biologically relevant protein motion to uncover
hidden druggable states and design oral small molecules for targets
traditionally treated by injected biologics. Originally a Roivant spinout,
now fully independent.

## Technical Focus

- QUAISAR platform: computational physics + AI for drug design
- Protein dynamics modeling to reveal hidden druggable conformations
- Oral small molecule design for biologic-validated targets
- TNF protein superfamily drug candidates
- Collaboration with Acellera on AI and quantum simulation methods

## Scale & Stage

Private (rebranded as PsiThera). ~35 employees. Raised $97.5M total,
including a $47.5M Series A in December 2025 led by Samsara BioCapital
and Lightstone Ventures. Backed by Roivant, YK Bioventures, and
Eurofarma Ventures.

## Hiring

Small, focused team with emphasis on computational chemistry, molecular
dynamics, and biophysics expertise. Hiring bar is high given the deeply
technical nature of the platform. Roles likely span computational
scientists, medicinal chemists, and ML engineers.

Early-stage culture with startup intensity. Founded by Woody Sherman
(Chief Innovation Officer), with Eric Shaff (former CEO of Seres
Therapeutics) appointed as CEO. Expect hands-on technical work and
direct impact on pipeline decisions.

## Recent Context

Rebranded from Psivant Therapeutics to PsiThera in December 2025 alongside
the $47.5M Series A, appointing Eric Shaff as CEO. First drug candidates
focus on the TNF protein superfamily for inflammatory and immunological
conditions. Collaborating with Acellera to integrate AceForce technology
with the QUAISAR platform for AI and quantum simulation-driven discovery.
