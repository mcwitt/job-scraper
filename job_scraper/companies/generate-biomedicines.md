# Generate Biomedicines

## Overview

Clinical-stage biotech pioneering generative biology --
using ML to design novel protein therapeutics from scratch.
Founded in 2018 by Flagship Pioneering scientists Avak
Kahvejian, Geoffrey von Maltzahn, Molly Gibson, and
Gevorg Grigoryan. HQ in Somerville, MA.

## Technical Focus

- Generative ML platform for de novo protein design
- Antibody and protein therapeutic generation
- Structure-based drug design using foundation models
- Computational biology and protein engineering
- Lead program GB-0895 (anti-TSLP) for severe asthma

## Scale & Stage

Public (IPO March 2026, raised ~$400M at $16/share).
Previously raised $800M+ in venture capital including
$273M Series C (2023). ~320 employees. Collaborations
with Amgen and Novartis (~$110M in milestone payments).
Flagship Pioneering is the founding investor.

## Hiring

Interdisciplinary team of ML scientists, protein
engineers, structural biologists, and drug developers.
Roles span ML research, computational biology, software
engineering, and clinical development. Culture rooted in
Flagship's "pioneering" ethos -- expects comfort with
ambiguity and novel science. Strong academic pedigree
valued alongside engineering ability.

## Recent Context

IPO priced March 2026, raising $400M to fund Phase 3
trials of GB-0895 in severe asthma and Phase 1b in COPD.
One of the first pure-play generative biology companies
to go public. Proceeds also funding next-gen platform
development (~$75M allocated to technology). Represents
a major validation of the generative biology thesis.
