# Ambience Healthcare

## Overview

AI-native clinical documentation platform that uses
ambient AI to automate medical documentation, coding,
and clinical documentation integrity (CDI) for health
systems. Founded in 2020 by Mike Ng and Nikhil Buduma,
MIT classmates motivated by personal losses to cancer.
Headquartered in San Francisco, CA.

## Technical Focus

- Ambient AI for real-time clinical documentation
- Proprietary AI reasoning models for healthcare
- Medical coding automation and CDI
- Integration with health system EHR workflows

## Scale & Stage

Private, Series C. Raised $243M Series C in July 2025
co-led by Oak HC/FT and Andreessen Horowitz ($313M
total). Valued at $1B. ~200+ employees. Previous
investors include OpenAI Startup Fund and Kleiner
Perkins.

## Hiring

Engineering and ML roles focused on healthcare AI,
NLP, and health system integrations. Domain knowledge
of clinical workflows and regulatory requirements is
valued. Fast-growing team that has roughly doubled
in size year-over-year.

## Recent Context

Reached unicorn status ($1B valuation) with July 2025
Series C. Platform deployed across multiple major
health systems for ambient documentation. Backed by
both top-tier VC (a16z, Kleiner Perkins) and strategic
healthcare investors (Optum Ventures, Frist Cressey).
