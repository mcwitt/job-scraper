# Anthropic

## Overview

AI safety company building reliable and interpretable AI systems.
Makers of Claude, a family of large language models. Founded in 2021
by Dario and Daniela Amodei along with other ex-OpenAI researchers.
Headquartered in San Francisco.

## Technical Focus

- Large language model training and alignment
- Constitutional AI (RLHF variant using AI feedback)
- Mechanistic interpretability research
- Inference infrastructure and model serving
- Tool use, agents, and developer APIs

## Scale & Stage

Private, Series D+ funding (~$7B+ raised). Valued at $60B+.
~1000+ employees. Revenue growing via API and enterprise products.

## Hiring

Very selective. Research roles expect strong ML publications or
demonstrated alignment/interpretability work. Engineering roles
emphasize systems depth — distributed training, inference
optimization, or large-scale infrastructure experience. Culture
leans academic; many hires from top research labs and universities.

Product and platform engineering roles are more accessible for
strong generalist engineers with relevant systems backgrounds.

## Recent Context

Shipping Claude model family (Opus, Sonnet, Haiku). Expanding
developer platform with tool use, computer use, and agentic
capabilities. Competing directly with OpenAI and Google on
frontier model capabilities while maintaining safety focus.
