# Achira

## Overview

AI-powered biotech startup that integrates machine learning, quantum
chemistry, and physics-based molecular simulations to accelerate drug
discovery. Founded in 2024 by Dr. John Chodera (Memorial Sloan
Kettering), the company builds high-precision computational models to
replace time-consuming experimental methods in pharmaceutical R&D.

## Technical Focus

- AI-powered molecular simulation and drug discovery
- Quantum chemistry integration with ML models
- Physics-based molecular modeling
- Computational approaches to drug-target interaction prediction
- High-precision force field development

## Scale & Stage

Private, seed stage. Raised ~$33M in an NVIDIA-backed seed round from
Dimension, Amplify Partners, Compound, and NVentures. Founded in 2024,
headquartered in New York (NoHo) with a San Francisco office. Small
team in early growth phase.

## Hiring

Early-stage startup with a research-intensive culture rooted in
academic computational chemistry (founder runs a lab at Memorial Sloan
Kettering). Expect hiring focused on computational chemists, ML
researchers, and software engineers with physics or chemistry domain
knowledge.

Given the seed stage and NVIDIA backing, roles will likely involve
building core platform infrastructure and developing foundational AI
models. High technical bar with emphasis on scientific rigor and
publication-quality research.

## Recent Context

Emerged from stealth in early 2025 with NVIDIA-backed seed funding.
Expected to unveil first AI models in 2025. The company sits at the
intersection of academic computational chemistry research and
industry-scale drug discovery, leveraging the founder's deep expertise
in molecular dynamics and free energy calculations.
