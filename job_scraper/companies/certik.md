# CertiK

## Overview

Blockchain security company providing AI-powered smart
contract auditing, on-chain monitoring, and compliance
solutions. Founded 2018 by Ronghui Gu (Columbia) and
Zhong Shao (Yale). HQ in New York, NY.

## Technical Focus

- Smart contract security auditing (Solidity, Rust, Go)
- AI-powered vulnerability detection
- On-chain monitoring and incident response
- Proof of reserves and compliance tooling
- Formal verification for blockchain protocols

## Scale & Stage

Private. $296M total funding over 9 rounds, last at $2B+
valuation. Investors include Sequoia, Coatue, Goldman
Sachs, Insight Partners, and Shunwei Capital. ~200+
employees (down ~19% YoY).
