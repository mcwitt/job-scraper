# Nuro

## Overview

Autonomous driving technology company founded in 2016 by two former
Waymo engineers (Dave Ferguson and Jiajun Zhu). Originally built
purpose-built autonomous delivery vehicles; pivoted in 2024 to licensing
its Level 4 self-driving system, Nuro Driver, to automakers and mobility
providers.

## Technical Focus

- Level 4 autonomous driving software (Nuro Driver)
- Perception, planning, and prediction for urban driving
- Simulation and testing infrastructure for AV validation
- Integration of autonomy stack into third-party vehicle platforms

## Scale & Stage

Private, Series E. Raised $2.3B+ total. $203M Series E in August 2025
at $6B valuation, with investment from Uber and Nvidia. Earlier rounds
include a $940M Series B (2019) and $600M Series D (2021). ~1,000
employees. Headquartered in Mountain View, CA.

## Hiring

Engineering-heavy organization focused on autonomy, robotics, and ML.
Hiring perception, planning, and simulation engineers, as well as
software engineers for vehicle integration and infrastructure. Strong
Waymo/Google DNA in the founding team.

Mid-sized team that has been through significant restructuring (layoffs
in 2024 during the pivot from delivery robots to licensing). Now
growing again with the Uber/Lucid partnership driving hiring.

## Recent Context

Signed landmark partnership with Uber and Lucid Motors in July 2025 to
deploy Nuro Driver in 20,000+ Lucid robotaxis over six years, with
first vehicles expected in 2026. Nvidia and Uber both invested in the
August 2025 round, signaling strong strategic alignment. The licensing
model represents a fundamental shift from operating vehicles to being
an autonomy platform provider.
