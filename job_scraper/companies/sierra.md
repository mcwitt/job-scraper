# Sierra

## Overview

Enterprise AI company building an agent platform for customer
experience. Enables businesses to deploy AI agents across chat,
SMS, WhatsApp, email, and voice for customer service, sales, and
support. Founded in 2023 by Bret Taylor (former Salesforce co-CEO,
OpenAI board chair) and Clay Bavor (former Google VP).

## Technical Focus

- Multi-model agent orchestration (15+ frontier, open-weight, and
  proprietary models selected per task)
- Agent OS: no-code and programmatic agent development platform
- Agent Data Platform for memory, personalization, and context
- Omnichannel deployment (chat, voice, SMS, WhatsApp, email)
- Agent Studio for building production agents without engineering

## Scale & Stage

Private, Series B. Raised $350M Series B in September 2025 at
$10B valuation (led by Greenoaks). $635M total funding. Previously
raised $175M Series A at $4.5B in October 2024. Hit $100M revenue
in November 2025 with ~165 employees, now ~600 employees. Signed
SF's largest office lease since OpenAI.
