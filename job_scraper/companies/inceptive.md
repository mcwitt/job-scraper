# Inceptive

## Overview

Biotech company designing RNA molecules using deep
learning. Founded in 2021 by Jakob Uszkoreit (co-author
of "Attention Is All You Need" Transformer paper) and
Rhiju Das (Stanford biochemistry professor). Based in
Palo Alto, CA.

## Technical Focus

- Generative AI for RNA molecule design
- Transformer-based models for biological sequences
- mRNA therapeutics and vaccine optimization
- Computational RNA structure prediction
- Wet lab validation of AI-designed RNA

## Scale & Stage

Private, Series A. Raised ~$120M total across two
rounds, including a $100M round led by Andreessen
Horowitz and Nvidia (2023). Investors include a16z,
Obvious Ventures, Section 32, and Nvidia. Small team,
estimated 30-50 employees.

## Hiring

Elite team bridging frontier AI and RNA biology.
Founded by one of the inventors of the Transformer
architecture. Roles in ML research, computational
biology, and RNA science. Strong preference for
candidates who can work across AI and biology.
Academic rigor with startup intensity.

## Recent Context

Demonstrated AI-designed RNA molecules performing as
expected in mouse models (early 2025). Founder Jakob
Uszkoreit active as angel investor in adjacent biotech
(e.g., Inductive Bio). Positioned at the intersection
of LLM scaling and therapeutic RNA design. Building
toward clinical-stage RNA therapeutics.
