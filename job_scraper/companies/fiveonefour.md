# Fiveonefour

## Overview

Fiveonefour (514) builds open-source developer tooling
and hosting infrastructure for AI-assisted analytics
engineering. The company's flagship product, MooseStack,
is an open-source development harness that lets AI coding
agents (Claude, Cursor, Copilot) build and deploy
real-time analytical workloads on ClickHouse and other
OLAP databases. Founded circa 2023, headquartered in
Montreal, QC, Canada.

## Technical Focus

- Open-source analytics engineering framework (MooseStack)
- ClickHouse and OLAP database tooling
- LLM/AI agent integration for data engineering tasks
- ETL/ELT pipelines, schema evolution, semantic layers
- Cloud hosting platform for production analytics
- Developer experience for analytical API development

## Scale & Stage

Private, seed/early-stage. Backed by Dimension Capital.
Small team (~20–40 employees) distributed primarily in
Canada. Revenue from cloud platform subscriptions and
enterprise contracts.

## Hiring

Fiveonefour hires full-stack engineers with LLM/AI
experience, principal UX/design engineers, forward
deployed engineers (customer-facing technical roles),
developer relations leads, and account executives. The
company values deep technical depth combined with
customer empathy. Forward deployed roles require comfort
working directly with customers on analytics
infrastructure problems.

## Recent Context

Fiveonefour is growing adoption of MooseStack in the
developer community and expanding its enterprise customer
base. The company is investing in AI agent capabilities
within its platform to further reduce the manual work of
analytics engineering, positioning itself at the
intersection of the AI coding agent and modern data
stack trends.
