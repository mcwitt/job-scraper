# Xaira Therapeutics

## Overview

AI-driven drug discovery company combining machine
learning with therapeutic development. Launched April
2024, incubated by ARCH Venture Partners and Foresite
Labs. Co-founded by David Baker (Nobel laureate, UW
Institute for Protein Design). Led by CEO Marc
Tessier-Lavigne. HQ in South San Francisco.

## Technical Focus

- AI-powered protein and antibody design
- RFdiffusion and RFantibody generative models
- De novo molecule engineering for hard-to-drug targets
- Computational and experimental drug discovery
- Large-scale biological data generation

## Scale & Stage

Private, Series A. Launched with $1B+ in committed
funding from ARCH, Foresite, F-Prime, NEA, Sequoia,
Lux Capital, Lightspeed, and others. ~190 employees
as of early 2026. Valued at ~$3.5B.

## Hiring

Rapidly scaling — doubled headcount since launch.
Roles in ML research, computational biology, protein
engineering, and clinical development. Board includes
former FDA Commissioner Scott Gottlieb and Nobel
laureate Carolyn Bertozzi. High hiring bar; attracts
top academic and industry talent.

## Recent Context

Relocated HQ to Gateway of Pacific III campus in
South SF. Appointed Dr Paulo Fontoura as CMO and
Dr Hetu Kamisetty as CTO (early 2025). Advancing
toward clinical programs though specific pipeline
details remain undisclosed. Continued aggressive
hiring despite broader biotech market headwinds.
