# Fluence Energy

## Overview

Fluence is a global leader in battery energy storage system (BESS)
integration, providing technology products and services for utility-scale
energy storage. A joint venture originally formed by Siemens and AES, the
company delivers storage hardware, software optimization, and asset
management across global markets.

## Technical Focus

- Utility-scale battery energy storage system integration
- Smartstack modular storage platform
- Energy management and optimization software
- Digital intelligence and AI-driven asset optimization
- Behind-the-meter storage for data centers

## Scale & Stage

Public (NASDAQ: FLNC). FY2025 revenue of $2.3B. Record Q4 2025 order
intake of $1.4B. FY2026 guidance of $3.2B-$3.6B revenue. Named top-three
global BESS provider by S&P Global. System Integrator of the Year three
consecutive years.

## Hiring

Fluence hires across hardware engineering, software development, project
management, and energy market operations. Engineering roles span power
electronics, controls systems, battery management, and software platform
development. The company values experience with large-scale energy
infrastructure and grid-connected systems.

Given its global footprint, Fluence looks for candidates who can work
across regions and navigate complex utility and regulatory environments.
The engineering bar emphasizes systems thinking and the ability to deliver
reliable, grid-scale solutions. Over 30 GWh of their pipeline involves
data center projects, creating demand for software and systems roles.

## Recent Context

Fluence reported FY2025 revenue of $2.3B with a record $1.4B in Q4 orders.
The company is targeting FEOC (Foreign Entity of Concern) compliance by
regulatory deadlines and expanding into behind-the-meter data center
storage, with over 30 GWh of data center projects in its pipeline. FY2026
revenue guidance is $3.2B-$3.6B with $40M-$60M adjusted EBITDA.
