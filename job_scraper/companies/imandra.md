# Imandra

## Overview

Automated reasoning and formal verification company
building tools that mathematically verify algorithm
correctness. Co-founded in 2014 by Grant Passmore
(computational logic researcher, Edinburgh) and
Denis Ignatovich (former Central Risk Trading Desk
lead at Deutsche Bank). Headquartered in Austin, TX
with offices in the UK and Europe.

## Technical Focus

- ImandraX: cloud-native automated reasoning engine
- IML (Imandra Modeling Language): pure OCaml subset
  for expressing verifiable algorithms
- SMT solving, symbolic AI, and theorem proving
- Formal verification of financial trading systems
  and regulatory compliance
- Bounded and unbounded verification with computable
  counterexamples

## Scale & Stage

Private, Seed stage. Raised ~$10M total (Seed round
in 2019 from IQ Capital, AlbionVC, Live Oak
Ventures). Approximately 20 employees. Small team
with deep expertise in formal methods, logic, and
OCaml.

## Hiring

Highly specialized team hiring automated reasoning
researchers, OCaml engineers, and formal methods
experts. Financial sector domain knowledge valued.
Small company where individual contributors have
outsized impact. Strong academic backgrounds
typical across the team.

## Recent Context

Building out ImandraX as a cloud-native "Reasoning
as a Service" platform. Expanding beyond financial
services into broader algorithm verification use
cases. OCaml ecosystem plays a key role, leveraging
the language's presence in finance (Jane Street,
LexiFi) for market validation.
