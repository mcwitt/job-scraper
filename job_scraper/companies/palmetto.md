# Palmetto

## Overview

Palmetto operates a clean energy software and fulfillment platform that
connects homeowners with solar, storage, and energy efficiency
solutions. The company's technology platform handles the end-to-end
workflow including rooftop analysis, system design, financing
(LightReach leasing/PPA), permitting, and ongoing energy intelligence.

## Technical Focus

- Clean Energy Operating Platform (sales, financing, fulfillment, permitting)
- Rooftop solar potential analysis and automated system design
- LightReach financing platform (leases and PPAs)
- Energy intelligence and customer experience software

## Scale & Stage

Private. ~650 employees. Headquartered in Charlotte, NC. Secured $1.2B
in financing in early 2025 (Morgan Stanley, Truist Bank). Closed $420M
in asset-backed securitization in October 2025, bringing total 2025 ABS
issuances to over $716M. Total venture funding of ~$491M across
multiple rounds.

## Hiring

Palmetto hires across software engineering, data science, operations,
and solar energy roles. The company's platform-centric model means
engineering work focuses on marketplace software, financing workflows,
and energy analytics rather than physical installation.

The company operates with a distributed workforce and has gone through
headcount adjustments as the residential solar market fluctuated.
Candidates with experience in fintech, marketplace platforms, or energy
technology are strong fits. Appointed new CFO Liz Coddington in
February 2026, signaling continued growth focus.

## Recent Context

Palmetto's LightReach financing platform has become its primary growth
engine, with new partnerships like SUNation Energy adopting it for 2026
residential solar deployments. The company raised significant capital
through asset-backed securitizations in 2025, positioning itself as a
financing-first platform in the residential solar space.
