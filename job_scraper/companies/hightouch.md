# Hightouch

## Overview

Hightouch provides a composable customer data platform
(CDP) and AI decisioning engine that syncs data from
cloud warehouses to business tools via reverse ETL.
Founded in 2019 by Tejas Manohar, Joshua Curl, and
Kashish Gupta, the company is headquartered in San
Francisco, CA. Customers include PetSmart, Spotify,
and Accor Hotels.

## Technical Focus

- Reverse ETL from warehouses to 200+ SaaS tools
- Composable CDP built on existing data infrastructure
- AI decisioning for marketing personalization
- Audience segmentation and identity resolution
- Native integrations with Snowflake, Databricks,
  BigQuery

## Scale & Stage

Private, Series C (Jul 2025) with an $80M raise in
Feb 2025 led by Sapphire Ventures, reaching a $1.2B
unicorn valuation. Total raised ~$172M from Amplify,
Bain Capital, ICONIQ, and Y Combinator. Approximately
500 employees as of early 2026. Founded 2019.

## Hiring

Hightouch hires software engineers, ML engineers, and
go-to-market roles. The engineering team builds data
sync infrastructure, identity resolution systems,
and AI decisioning models. San Francisco HQ with a
rapidly growing EMEA team (5 to 32 people in 2025).

## Recent Context

In February 2025, Hightouch raised $80M at a $1.2B
valuation. The company was named a Leader in the 2025
Gartner Magic Quadrant for Customer Data Platforms
(published January 2026). Revenue more than doubled
in the past year, driven by AI Decisioning and the
composable CDP product. Hightouch has benefited from
Fivetran's acquisition of competitor Census in May
2025.
