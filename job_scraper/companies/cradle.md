# Cradle

## Overview

AI-powered protein engineering platform based in Amsterdam that uses
generative AI to design and optimize proteins with specific properties.
Replaces traditional trial-and-error protein engineering with ML models
that reverse-engineer protein sequences for desired functions.

## Technical Focus

- Generative AI models for protein sequence design and optimization
- Antibody discovery and optimization
- Integration with experimental wet-lab workflows
- ML-driven prediction of protein properties (potency, safety, manufacturability)
- Platform accessible as SaaS for pharma R&D teams

## Scale & Stage

Private. ~80 employees (doubled headcount in 2025), headquartered in
Amsterdam with expanding US presence. Raised $73M Series B (November 2024)
led by IVP, with Index Ventures and Kindred Capital. Total funding over
$100M. Grew from 2 customers at Series A to 21 customers including 6 of
the top 25 pharma companies.

## Hiring

Growing rapidly across engineering, ML research, and commercial roles.
Technical bar emphasizes ML applied to biology, protein science, and
platform engineering. US expansion means new hiring across both
Amsterdam and US locations.

Culture is product-focused with tight integration between ML research
and customer-facing delivery. Strong pharma customer base (J&J, AbbVie,
Novo Nordisk) validates the platform and provides real-world protein
engineering challenges to work on.

## Recent Context

Announced in December 2025 that the platform now powers 50+ projects
across 6 of the top 25 pharma companies. Signed a three-year strategic
collaboration with Bayer in January 2026 for AI-powered antibody
discovery and optimization. Doubled headcount and expanded US operations
throughout 2025.
