# Sparkfund

## Overview

Sparkfund is a subscription-based energy-as-a-service
provider that helps utilities deploy distributed energy
resources (DERs) at scale. The platform handles procurement,
financing, installation, and ongoing management of energy
technology upgrades for commercial and institutional
buildings. Founded in 2013, Sparkfund is headquartered in
Washington, DC.

## Technical Focus

- Energy-tech-as-a-service subscription platform
- Distributed energy resource (DER) deployment
- Battery storage network orchestration
- Building energy technology procurement and management
- Utility program design and implementation
- Asset lifecycle management and monitoring

## Scale & Stage

Private, raised $50-116M total (estimates vary by source)
over 9+ rounds. Investors include Lago Innovation Fund,
E8 Ventures, Green Edge Ventures, and RET Ventures.
Company size is small (estimated under 100 employees).
Operates primarily through utility partnerships.

## Hiring

Sparkfund hires software engineers, energy analysts, project
managers, and business development roles. The platform
requires engineering across subscription billing, asset
management, and utility integration. Domain knowledge in
energy markets, DERs, or utility programs is a strong
differentiator for candidates.

## Recent Context

Sparkfund partnered with Xcel Energy on a proposed
distributed battery storage network across Minnesota,
deploying DERs to support grid infrastructure. The company
continues to grow its utility partnership channel as
utilities increasingly look to third parties for DER
program management and deployment services.
