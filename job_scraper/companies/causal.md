# Causal

## Overview

Business planning platform replacing spreadsheets for financial
modeling, forecasting, and scenario analysis. Built a custom
calculation engine scaling to billions of cells with human-readable
formulas, live data integrations, and interactive dashboards. Acquired
by Lucanet in October 2024.

## Technical Focus

- Custom multidimensional calculation engine (~40K lines of code)
- Data-oriented engineering for order-of-magnitude performance gains
- Live integrations with accounting (QuickBooks, Xero), CRMs, Stripe, HRIS
- Scenario analysis, sensitivity analysis, and uncertainty modeling
- Cloud-based platform with no-code modeling experience

## Scale & Stage

Acquired by Lucanet (Oct 2024). ~50 employees, primarily in UK and US.
Raised $25.5M total funding pre-acquisition. Founded 2019 by Taimur
Abdaal and Lukas Kobis. Operating as standalone business unit within
Lucanet with original team intact.

## Hiring

Small, tight-knit engineering team. Pre-acquisition, the company
valued deep technical ability — their blog documents sophisticated
data-oriented engineering work on the calculation engine. Expect
candidates to be comfortable working on performance-critical systems
and complex data structures.

As part of Lucanet, the team continues operating independently for
now. Engineering culture likely retains its startup character with
emphasis on ownership and technical depth, though long-term direction
will be shaped by Lucanet's enterprise platform strategy.

## Recent Context

Joined Lucanet to deliver Extended Planning and Analysis (xP&A) as
part of the Lucanet CFO Solution Platform. Product remains fully
operational as a standalone unit. Scaled their spreadsheet engine from
thousands to billions of cells through fundamental data structure
redesign.
