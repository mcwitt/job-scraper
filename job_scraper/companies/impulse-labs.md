# Impulse Labs

## Overview
Impulse Labs builds high-performance sustainable home
appliances. Flagship product is an induction cooktop with
an integrated 3 kWh battery, delivering up to 10,000W per
burner without requiring a new electrical panel. Founded
by Sam D'Amico (CEO, former Facebook Product Architect).
HQ in San Francisco, CA.

## Technical Focus
- Battery-integrated induction cooking appliances
- Power electronics and energy storage at appliance scale
- Whole-home electrification without panel upgrades
- Proprietary modular battery-integrated power electronics
  platform (unveiled at KBIS 2025)
- UL-certified battery-integrated appliance (first of its
  kind)

## Scale & Stage

- Small, rapidly growing team
