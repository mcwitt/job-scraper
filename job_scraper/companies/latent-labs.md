# Latent Labs

## Overview

AI protein design company using generative models to
make biology programmable. Founded by Dr Simon Kohl,
former co-lead of DeepMind's protein design team and
senior research scientist on AlphaFold2. Incorporated
in London (mid-2023), emerged from stealth Feb 2025.
Co-headquartered in London and San Francisco.

## Technical Focus

- Generative AI for de novo protein design
- Foundation models for protein structure (LatentX)
- Computational protein design and engineering
- Web-based protein design platform (LatentX2)
- Wet lab validation (SF-based)

## Scale & Stage

Private, Series A. Raised $50M total including a $40M
Series A co-led by Radical Ventures and Sofinnova
Partners. Angel investors include Jeff Dean (Google),
Aidan Gomez (Cohere), and Mati Staniszewski
(ElevenLabs). ~20 employees.

## Hiring

Small, elite team drawn from DeepMind, Microsoft,
and Cambridge. High bar for ML and computational
biology talent. London team focuses on AI model
development; SF team handles wet lab and computational
protein design. Expect early-stage startup pace with
outsized individual contribution.

## Recent Context

Launched LatentX2 web-based AI model (Jul 2025) to
democratize protein design for academic and industry
users. Enables novel protein design with precise
atomic structures directly in browser. Rapidly growing
from stealth-stage startup to platform company.
