# Plus Power

## Overview

Plus Power develops, owns, and operates utility-scale
standalone battery energy storage systems (BESS) that provide
capacity, energy, and ancillary services to the grid. Founded
in 2018 by Brandon Keefe (CEO), Alex Fraenkel, and Bob Rudd.
Headquartered in The Woodlands, TX, with offices in San
Francisco, Chicago, and Miami.

## Technical Focus

- Utility-scale lithium-ion battery energy storage systems
- Transmission-connected standalone storage development
- Grid reliability and renewable integration services
- Energy market optimization and ancillary services
- Large-scale project finance and tax equity structures

## Scale & Stage

Private. Raised ~$2B in project financing since October 2023.
~255 employees. Portfolio of 11 GW across 20+ states; eight
operating facilities totaling 1,650 MW / 4,150 MWh in AZ, HI,
MA, TX, and ME.

## Hiring

Plus Power hires across energy development, project finance,
engineering, and operations. Roles require deep expertise in
power markets, energy storage, or infrastructure finance. The
team blends renewables industry veterans with technology and
finance professionals. Rapid headcount growth (from ~130 to
~255 in two years) signals active hiring across functions.

## Recent Context

Brought Cranberry Point (150 MW, Carver MA) online spring
2025 and Cross Town (175 MW, Gorham ME) online February 2026,
making them the two largest BESS on the ISO New England grid.
Both facilities were 100% available during Winter Storm Fern.
Secured $160M in tax equity from Morgan Stanley for the two
New England projects. Won a 20-year PNM contract for the
150 MW / 600 MWh Corazon project in New Mexico.
