# Granica

## Overview

AI infrastructure company building the world's first
AI Data Readiness Platform — tools for data optimization,
compression, and foundational data systems for AI
workloads. Founded 2019 by Tarang Vaish and Rahul
Ponnala. HQ in Mountain View, CA.

## Technical Focus

- AI data readiness and optimization platform
- Data compression and deduplication for AI pipelines
- Foundational data systems for large-scale ML
- Tabular and structured machine learning research
- Cloud data infrastructure efficiency

## Scale & Stage

Private. $45M Series A (June 2023) from NEA, Bain
Capital Ventures, and former Tesla CFO Deepak Ahuja.
Total funding ~$49M. Relatively small team, scaling
engineering and research.

## Hiring

Bay Area onsite roles across research, engineering,
go-to-market, and operations. Looking for staff-level
engineers in foundational data systems, research
scientists in ML, and enterprise sales. Engineering
Manager roles suggest team growth phase.

## Recent Context

Positioned at the intersection of data infrastructure
and AI efficiency — helping enterprises reduce the cost
and complexity of preparing data for AI training and
inference at scale.
