# Perplexity

## Overview

AI-powered answer engine and search platform that
delivers cited, real-time answers by combining large
language models with live web indexing. Founded in 2022
by Aravind Srinivas, Denis Yarats, Johnny Ho, and Andy
Konwinski. Headquartered in San Francisco.

## Technical Focus

- AI search and retrieval-augmented generation (RAG)
- Large language model orchestration and routing
- Real-time web indexing and citation extraction
- Comet AI browser with agentic browsing capabilities
- Enterprise AI search and knowledge management
- Embedding models and deep research pipelines

## Scale & Stage

Private, Series E+ (~$1.7B+ raised). Valued at ~$20B
as of late 2025. ~1,500 employees. Revenue ~$200M ARR,
processing 60M+ queries per day with 30M+ monthly
active users. Backed by NVIDIA, Jeff Bezos, Accel,
SoftBank, IVP, NEA.

## Hiring

Competitive hiring bar; engineering roles span
full-stack, backend, ML, and infrastructure with strong
Python and TypeScript emphasis. Compensation ranges
$120K-$440K including equity. Values autonomy, speed,
and ability to ship. Four days per week in office (SF,
NYC, Austin, DC, London, Berlin, Belgrade).
