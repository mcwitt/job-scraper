# Helix

## Overview

Population genomics company enabling health systems
and life science companies to integrate genomic data
into clinical care. Founded in 2015. Headquartered
in San Mateo, CA.

## Technical Focus

- Large-scale genomic data pipelines and
  bioinformatics infrastructure
- Clinical informatics and population health analytics
- Data engineering (dbt, Snowflake/BigQuery, Airflow)
- Microservices architecture (Python, Go, TypeScript)
- AI tooling integration (Copilot, Claude, Gemini)
- CLIA/CAP-regulated laboratory information systems

## Scale & Stage

Private, $435M total funding. ~300-500 employees.
Operates CLIA-certified, CAP-accredited genomics lab.
Partners with major health systems nationwide.

## Hiring

Engineering roles span data, infrastructure, cloud,
and bioinformatics. Strong data engineering focus —
dbt, SQL, cloud data platforms. Hybrid model with
office in Mexico City (WeWork Varsovia) plus US-based
remote roles. Internal AI team supporting engineering
productivity.

## Recent Context

Positioning as the leading population genomics
platform for health systems. Expanding genomic data
integration into routine clinical care. Growing
engineering team in Mexico City alongside US-based
workforce.
