# Center for AI Safety

## Overview

Nonprofit research institute focused on reducing
societal-scale risks from artificial intelligence. Founded
in 2022 by Dan Hendrycks and Oliver Zhang. Headquartered
in San Francisco. Best known for the May 2023 "Statement
on AI Risk" signed by hundreds of AI researchers and
industry leaders warning of extinction-level risk.

## Technical Focus

- Technical AI safety research and benchmarks
- Compute cluster supporting ~20 external research labs
- AI safety field-building and education ("Intro to ML
  Safety" course)
- Evaluation and benchmarking (co-developed Remote Labor
  Index with Scale AI)
- Policy advocacy via CAIS Action Fund (501(c)(4))

## Scale & Stage

501(c)(3) nonprofit. Funded by Open Philanthropy and other
donors. Small core team with significant research
infrastructure. Operates a large GPU compute cluster
shared across the AI safety research community.

## Hiring

Small, research-focused organization. Roles span ML
research, infrastructure engineering, and field-building.
Strong preference for candidates with AI safety research
experience or demonstrated ML engineering skills. Academic
culture with emphasis on high-impact publications and
open-source contributions.

## Recent Context

Releasing the Remote Labor Index (RLI) with Scale AI,
the first benchmark evaluating AI agents on real paid
freelance work. Continuing to expand compute cluster
access for safety researchers. CAIS Action Fund actively
engaged in AI policy advocacy.
