# Nextracker

## Overview

Nextracker (rebranded to Nextpower in November 2025) is the global leader
in solar tracking systems, now expanding into a full-platform energy
solutions provider. The company delivers an integrated portfolio of
tracking, power conversion, robotic cleaning, AI inspection, and
electrical balance-of-system technologies for utility-scale solar plants.

## Technical Focus

- Single-axis solar tracking systems (NX Horizon platform)
- Utility-scale power conversion systems (new product line)
- AI-powered visual inspection and fire detection (Onsight Technology)
- Autonomous robotic panel cleaning (Amir Robotics)
- Electrical balance-of-system (eBOS) manufacturing (Bentek acquisition)
- Roll-formed steel solar panel frames (Origami Solar acquisition)

## Scale & Stage

Public (NASDAQ: NXT). Q2 FY2026 revenue of $905M (up 42% YoY). Targeting
one-third of revenue from non-tracker products by 2030. Long-term revenue
target of $4.8B-$5.6B by FY2030. Multiple strategic acquisitions in 2025
(Amir Robotics, Onsight, Bentek, Origami Solar).

## Hiring

Nextracker/Nextpower hires across mechanical engineering, software/AI,
robotics, power electronics, and manufacturing. The acquisition spree
in 2025 has broadened the technical talent needs significantly beyond
traditional tracker engineering into AI/ML, robotics, and power
conversion systems.

The company's evolution from tracker manufacturer to integrated energy
platform creates demand for systems-level thinkers who can work across
hardware and software boundaries. Experience with utility-scale solar,
power electronics, industrial automation, or computer vision is valued.
The global footprint means many roles involve international collaboration.

## Recent Context

Nextracker rebranded to Nextpower in November 2025, signaling its
expansion beyond solar tracking. The company made four acquisitions in
2025 (Amir Robotics, Onsight Technology, Bentek Corporation, and Origami
Solar for $53M) to build an integrated energy platform. First shipments of
a new utility-scale power conversion system line are expected in 2026.
