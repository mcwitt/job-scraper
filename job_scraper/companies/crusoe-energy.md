# Crusoe Energy

## Overview

Vertically integrated AI infrastructure company that builds
and operates data centers powered by stranded and wasted
energy sources, including flare gas from oil fields. Founded
in 2018 by Chase Lochmiller (CEO) and Cully Cavness (COO).
Headquartered in Denver, Colorado with data centers across
the US, Norway, and Iceland.

## Technical Focus

- Custom modular data center design (Spark product line)
- Crusoe Cloud platform for AI/ML workloads
- Energy-first site selection and power infrastructure
- GPU orchestration and cluster management
- Software-defined networking for AI compute
- Stranded energy capture and utilization

## Scale & Stage

Private. Series E at $10B+ valuation (Oct 2025), raising
$1.375B. Total funding ~$4B across debt and equity. ~1,100
employees. Revenue projected ~$1B in 2025, targeting $2B by
2026. Divested Bitcoin mining operations in March 2025 to
focus entirely on AI infrastructure.

## Hiring

Aggressively hiring across cloud engineering, SRE, security,
data center operations, networking, and solutions engineering.
~400 open roles. Cross-disciplinary culture spanning energy,
manufacturing, and cloud. Interview process ~2 weeks. Rapid
growth phase with emphasis on high-impact individual
contributors and promotion from within.

## Recent Context

Key infrastructure partner in OpenAI's $500B Stargate
project. Partnered with Tallgrass on a 1.8GW (scalable to
10GW) AI campus in Wyoming. Deploying modular data centers
with Energy Vault in Texas. Acquired Israeli GPU orchestration
startup Atero for $150M (Aug 2025). Expanding internationally
in Norway (hydroelectric) and Iceland.
