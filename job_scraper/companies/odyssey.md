# Odyssey

## Overview

AI lab building general-purpose world models — a new
form of multimodal intelligence that generates
interactive, photorealistic 3D environments from video.
Founded by Oliver Cameron (ex-VP Product at Cruise,
founder of Voyage) and Jeff Hawke (founding engineer at
Wayve). HQ in Palo Alto, CA with a London presence.

## Technical Focus

- World models for interactive 3D environment generation
- Real-time streaming video generation (~40ms per frame)
- 360-degree backpack-mounted camera data collection
- Spatial consistency and long-horizon video coherence
- ML performance optimization and inference infrastructure

## Scale & Stage

Private. $27M total raised — $9M seed (GV) and $18M
Series A (EQT Ventures, GV, Air Street Capital).
Ed Catmull (Pixar co-founder) on the board. Small team
with alumni from Cruise, Waymo, Wayve, Tesla, Microsoft,
Meta, and NVIDIA.

## Hiring

Small, research-focused team. MTS roles in ML
performance, research engineering, research science,
data architecture, and infrastructure. Research
internships also available. Palo Alto and London.

## Recent Context

Demonstrated streaming 3D interactive worlds in May 2025.
Uses custom data capture rigs (backpack cameras) to build
proprietary training datasets, differentiating from
competitors training on public data alone.
