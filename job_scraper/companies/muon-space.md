# Muon Space

## Overview

End-to-end space systems company designing, building, and
operating satellite constellations for Earth observation and
climate monitoring. Founded in 2021 by Jonny Dyer (CEO),
Dan McCleese, Pascal Stang, Paul Day, and Reuben
Rohrschneider. Headquartered in Mountain View, CA with a
new 130,000 sq ft production facility in San Jose.

## Technical Focus

- Multi-sensor satellite constellations (IR, RF, EO)
- Spacecraft bus design and XL platform for larger payloads
- In-house propulsion (zinc-based thrusters via Starlight
  Engines acquisition)
- On-orbit compute and real-time decision-making
- Optical inter-satellite links (Starlink laser terminals)
- Wildfire detection (FireSat for Earth Fire Alliance)
- Environmental and weather monitoring

## Scale & Stage

Private. Raised ~$146M total through Series B (closed 2025,
led by Congruent Ventures with ACME Capital, Costanoa
Ventures, Radical Ventures, ArcTern Ventures). Employee
count ~100-200, more than doubled headcount in 2025.
Delivered 100%+ YoY revenue growth two years running.
