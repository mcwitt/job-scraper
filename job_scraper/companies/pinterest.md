# Pinterest

## Overview

Visual discovery and bookmarking platform with 619 million monthly
active users. Users search, save, and shop through image and video
"pins" organized into boards. Revenue comes from advertising, with
growing investment in shopping and commerce features. Heavy ML/AI
user -- personalization, visual search, and recommendations are core
to the product experience.

## Technical Focus

- Recommendation systems and personalization at scale (hundreds of
  millions of ML inferences per second)
- Visual search and computer vision
- Ads ranking and monetization ML
- MLEnv: unified ML engine standardizing training and inference
- Shopping and commerce graph
- Large-scale data infrastructure

## Scale & Stage

Public (NYSE: PINS). ~5,300 employees. 2025 revenue of ~$4.2B,
growing ~16% YoY. 619M MAUs as of Q4 2025. Profitable and
cash-flow positive. Guiding Q1 2026 revenue of ~$960M.

## Hiring

ML-heavy engineering organization -- hundreds of ML engineers running
hundreds of thousands of training jobs per month. Hiring across
recommendation systems, visual search, ads ranking, and shopping.
Opened a major AI and engineering hub in Zurich in 2025. Culture
emphasizes impact at scale; even a small team of ML engineers
supports enormous inference volumes.

## Recent Context

Reached record 619M MAUs in Q4 2025, with user growth of 12% YoY.
Expanded headcount by ~600 employees in 2025-2026. Opened Zurich
AI hub focused on recommendations, visual search, and shopping.
Continuing to invest in commerce features to convert visual
discovery into transactions.
