# Array Technologies

## Overview

Array Technologies is a leading manufacturer of ground-mounted solar
tracking systems for utility-scale solar projects. The company's DuraTrack
platform optimizes energy yield by tilting solar panels to follow the sun
throughout the day, and has expanded into fixed-tilt systems and foundation
infrastructure through acquisitions.

## Technical Focus

- Single-axis solar tracking systems (DuraTrack platform)
- Fixed-tilt mounting and foundation infrastructure (APA Solar acquisition)
- Integrated tracker + foundation solutions
- Domestic content manufacturing for IRA compliance
- International product portfolio expansion

## Scale & Stage

Public (NASDAQ: ARRY). FY2025 revenue of $1.28B (up 40% YoY). Adjusted
EBITDA of $187.6M. Record $2.2B orderbook. FY2026 guidance of $1.4B-$1.5B
revenue. Revolving credit facility upsized to $370M in February 2026.
35% volume growth in 2025.

## Hiring

Array hires mechanical engineers, structural engineers, software
developers, and field operations staff. Engineering roles focus on tracker
system design, structural analysis, controls and automation, and
manufacturing process improvement. The company also hires for project
engineering and technical sales.

With the APA Solar acquisition broadening the product line and
international expansion underway, Array values candidates who can work
across product development, manufacturing, and global deployment. New
product launches planned for 2026 (integrated tracker/foundation
solutions) create opportunities in R&D and product engineering.

## Recent Context

Array reported 40% revenue growth in 2025 with a record $2.2B orderbook
and 35% volume growth. The company completed its $179M acquisition of APA
Solar, adding fixed-tilt and foundation capabilities, and plans to launch
integrated tracker/foundation products in 2026. Leadership changes include
new Global Chief Revenue Officer and Chief Product Officer appointments in
January 2026.
