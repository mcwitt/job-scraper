# OpenAI

## Overview

AI research and deployment company building general-purpose AI systems.
Creator of ChatGPT, the GPT model family, and the o-series reasoning
models. Dominant player in the commercial LLM market with both consumer
and enterprise products.

## Technical Focus

- Large language models (GPT-5.x family, o3/o4 reasoning models)
- Multimodal AI: vision, audio, image generation, video (Sora)
- AI agents and tool use (Codex, deep research)
- Inference infrastructure and model serving at scale
- Alignment and safety research
- Developer APIs and platform (Realtime API, embeddings, fine-tuning)

## Scale & Stage

Private, transitioning toward for-profit structure with IPO planned for
2027. Raised $110B in February 2026 at $730B pre-money valuation
(SoftBank, NVIDIA, Amazon). ~7,000 employees. Annualized revenue ~$25B
as of early 2026, projecting $85B by 2030. Average stock-based
compensation ~$1.5M per employee.

## Hiring

Extremely competitive. OpenAI pays the highest equity compensation of
any tech startup in history. Strong preference for candidates with deep
ML research experience, systems engineering at scale, or demonstrated
contributions to the field. Expect rigorous technical interviews with
emphasis on first-principles thinking and research depth.

The company hires across research, engineering, product, and policy.
Adjacent backgrounds can succeed in platform and infrastructure roles,
but core research positions typically require publications or equivalent
demonstrated expertise.

## Recent Context

Launched GPT-5.2 and o3/o4-mini reasoning models in 2025, unifying
reasoning, coding, and multimodal capabilities. Raised a massive $110B
round in early 2026 at $730B valuation and is preparing for an IPO
filing in H2 2026.
