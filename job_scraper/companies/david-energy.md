# David Energy

## Overview

David Energy is a software-driven retail electricity
provider that uses technology to optimize energy
procurement, load management, and distributed energy
resource (DER) dispatch. Founded by Brian Maxwell, James
McGinniss, and Ahmed Salman, the company is headquartered
in Brooklyn, NY. David Energy operates as both an
electricity supplier and a virtual power plant (VPP)
operator in competitive retail markets.

## Technical Focus

- Retail electricity supply with real-time optimization
- Virtual power plant aggregation and dispatch
- DER management (batteries, thermostats, EVs)
- Wholesale market bidding (ERCOT, NYISO)
- Customer energy monitoring and demand response

## Scale & Stage

Private, Series B ($23M in Apr 2024) led by Cathay
Innovation. Total raised ~$49M across 5 rounds.
Approximately 30-50 employees. Founded 2019.

## Hiring

David Energy hires software engineers, energy traders,
and operations roles. The team builds real-time energy
management and trading systems. Small, fast-moving
team operating at the intersection of software and
energy markets.

## Recent Context

David Energy has been expanding its VPP capabilities,
participating in industry panels on DER integration
into wholesale markets. The company is positioned to
benefit from rapid electricity demand growth in Texas
and the mid-Atlantic, driven by data center and
industrial load expansion.
