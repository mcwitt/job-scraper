# Neurophos

## Overview

Photonic computing startup building optical processing
units (OPUs) for AI inference using micron-scale
metamaterial optical modulators -- 10,000x smaller than
previous photonic elements. Founded in 2020 by
Dr. Patrick Bowen (CEO) and Dr. Andrew Traverso.
Headquartered in Austin, TX.

## Technical Focus

- Optical processing units (OPUs) for AI acceleration
- Micron-scale metamaterial optical modulators
- Exaflop-scale photonic AI chip design
- Photonic computing for data center inference workloads

## Scale & Stage

Private, Series A. Raised $110M Series A in January
2026 led by Gates Frontier ($118M total). Investors
include M12 (Microsoft Ventures), Aramco Ventures,
Bosch Ventures, and Carbon Direct Capital. ~30-40
employees, planning to hire 80 more in Austin.

## Hiring

Hiring across chip design, photonics engineering, and
software. Team includes veterans from NVIDIA, Apple,
Samsung, Intel, AMD, Meta, ARM, and Lightmatter.
Expect strong backgrounds in photonic device design,
ASIC/chip architecture, or ML systems optimization.
Opening a Silicon Valley engineering site.

## Recent Context

Closed $110M Series A in January 2026, one of the
largest Series A rounds for a photonic computing
company. Partnering with Norwegian data center
operator Terakraft for a real-world OPU pilot in
2027, with production chips targeted for mid-2028.
Expanding Austin HQ and opening Silicon Valley office.
