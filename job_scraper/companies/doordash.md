# DoorDash

## Overview

On-demand delivery platform and the largest food delivery
service in the US (~56% market share). Also delivers
groceries, retail, and convenience items. Founded in 2013
by Tony Xu, Andy Fang, Stanley Tang, and Evan Moore.
Headquartered in San Francisco, CA. Operates in the US,
Canada, Australia, New Zealand, and via Deliveroo and Wolt
across 45+ countries.

## Technical Focus

- Real-time logistics and delivery optimization
- Kotlin backend microservices with gRPC
- Streaming data infra: Kafka, Flink (220 TB/day),
  Spark, Apache Pinot
- Delta Lake on S3 lakehouse architecture
- ML/AI for personalization, search ranking, ETA
  prediction, and fraud detection
- Monorepo with independently deployable services
- ChatGPT integration for grocery ordering

## Scale & Stage

Public (NASDAQ: DASH). ~23,700 employees (~2,400 in
engineering). 2025 revenue ~$13.7B, up ~28% YoY.
46.3 million active users; 26 million DashPass and
Wolt+ subscribers. Acquired Deliveroo for ~$12B in
2025 and Wolt in 2022. Reached profitability in 2025.

## Hiring

Engineering hubs in San Francisco, Seattle, New York,
and Los Angeles. Values ownership, humility, and bias
toward action. Active intern and new-grad pipeline.
Structured career ladders with clear performance
expectations. Competitive comp (E3 new-grad base
~$145-155k + bonus + RSUs).

## Recent Context

Acquired Deliveroo (mid-2025) to expand across the UK
and Europe. Became #1 in US third-party grocery and
retail order volume. Partnered with Yelp for AI-powered
restaurant recommendations. Added SNAP/EBT acceptance
at 35,000+ stores via Dollar General. Launched retail
partnerships with DSW, Old Navy, Family Dollar, and
Hibbett (January 2026).
