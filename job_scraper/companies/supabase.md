# Supabase

## Overview

Supabase is an open-source alternative to Firebase built on
PostgreSQL, providing a backend-as-a-service platform with
authentication, auto-generated APIs, real-time subscriptions,
file storage, and vector tooling. Founded in 2020 by Paul
Copplestone (CEO) and Ant Wilson (CTO), Supabase is a fully
remote company (YC S20).

## Technical Focus

- Managed PostgreSQL databases with extensions
- Auto-generated REST and GraphQL APIs (PostgREST)
- Real-time subscriptions via WebSockets
- Authentication and row-level security
- Edge Functions (Deno-based serverless)
- Vector toolkit for AI/embedding workloads
- File storage with CDN integration

## Scale & Stage

Private, valued at $5B (Oct 2025 Series E, $100M). Raised
$500M total. Series D ($200M at $2B) closed just four months
prior in Jun 2025. Over 4 million developer users.
Approximately 150-200 employees. Growth accelerated by the
rise of AI-powered "vibe coding" tools.

## Hiring

Supabase is fully remote and hires engineers, developer
advocates, and support roles. Engineering emphasizes
PostgreSQL expertise, distributed systems, and open-source
contribution. The team is small relative to user base and
funding, so individual contributors own large surface areas.
The company allowed community members to buy stock in its
Series E.

## Recent Context

Supabase raised $300M across two rounds in 2025, jumping
from $2B to $5B valuation in four months. The company
benefited significantly from the vibe-coding wave, with
its developer base growing from 1M to 4M+ in a year.
Open-source community engagement remains central to
the company's growth strategy.
