# Axiom

## Overview

Axiom is an AI-driven drug toxicity prediction company
building the largest human toxicology dataset to train
multimodal chemistry-to-biology models. Founded by Brandon
White (CEO) and Alex Beatson, headquartered in San
Francisco, CA. The company aims to reduce the
pharmaceutical industry's reliance on animal models in
drug discovery and development.

## Technical Focus

- Multimodal AI/ML models mapping chemistry to biology
- Human toxicology dataset curation and annotation
- Computational drug safety assessment
- Mass spectrometry and analytical chemistry data
- Backend systems and data engineering for scientific
  workflows

## Scale & Stage

Private, early-stage. Backed by Dimension Capital and
other life science/AI investors. Small team (~20-40
employees) with roles spanning computational science,
machine learning, engineering, and data.

## Hiring

Axiom hires computational scientists with backgrounds in
biology, medicinal chemistry, and mass spectrometry, as
well as ML engineers, data engineers, and backend systems
engineers. Roles require comfort at the intersection of
deep scientific domain knowledge and modern ML/data
infrastructure.

## Recent Context

Axiom launched publicly in 2024 with coverage in outlets
including Wired and STAT News around the vision of
replacing animal testing in pharma with human-derived
toxicology AI. The company is building out its core
platform and computational team.
