# Cloudflare

## Overview

Internet infrastructure company operating one of the world's largest
edge networks, serving ~21% of all websites. Products span CDN,
DDoS protection, DNS, Zero Trust security (SASE/SSE), and a growing
developer platform (Workers, R2, D1, Workers AI). Positioned at the
intersection of network security, edge computing, and AI inference.

## Technical Focus

- Global edge network and content delivery
- Zero Trust / SASE platform (Cloudflare One) with post-quantum
  encryption -- first SASE provider with standards-compliant PQ crypto
- Serverless compute (Workers), object storage (R2), SQL (D1)
- Edge AI inference (Workers AI on Nvidia GPUs across the network)
- DNS, HTTP/2, QUIC, TCP/UDP protocol engineering
- Data platform built on Apache Iceberg and R2

## Scale & Stage

Public (NYSE: NET). ~5,200 employees. 2025 revenue ~$2.0B, growing
~28% YoY. ~332,000 paying customers as of Q4 2025. Guiding for
~$2.8B revenue in 2026. Profitable on a free cash flow basis.

## Hiring

Large engineering org across network engineering, distributed systems,
security, and developer platform teams. Known for a strong engineering
blog culture and shipping frequently. Announced goal to hire 1,111
interns in 2026. Fast-tracks engineering candidates who build on the
Cloudflare developer platform.

## Recent Context

Became the first SASE platform with post-quantum encryption across
the full stack (February 2026). Launched Cloudflare Data Platform in
2025 for analytical workloads on Iceberg/R2. Added MCP server security
to Cloudflare One for GenAI usage controls. Named in 2025 Gartner
Magic Quadrant for Security Service Edge.
