# Runway

## Overview

AI video generation and world model company. Founded 2018
by Cristobal Valenzuela, Anastasis Germanidis, and
Alejandro Matamala at NYU's ITP program. Headquartered
in New York City.

## Technical Focus

- Generative video models (Gen-1 through Gen-4.5)
- World simulation and world models
- Text-to-video, image-to-video generation
- Native audio generation and multi-shot video
- Character consistency and advanced editing tools

## Scale & Stage

Private, Series E. Raised ~$860M+ total at $5.3B
valuation. $315M Series E in Feb 2026 led by General
Atlantic, with Nvidia, Adobe Ventures, AMD Ventures,
and Felicis. ~140 employees as of early 2026, growing
rapidly across research, engineering, and go-to-market.

## Hiring

Research and engineering roles spanning generative
models, video understanding, and production ML systems.
Founded by artists-turned-engineers; culture blends
creative and technical sensibilities. Strong shipping
culture. Expanding into gaming, robotics, medicine,
and climate science verticals.

## Recent Context

Released Gen-4.5 model, outperforming Google and OpenAI
on video generation benchmarks. Raised $315M Series E
in February 2026 to pre-train next-gen world models.
Expanding beyond media/entertainment into robotics,
gaming, and scientific simulation use cases.
