# Sunrun

## Overview

Sunrun is the largest residential solar and battery storage company in the
United States. The company designs, installs, finances, and monitors
rooftop solar and battery systems, and operates one of the largest
residential virtual power plants connecting home batteries to grid
programs.

## Technical Focus

- Residential solar + battery system design and installation
- Virtual power plant (VPP) orchestration across utility programs
- Home-to-grid dispatch and demand response
- Solar/storage financing and customer acquisition platforms
- Energy monitoring and fleet management software

## Scale & Stage

Public (NASDAQ: RUN). FY2025 revenue of $2.96B (up 45% YoY). Generated
$377M cash in 2025. 4 GW of networked battery capacity, 106,000+
customers in 18 home-to-grid programs delivering 425 MW during dispatches.
71% storage attachment rate. FY2026 cash generation guidance of
$250M-$450M.

## Hiring

Sunrun hires across software engineering, operations, sales, and
installation roles. Software positions focus on fleet management,
VPP dispatch optimization, customer platforms, and energy analytics.
The company also has significant field operations hiring for solar
installation and service technicians.

Engineering roles value experience with distributed systems, real-time
dispatch, energy market integration, and consumer-facing platforms. The
shift toward direct sales (reducing affiliate business by 40%+ in 2026)
and growing VPP operations create demand for software talent who can build
at scale across both consumer and grid-facing systems.

## Recent Context

Sunrun reported record 2025 results with a 71% storage attachment rate
and growing VPP capacity. The company is shifting from affiliate partners
to a direct model and closed a $500M joint venture with HASI for
residential system financing. Sunrun also partnered with NRG Energy in
Texas to build toward a 1 GW virtual power plant by 2035.
