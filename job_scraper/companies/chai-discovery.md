# Chai Discovery

## Overview

AI startup building foundation models for molecular structure
prediction and generative molecular design. Founded in 2023
by Joshua Meier (ex-OpenAI, ex-Meta FAIR where he co-led
ESM1 protein language model), Matthew McPartlon (ex-Absci AI
lead), Jacques Boitreaud (ex-Aqemia), and Jack Dent
(ex-Stripe ML). Headquartered in San Francisco.

## Technical Focus

- Molecular structure prediction (Chai-1, open-source,
  Apache 2.0, multi-task biomolecular prediction)
- De novo antibody and nanobody design (Chai-2, proprietary,
  ~16% hit rate, 100x improvement over prior methods)
- Multimodal generative models for therapeutic molecules
- Drug-like antibody design against challenging targets
- Large-scale ML infrastructure for molecular modeling

## Scale & Stage

Private, unicorn ($1.3B valuation as of Dec 2025). Raised
$225M+ total: $30M seed (Thrive Capital, OpenAI, Dimension),
$70M Series A (Aug 2025, Menlo Ventures + Anthropic Anthology
Fund), $130M Series B (Dec 2025, Oak HC/FT and General
Catalyst). Roughly 30 employees, growing fast.

## Hiring

Small, research-heavy team. Looking for ML researchers and
engineers with experience in structural biology, protein
modeling, or large-scale model training. High bar given
founders' pedigrees (Meta FAIR, OpenAI, Stripe). Expect
a fast-paced research-to-product culture.

## Recent Context

Released Chai-2 (mid-2025) for fully de novo antibody design
with experimentally validated results. Series B at unicorn
valuation (Dec 2025) backed by OpenAI and General Catalyst.
Chai-1 remains a widely used open-source baseline for
biomolecular structure prediction on GitHub.
