# DeepMind (Google DeepMind)

## Overview

AI research laboratory, now part of Alphabet as Google DeepMind
(merged with Google Brain in 2023). Founded in 2010 by Demis
Hassabis, Shane Legg, and Mustafa Suleyman. Known for
foundational AI breakthroughs. Primary offices in London and
Mountain View.

## Technical Focus

- Frontier model research (Gemini family)
- Scientific AI: protein structure (AlphaFold), materials, math
- Reinforcement learning and game-playing AI (AlphaGo, AlphaZero)
- Neuroscience-inspired AI research
- AI safety and alignment research
- Large-scale distributed training infrastructure

## Scale & Stage

Part of Alphabet (NASDAQ: GOOGL). ~2500+ researchers and
engineers. Operates with significant autonomy and research
budget. Publishes extensively in top venues.

## Hiring

Extremely selective, especially for research roles. Strong
publication record expected for research scientists. Engineering
roles require deep systems skills — distributed training, TPU/
GPU optimization, large-scale ML infrastructure. Compensation
is top-tier Google-level with London and Bay Area pay scales.

Research internships are a common pipeline. PhD strongly
preferred for research roles; engineering roles more accessible
to strong industry candidates.

## Recent Context

Leading Gemini model development for Google products. AlphaFold
impact recognized with Nobel Prize in Chemistry (2024). Expanding
applied research across science, health, and product integration
with Google.
