# Brex

## Overview

Corporate card and spend management platform built for growing
businesses. Integrates cards, expense management, bill pay,
travel, and banking into a unified financial stack. Founded in
2017 by Henrique Dubugras and Pedro Franceschi (both Brazilian
entrepreneurs). HQ in San Francisco, CA.

## Technical Focus

- Kotlin and Elixir services on Kubernetes (AWS)
- Federated GraphQL API layer with gRPC internals
- Event-driven architecture with Kafka
- PostgreSQL, ArangoDB, and Snowflake for data
- AI-native systems (multi-agent onboarding, Brex AI
  assistant for spend management)
- React.js frontend; Bazel build system
- Stablecoin / USDC payment rails via partner banks

## Scale & Stage

Private; raised ~$1.2B across 11 rounds. Last valued at
$12.3B (2021). Capital One agreed to acquire Brex for $5.15B
in January 2026; deal expected to close mid-2026. ~$700M
annualized revenue as of August 2025, growing ~50% YoY.
Approximately 1,700 employees.

## Hiring

Engineering spans software, data, security, and IT teams
with high autonomy. Roles emphasize ownership, architecture
through deployment. Interview process averages ~3 weeks with
phone screen, technical round, manager round, and VP round.
Culture values ambition, grit, and moving fast. Actively
hiring ~180 roles as of early 2026, including intern cohorts.

## Recent Context

Capital One acquisition announced January 2026 for $5.15B in
cash and stock. Fifth Third Bank partnered with Brex to power
its commercial card offering. Launched stablecoin (USDC)
payments in September 2025. Expanded into the EU market with
a Payment Institution licence, targeting early 2026 launch.
Summer 2025 release added AI upgrades, purchasing controls,
and Zip procurement integration.
