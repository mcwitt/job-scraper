# Recursion

## Overview

AI-driven drug discovery company that integrates biology, chemistry, and
machine learning to industrialize pharmaceutical R&D. Operates one of the
largest biological datasets in the world, generated through automated wet-lab
experiments at scale, and applies foundation models to accelerate every stage
of drug development.

## Technical Focus

- Large-scale biological data generation (automated microscopy, omics)
- Foundation models for drug discovery (trained on proprietary datasets)
- BioHive supercomputing infrastructure (built with NVIDIA)
- Computational chemistry and molecular design
- Clinical translation of AI-discovered candidates

## Scale & Stage

Public (NASDAQ: RXRX). ~800 employees across 4 continents (post-Exscientia
merger and subsequent 20% workforce reduction in mid-2025). Merged with
Exscientia in a $565M deal combining two leading AI pharma platforms.

## Hiring

Engineering bar is high, with emphasis on ML/AI expertise applied to
biological and chemical problems. Roles span ML research, software
engineering, data science, and computational biology. Strong preference for
candidates who can work at the intersection of software and science.

Culture emphasizes speed and iteration on a vertically integrated platform
(Recursion OS). Expect technical depth in interviews plus ability to
reason about biological systems and experimental design.

## Recent Context

Najat Khan became CEO in January 2026, with co-founder Chris Gibson
transitioning to Chairman. Achieved first AI-enabled clinical proof of
concept with REC-4881 in FAP (43% median polyp reduction in Phase 1b/2).
Trimmed three pipeline programs post-merger to focus resources on highest-
conviction assets.
