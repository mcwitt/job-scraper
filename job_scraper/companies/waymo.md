# Waymo

## Overview

Alphabet's autonomous driving subsidiary and the clear leader in
commercial robotaxi deployment. Operates fully driverless ride-hailing
services across multiple US cities, with over 400,000 paid rides per
week and expanding internationally.

## Technical Focus

- Full self-driving stack (perception, prediction, planning)
- 6th-generation Waymo Driver hardware and software
- Lidar, camera, and radar sensor fusion
- ML for autonomous driving (behavior prediction, scene understanding)
- Fleet management and operations at scale
- Simulation and testing infrastructure

## Scale & Stage

Subsidiary of Alphabet. Raised $16B round at $126B valuation. ~3,800
employees and growing rapidly (up from ~2,500 in mid-2025).
Manufacturing partnership with Magna for vehicle production. Operating
commercially in San Francisco, Phoenix, Los Angeles, Austin, and
expanding.

## Hiring

Aggressive hiring binge — headcount grew 50%+ in 2025. Roles span ML/
AI research, robotics, systems engineering, simulation, fleet
operations, and business functions. Strong preference for candidates
with experience in perception, prediction, planning, or large-scale
ML systems.

The scale of operations (millions of miles driven, hundreds of
thousands of weekly rides) means infrastructure and reliability
engineering are as important as core autonomy research. SWE roles
require comfort with safety-critical systems.

## Recent Context

Began fully autonomous operations with 6th-gen Driver (costs <$20K per
unit, 50%+ reduction from 5th-gen). Targeting 1M weekly rides by end of
2026. Expanding to San Diego, Detroit, Las Vegas, Miami, Dallas,
Nashville, DC, and internationally to London. Doubling Arizona
manufacturing capacity to 2,000+ vehicles by end of 2026.
