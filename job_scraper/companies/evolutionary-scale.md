# EvolutionaryScale

## Overview

AI research lab building protein language models (ESM family)
to understand and design proteins. Founded in 2023 by
Alexander Rives, Roshan Rao, Thomas Hayes, Halil Akin, and
Tom Sercu, all former Meta FAIR researchers. Originally HQ'd
in New York; team joined the Chan Zuckerberg Biohub in 2025,
now operating from Redwood City, CA and New York.

## Technical Focus

- Protein language models (ESM3, ESM Cambrian)
- Generative protein design (sequence, structure, function)
- Large-scale unsupervised learning on biological sequences
- Protein representation learning (ESM C up to 6B params)
- Trained on 3.15B protein sequences, 236M structures

## Scale & Stage

Private (integrated into CZ Biohub in 2025). Raised $142M
seed (Lux Capital, NVentures, Amazon) plus additional
funding totaling ~$182M. ~40 employees. Partnered with
NVIDIA BioNeMo and AWS for compute infrastructure.

## Hiring

Small, research-intensive team at the intersection of ML
and biology. Roles include research scientists (AI and
biology focus) and research engineers. Very high bar --
expects deep expertise in either ML or structural biology,
ideally both. Academic publication record valued. Hybrid
positions in Redwood City and New York.

## Recent Context

ESM3 paper published in Science (Jan 2025), demonstrating
generation of a novel fluorescent protein -- equivalent to
500M years of natural evolution. Released ESM Cambrian
model family for protein representation learning. Team
formally joined CZ Biohub in late 2025 with Alex Rives
as Head of Science, scaling up compute and research
infrastructure for next-generation biology models.
