# Mistral

## Overview

Paris-based AI company building open-weight and commercial large
language models. Europe's most valuable AI startup, positioning itself
as the European alternative to US AI labs. Founded in 2023 by former
Meta and Google DeepMind researchers including Arthur Mensch.

## Technical Focus

- Open-weight LLMs (Mistral Large, Mistral Small, Ministral)
- Reasoning models (Magistral family)
- Code generation models (Devstral)
- Efficient model architectures (mixture of experts, small models)
- Le Chat consumer product and API platform

## Scale & Stage

Private, valued at ~$14B (EUR 12B) after EUR 2B raise in September
2025. Total funding ~$3B. ~860 employees, up from 35 in early 2024.
Revenue ~$100M in 2025. Highest-valued AI startup in Europe.

## Hiring

Rapidly scaling from a small research lab to a large organization.
Strong preference for candidates with deep ML research backgrounds,
particularly in model architecture, training optimization, and
efficient inference. The team has roots in top academic programs and
major AI labs (Meta, Google DeepMind).

European headquarters in Paris with growing presence. Competitive
compensation for the European market. Culture retains startup intensity
despite rapid growth. French language not required but helpful for
integration.

## Recent Context

Secured EUR 2B investment in September 2025 at $14B valuation. Released
a steady cadence of models throughout 2025 including reasoning models
(Magistral) and coding models (Devstral 2). Signed a framework
agreement with the French Ministry of Defense in January 2026.
