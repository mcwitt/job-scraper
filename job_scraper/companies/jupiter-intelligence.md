# Jupiter Intelligence

## Overview

Jupiter Intelligence provides climate risk analytics
that help enterprises, financial institutions, and
governments quantify physical climate risk and plan
resilience investments. Founded in 2017 by climate
scientists, the company is headquartered in San Mateo,
CA. Its ClimateScore Global platform delivers
decision-grade climate intelligence.

## Technical Focus

- Physical climate risk modeling and forecasting
- ClimateScore Global analytics platform
- Jupiter Adaptation: ROI quantification for 10+
  resilience strategies
- Wildfire, flood, wind, and heat risk assessment
- Investment-grade climate analytics for finance

## Scale & Stage

Private, Series C. Raised ~$97M total including a $54M
Series C (2021). Investors include Energize Capital,
Elemental Impact, and Clearvision Ventures. Platform
trusted by 20% of the world's largest companies and
50% of the largest U.S. lenders. Won CleanTech
Analytics Company of the Year. Founded 2017.

## Hiring

Jupiter hires across climate science, data science,
software engineering, and enterprise sales. The
platform requires deep expertise in atmospheric
modeling, catastrophe risk, and financial risk
analytics. Experience with climate data, geospatial
systems, or insurance/banking risk frameworks is
valued. Science-driven culture with rigorous modeling
standards.

## Recent Context

In July 2025, Jupiter launched enhanced ClimateScore
Global with investment-grade climate analytics and
Jupiter Adaptation for resilience ROI planning. In
February 2026, Jupiter partnered with ERM to help
organizations quantify physical risk and prioritize
resilience investments. Fitch Ratings partnered with
Jupiter for its Climate Vulnerability Signals framework
covering wildfires, floods, and extreme winds.
