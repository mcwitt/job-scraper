# Energy Vault

## Overview

Energy Vault develops utility-scale energy storage solutions, originally
known for its gravity-based storage technology. The company has expanded
into battery energy storage systems (BESS) and pursues an "Own & Operate"
asset management strategy to generate recurring tolling revenue from its
storage projects.

## Technical Focus

- Gravity-based long-duration energy storage systems
- Battery energy storage system integration and deployment
- Energy management software and optimization
- Hybrid gravity + battery storage projects
- Sodium-ion battery co-development (partnership with Peak Energy)

## Scale & Stage

Public (NYSE: NRGV). Closed $135.5M financing in early 2026, ending 2025
with over $100M cash (300%+ growth over four quarters). Expanding globally
with projects in the U.S., Europe, Australia, and China.

## Hiring

Energy Vault hires across engineering, project development, and asset
management roles. Technical positions span energy storage system design,
power electronics, software for energy optimization, and construction
management for large-scale infrastructure projects.

The company values experience in utility-scale energy projects, power
systems engineering, and renewable energy development. As the business
shifts toward an own-and-operate model, operational and asset management
expertise is increasingly important.

## Recent Context

In 2026, Energy Vault partnered with Peak Energy for 1.5 GWh of sodium-ion
batteries targeting AI data centers, and signed a deal with Crusoe for
modular AI factory deployments at its Texas facility. The company also
signed a $250M framework agreement with EU Green Energy for 1.8 GWh of
BESS deployment in Europe.
