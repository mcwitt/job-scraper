# Samsara

## Overview

IoT platform company providing the Connected Operations Cloud for
organizations that depend on physical operations. Combines telematics,
video-based safety, route planning, and asset tracking to help fleets
and industrial operations run more efficiently and safely.

## Technical Focus

- IoT data platform ingesting 25+ trillion data points annually
- Computer vision and edge AI on dash cams and wearables
- Backend in Go, frontend in TypeScript/React, mobile in React Native
- Embedded software: edge AI models, multimedia, power management
- Real-time data processing, GraphQL APIs, cloud infrastructure

## Scale & Stage

Public (NYSE: IOT). ~5,200 employees. FY2026 revenue ~$1.9B ARR (up
30% YoY). Achieved first GAAP profitability in Q3 FY2026. Raised $915M
total pre-IPO; IPO in Dec 2021 at $11.5B valuation.

## Hiring

Hyper-growth hiring culture with 90+ engineering positions open at any
given time. Values customer focus, growth mindset, and long-term
thinking. Active new-grad and intern pipeline. Engineering bar focuses
on practical systems skills — Go, distributed systems, real-time data
processing, and embedded development.

Strong "build for the long term" ethos. Engineers work on high-impact
products touching physical operations across transportation,
logistics, and industrial sectors. Rapid career growth opportunities
in a scaling organization.

## Recent Context

Launched AI Multicam for 360-degree vehicle video coverage, a wearable
for frontline worker safety, and integrated route planning and
commercial navigation products. Named #1 Supply Chain & Logistics
Software Product of 2026 by G2.
