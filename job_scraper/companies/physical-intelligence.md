# Physical Intelligence

## Overview

Robotics AI company building general-purpose foundation models for
physical interaction, enabling robots to perform diverse real-world
tasks from a single model. Known as "pi" (styled as the Greek letter),
the company develops vision-language-action (VLA) models trained on
thousands of hours of real-world robot data.

## Technical Focus

- Vision-language-action (VLA) models for robotics
- pi-0 foundation model (3B parameter transformer on PaliGemma)
- pi-0 FAST: autoregressive model with Flow matching Action Space Tokenizer
- Multi-embodiment training across 7+ robot platforms and 68+ tasks
- Natural language instruction following for physical tasks

## Scale & Stage

Private. Series B of $600M (Nov 2025) led by CapitalG, total funding
~$1.1B at $5.6B valuation. ~190 employees. Investors include Sequoia,
Lux Capital, Nvidia NVentures, T. Rowe Price, Redpoint. Founded by
Karol Hausman (ex-Google DeepMind), Sergey Levine (UC Berkeley),
Chelsea Finn (Stanford), Lachy Groom, and others.

## Hiring

World-class research team with deep academic connections to Stanford
and UC Berkeley robotics labs. Hiring ML researchers, robotics
engineers, and infrastructure engineers. The founding team's pedigree
(DeepMind, Stanford, Berkeley) sets an extremely high research bar.

Scaling data collection, compute infrastructure, and hardware
partnerships, which creates roles beyond pure research — systems
engineering, data pipeline, and deployment operations.

## Recent Context

Open-sourced pi-0 code and weights in February 2026. Released pi-0
v0.6 with 2x throughput improvement on real-world tasks. Demonstrations
include cleaning unseen homes, folding laundry, organizing objects,
and following complex natural language instructions across multiple
robot embodiments.
