# Cohere

## Overview

Enterprise-focused AI company building large language models and
platforms for business applications. Differentiates on security-first
design, private cloud deployment, and enterprise-grade reliability.
Headquartered in Toronto, founded by former Google Brain researchers.

## Technical Focus

- Enterprise LLMs (Command family) optimized for RAG and tool use
- Agentic AI platform (North) for complex enterprise workflows
- Embedding models and reranking for search and retrieval
- Security-first architecture with private/sovereign cloud deployment
- Multilingual models and cross-language capabilities

## Scale & Stage

Private, Series D. Raised $500M at $6.8B valuation in August 2025
(Radical Ventures, Inovia, AMD, NVIDIA, Salesforce), plus $100M
extension in September 2025. Total funding ~$1.5B. ~840 employees.
Annualized revenue doubled to ~$100M by mid-2025.

## Hiring

Growing rapidly, particularly in applied ML, infrastructure, and
enterprise go-to-market roles. Strengthened leadership with Joelle
Pineau (former Meta FAIR head) as Chief AI Officer and former Uber exec
as CFO. Engineering bar is high with emphasis on practical ML systems
and production reliability.

Toronto-headquartered with distributed teams. Values candidates who can
bridge research and production, especially those with experience
deploying ML systems in enterprise environments with strict security
and compliance requirements.
