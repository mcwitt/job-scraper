# xAI

## Overview

AI company founded by Elon Musk building the Grok family of large
language models. Operates one of the world's largest GPU clusters
(Colossus) and integrates AI across Musk's ecosystem of companies.
Acquired by SpaceX in February 2026 in an all-stock transaction.

## Technical Focus

- Large language model development (Grok family)
- Massive GPU cluster operations (~200K GPUs, Colossus)
- AI infrastructure and supercomputing at extreme scale
- Integration with X (Twitter) platform and Musk ecosystem
- Multimodal AI and real-time information processing

## Scale & Stage

Now a wholly owned subsidiary of SpaceX after February 2026
acquisition. Valued at ~$250B in the acquisition (SpaceX at $1T
combined). Previously raised $20B in January 2026 at $230B valuation.
Investors included NVIDIA, Cisco, Fidelity, QIA, and MGX.

## Hiring

Large-scale hiring focused on ML research, infrastructure, and GPU
cluster operations. The company has undergone significant organizational
changes including layoffs of 500 data annotation workers in September
2025 and restructuring into four development teams post-SpaceX
acquisition in February 2026.

Given the Musk-ecosystem integration and rapid organizational changes,
candidates should be comfortable with high-intensity culture and
shifting priorities. Infrastructure and systems roles are particularly
valued given the scale of compute operations.

## Recent Context

Acquired by SpaceX in February 2026 in an all-stock transaction,
restructured into four development teams with some layoffs. Previously
raised $20B in January 2026, one of the largest private funding rounds
in history. The Colossus GPU cluster continues to expand through 2026.
