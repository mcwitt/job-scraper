# NewLimit

## Overview

NewLimit is a longevity biotechnology company focused on
understanding and reversing epigenetic aging. Co-founded
in 2021 by Blake Byers and Brian Armstrong (Coinbase CEO),
headquartered in San Francisco, CA. The company takes a
data-driven, functional genomics approach to identifying
epigenetic reprogramming strategies that could extend
healthy human lifespan.

## Technical Focus

- Epigenetic reprogramming and aging biology
- Functional genomics and single-cell sequencing
- Drug discovery targeting aging hallmarks
- Computational biology and bioinformatics
- Drug product development and formulation
- Immunology and metabolism research

## Scale & Stage

Private, well-funded. Raised $105M Series A in 2023 led
by Dimension Capital with participation from Andreessen
Horowitz and others. Team of approximately 40–70
scientists and operators. Brian Armstrong committed
significant personal capital at founding.

## Hiring

NewLimit hires research scientists and associates in
functional genomics, computational biology, immunology,
single-cell technologies, drug product development, and
metabolism. The company also hires for operations and
translation roles. Strong scientific credentials and
comfort with high-throughput experimental platforms are
key. Culture is research-intensive with a focus on
ambitious long-term bets.

## Recent Context

NewLimit published key research on epigenetic
reprogramming factors and continues to build its platform
for identifying interventions that restore youthful
epigenetic states. The company is deepening its drug
discovery pipeline and growing its scientific team
across biology, chemistry, and computation.
