# Relay Therapeutics

## Overview

Clinical-stage precision medicine company using computational biology
to discover small-molecule drugs targeting protein motion. The Dynamo
platform combines molecular dynamics simulations, ML models, and
experimental structural biology to understand how proteins move and
how that motion relates to function -- enabling drug design against
previously intractable targets. Public (NASDAQ: RLAY), based in
Cambridge, MA.

## Technical Focus

- Dynamo platform: ML + molecular dynamics for protein motion analysis
- Computational chemistry and structure-based drug design
- Integration of biophysics, enzymology, and medicinal chemistry
- Lead program: zovegalisib (RLY-2608), a pan-mutant selective
  PI3Ka inhibitor for breast cancer and vascular malformations
- Received FDA Breakthrough Therapy designation

## Scale & Stage

Public (NASDAQ: RLAY). Raised $520M total pre-IPO (including $400M
Series C from SoftBank). $554M cash on hand as of December 2025,
projected runway into 2029. ~200 employees (down from ~260 after
multiple rounds of restructuring in 2024-2025). $15.4M revenue in
2025.

## Hiring

Cambridge, MA based.

## Recent Context

Zovegalisib in Phase 3 trial (ReDiscover-2) for HR+/HER2- metastatic
breast cancer. Phase 1/2 breast cancer data presented at ESMO TAT in
March 2026. Exploring PI3Ka-driven vascular malformations as a genetic
disease indication. Company narrowed 2025 net loss to $277M (from
$338M in 2024) through cost reductions. Key 2026 milestones include
triplet combination data and frontline Phase 3 trial design.
