# Matter Intelligence

## Overview
Matter Intelligence builds hyperspectral and thermal imaging
sensors for satellites, drones, and aircraft, delivering
sub-meter resolution Earth observation data with 500x more
information density than existing hyperspectral systems.
Founded early 2023 by Vishnu Sridhar (CEO, former NASA JPL),
Thomas Chrien (former Millennium Space / Air Force), and
Nathan Stein (former Caltech). HQ in California.

## Technical Focus
- Hyperspectral imaging spectroscopy (thousands of spectral
  bands) for material composition detection at molecular
  level
- Thermal imaging sensors for satellite, drone, and
  aircraft platforms
- Sub-meter resolution Earth observation data products
- Applications: national security, agriculture,
  environmental monitoring
- First satellite mission: EARTH-1 (global material
  composition imaging)
