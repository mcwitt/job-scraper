# Atomic Semi

## Overview

Semiconductor fab startup building a small, fast
fabrication facility with custom manufacturing tools.
Founded 2022 by Sam Zeloof (CEO, famous for making chips
in his garage) and Jim Keller (co-founder, 40+ year
semiconductor veteran, ex-AMD/Apple/Tesla/Intel). HQ and
first fab in San Francisco.

## Technical Focus

- Small-scale semiconductor fabrication
- Custom fab equipment and tooling (e-beam writers, 3D
  printers, microscopes)
- Process development for smaller geometries
- Mechanical, electrical, and embedded engineering
- Rust-based fab software infrastructure

## Scale & Stage

Private. Seed round ~$15M at ~$100M valuation (2023).
Investors include OpenAI Startup Fund, Fred Ehrsam
(Paradigm), Nat Friedman (ex-GitHub CEO), Naval Ravikant,
Fontinalis Partners. ~20-50 employees, rapidly growing.

## Hiring

Building a team of exceptional, hands-on engineers across
mechanical, electrical, software, and process disciplines.
Roles span engineering, manufacturing (technicians,
machinists), and operations. All positions in-person in
San Francisco. U.S. export control compliance required.

## Recent Context

Actively scaling with 24+ open positions including summer
internships. Fab facility operational in San Francisco.
Partnered with UC Berkeley BWRC for collaboration.
