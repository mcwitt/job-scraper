# Iambic Therapeutics

## Overview

Clinical-stage biotech using AI to discover and develop
small-molecule drugs, primarily in oncology. Founded in
2019 by Tom Miller (CEO) and Fred Manby (CTO). HQ in
San Diego, CA. Originally called Entos before rebranding.

## Technical Focus

- NeuralPLexer: protein-ligand structure prediction
  (outperforms AlphaFold on binding pose prediction)
- Enchant: multimodal transformer predicting clinical
  outcomes from early-stage discovery data
- AI-driven molecular design and optimization
- High-throughput wet-lab synthesis and testing
- End-to-end platform from hit ID to clinical candidate

## Scale & Stage

Private, clinical stage. Raised over $300M total: Series B
extension ($50M), plus $100M oversubscribed round (Nov
2025) from ARK, Regeneron Ventures, Sequoia, and others.
~100 employees. Cash runway into 2028. Named to CNBC
Disruptor 50 list in 2025.

## Hiring

Cross-functional culture blending ML researchers, comp
chemists, and wet-lab scientists. Values ambitious problem
solving and open collaboration. Roles span ML engineering,
computational chemistry, drug design, and biology. High
bar for technical depth; interest in drug discovery is
a plus. Moved into new San Diego HQ in 2024.

## Recent Context

Expects three AI-discovered drugs in clinical trials by
2026. Signed major deal with Takeda (Feb 2026) worth up
to $1.7B for AI-driven small molecule discovery across
oncology, inflammation, and GI disease. Represents one
of the largest AI drug discovery partnerships to date.
