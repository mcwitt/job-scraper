# Equilibrium Energy

## Overview

Equilibrium Energy is a clean power company that uses advanced analytics
and power market modeling to trade electricity, manage battery storage
assets, and optimize clean energy portfolios. The company buys and
sells power at optimal times and prices while operating battery storage
assets across ERCOT and CAISO.

## Technical Focus

- Power market analytics and price prediction modeling
- Battery storage optimization and dispatch (260 MW / 380 MWh portfolio)
- EQ Mission Control software platform for portfolio management
- Energy trading algorithms and risk management

## Scale & Stage

Private, growth-stage. ~100-130 employees. Headquartered in Boston, MA.
Founded 2021. Raised $28M Series B2 in March 2025 led by GS Energy
with participation from NRG Energy. Total funding of ~$80M+. Operates
battery tolls across ERCOT and CAISO markets.

## Hiring

Equilibrium Energy hires quantitative engineers, data scientists,
software engineers, and energy traders. The company sits at the
intersection of energy markets and technology, so candidates with
backgrounds in quantitative finance, power systems, or ML applied to
trading are strong fits.

Built from scratch over the past few years, the company has a startup
culture with a quantitative, analytical bent. The launch of EQ Mission
Control signals growing investment in software platform engineering
alongside the core trading and asset management business.

## Recent Context

Equilibrium raised $28M Series B2 in March 2025 and launched its
flagship EQ Mission Control software platform. The company expanded
its battery storage portfolio to 260 MW / 380 MWh with new Ormat
project tolls in ERCOT, reflecting growing scale in clean energy asset
management and trading.
