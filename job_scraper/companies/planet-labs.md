# Planet Labs

## Overview

Earth observation company operating the largest
constellation of imaging satellites, capturing 30+ TB of
data daily. Founded in 2010 by NASA scientists Will
Marshall, Robbie Schingler, and Chris Boshuizen.
Headquartered in San Francisco, CA with offices in
Berlin and other international locations.

## Technical Focus

- Earth-imaging satellite design and manufacturing
- High-resolution optical imaging (Pelican, SuperDove)
- Geospatial data pipelines and analytics platforms
- On-board edge AI (NVIDIA Jetson) for real-time insight
- Computer vision and change detection at scale
- Cloud-based imagery APIs and developer tools

## Scale & Stage

Public (NYSE: PL). ~970 employees as of Jan 2025.
Trailing 12-month revenue ~$282M. Growing government
and commercial customer base across defense,
agriculture, insurance, and environmental monitoring.

## Hiring

Roles span satellite hardware, software engineering,
data science, and geospatial analytics. Mission-driven
culture with emphasis on hard problems and learning.
Engineering covers the full stack from spacecraft
hardware to cloud data platforms. Some roles may
require or benefit from security clearance given
growing defense contracts.

## Recent Context

Launched Pelican-5/6 and 36 SuperDoves (Nov 2025).
Selected as prime under MDA's $151B SHIELD IDIQ
(Mar 2026). Awarded €240M German government satellite
services deal (Jul 2025). Signed $230M agreement with
SKY Perfect JSAT for 10 dedicated Pelican satellites.
Opening new satellite manufacturing facility in Berlin.
Partnered with AXA for AI risk-management platform.
Next-gen Owl monitoring fleet planned for late 2026.
