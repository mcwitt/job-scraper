# Genesis Therapeutics

## Overview

AI drug discovery company (rebranded to Genesis Molecular AI in October 2025)
that combines deep learning with molecular simulations to design novel small
molecule therapeutics. Operates the GEMS (Genesis Exploration of Molecular
Space) platform for computational drug design and maintains an internal
therapeutics pipeline.

## Technical Focus

- GEMS platform: deep learning + molecular simulation for drug design
- Pearl foundation model for drug-protein structure prediction
- Synthetic data scaling laws for AI-driven molecular modeling
- Small molecule drug discovery for high-impact targets
- Physics-based molecular simulations

## Scale & Stage

Private. ~155 employees, headquartered in South San Francisco. Raised $304M
total across 5 rounds, including a $224M Series B led by Andreessen Horowitz
in 2023. Backed by NVIDIA (NVentures), a16z, and other leading AI/life
science investors.

## Hiring

Engineering-heavy culture with deep technical bar in ML, computational
chemistry, and molecular simulation. Roles emphasize ability to work at
the intersection of AI systems and drug discovery science. Research
publications and domain expertise valued.

Growing rapidly post-Series B with hiring across ML research, platform
engineering, and drug discovery. Collaborative environment with multiple
pharma partnerships (Incyte, Gilead) providing real-world validation of
the platform.

## Recent Context

Rebranded from Genesis Therapeutics to Genesis Molecular AI in October 2025.
Launched Pearl, a proprietary foundation model that outperforms AlphaFold 3
on drug-protein structure prediction benchmarks using synthetic data scaling.
Signed strategic AI research collaboration with Incyte in January 2025.
