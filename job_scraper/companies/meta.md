# Meta

## Overview

Social media and technology conglomerate operating Facebook, Instagram,
WhatsApp, and Messenger. Increasingly positioned as an AI-first company
with open-source Llama models and massive infrastructure investment.
Also pursues mixed reality hardware through Reality Labs (Quest
headsets, Ray-Ban Meta glasses).

## Technical Focus

- Open-source LLMs: Llama 4/5 family, Meta AI assistant
- AI infrastructure: custom silicon, massive GPU clusters
- Social platforms at billion-user scale (ranking, ads, integrity)
- Reality Labs: Quest headsets, Ray-Ban Meta glasses, Horizon OS
- PyTorch ecosystem and AI research (FAIR)

## Scale & Stage

Public (NASDAQ: META). ~76,000 employees. FY2025 revenue $201B (up 22%
YoY). Spending $115-135B capex in 2026, nearly double 2025, almost
entirely on AI infrastructure (data centers, servers, networking).
30% increase in output per engineer from AI-powered dev tools.

## Hiring

Aggressively hiring AI talent, poaching from Apple, OpenAI, and peers.
Entry-level SWE roles start around $178-258K. High bar on coding and
system design, with structured interview loops. Meta values speed of
execution and impact — engineers are expected to ship quickly and
operate with significant autonomy.

Culture emphasizes "move fast" with a flat engineering hierarchy.
Performance expectations are high and underperformers are managed out.
Strong new-grad pipeline but also heavy experienced hiring, especially
in AI/ML and infrastructure roles.

## Recent Context

Llama 4 released early 2025 with Scout model featuring 10M token
context window. Formed Meta Superintelligence Labs targeting "world
models" and reasoning. Ray-Ban Meta glasses sold 7M units in 2025,
becoming a surprise consumer hit.
