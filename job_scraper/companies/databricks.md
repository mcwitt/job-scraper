# Databricks

## Overview

Unified data analytics and AI platform company. Created Apache
Spark and popularized the data lakehouse architecture (combining
data lakes and data warehouses). Headquartered in San Francisco
with offices worldwide.

## Technical Focus

- Data lakehouse platform (Delta Lake, Unity Catalog)
- Apache Spark and distributed data processing
- MLflow for ML lifecycle management
- Large-scale data engineering and ETL
- AI/ML model training and serving on unified platform
- SQL analytics and BI integration

## Scale & Stage

Private. Valued at $43B+. ~7000+ employees. Strong revenue
growth from enterprise subscriptions. Runs on all major clouds
(AWS, Azure, GCP). One of the most valuable private tech
companies.

## Hiring

Large engineering org hiring across data infrastructure, ML
platform, security, and frontend. Engineering bar is solid —
expect distributed systems knowledge and data engineering
depth. Culture is engineering-driven. Compensation competitive
with Big Tech.

Accessible to strong backend and infrastructure engineers
even without deep data/ML backgrounds.

## Recent Context

Expanding AI capabilities with foundation model training
(DBRX) and MosaicML acquisition. Competing with Snowflake on
data warehousing and with cloud providers on ML platforms.
Building out governance and security features for enterprise
adoption.
