# Dandelion Energy

## Overview

Dandelion Energy makes residential geothermal heating and cooling accessible
and affordable by combining proprietary heat pump hardware with streamlined
installation services. Headquartered in Mount Kisco, NY, the company spun
out of Alphabet's X moonshot lab to bring geothermal HVAC to mainstream
homeowners.

## Technical Focus

- Geothermal heat pump design (Dandelion Geo, highest heating efficiency)
- Ground loop drilling and installation optimization
- HVAC system integration and controls
- Geothermal-as-a-service leasing platform
- New construction geothermal deployment at scale

## Scale & Stage

Private, raised approximately $175M total. 120+ employees. Operates
primarily in the Northeast (NY, CT) with expansion into new construction
markets across 16+ states. Manufacturing partnership with Enertech Global.
Founded 2017, spun out of Alphabet X.

## Hiring

Dandelion hires across mechanical engineering, HVAC design, field
operations, and software. The company needs people who can bridge hardware
product development with field deployment at scale. Experience with HVAC
systems, heat pump technology, or residential construction is valuable.

As Dandelion pivots toward new construction partnerships (e.g., Lennar),
roles in business development, project management, and operations are
growing. The Alphabet X heritage means a culture that values ambitious
technical goals paired with practical execution.

## Recent Context

In 2025, Dandelion launched a first-of-its-kind residential geothermal
leasing program across 16 states and partnered with Lennar to deploy
geothermal in 1,500+ new Colorado homes across 14 communities. The company
surpassed one million linear feet of installed ground loops and won the
2025 Builder Innovator Brilliance Award for HVAC.
