# Baseten

## Overview

Serverless ML inference platform that converts machine learning models into
production-ready APIs. Functions similarly to AWS Lambda for AI workloads,
handling autoscaling, GPU provisioning, and model serving infrastructure.
Customers include Cursor, Notion, Abridge, and Clay.

## Technical Focus

- Serverless model inference with autoscaling GPU infrastructure
- Model APIs (pre-optimized open-source model endpoints)
- Multi-node fine-tuning and training with promotion to inference
- Truss: open-source model packaging framework

## Scale & Stage

Private, Series E. Raised $300M Series E in January 2026 at $5B valuation
(led by IVP and CapitalG, with $150M from Nvidia). Total funding ~$585M.
Became a unicorn in 2025. Experienced 100x inference volume growth in 2025.

## Hiring

and distributed systems engineering. Values experience with ML serving
infrastructure, CUDA optimization, and cloud-native platform development.
The rapid growth (Series C to E in under a year) means aggressive hiring
across engineering.

Product and platform roles also growing as Baseten expands from inference
into training and model lifecycle management. Acquired Parsed in December
2025, indicating interest in data pipeline talent.
