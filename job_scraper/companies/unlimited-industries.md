# Unlimited Industries

## Overview
Unlimited Industries is an AI-native construction company
that designs and builds energy infrastructure, data centers,
critical minerals facilities, and advanced manufacturing
plants. Founded 2025 by Alex Modon, Tara Viswanathan, and
Jordan Stern (Viswanathan and Stern previously founded Rupa
Health, acquired 2024). HQ in San Francisco, CA.

## Technical Focus
- AI-powered platform automating engineering design and
  construction end-to-end
- Reduces pre-construction timelines from months to weeks
- Cost optimization: demonstrated 50%+ capital cost
  reduction on recent projects
- Focus areas: energy infrastructure, data centers,
  critical minerals, advanced manufacturing

## Scale & Stage
- Private; seed stage
- $12M seed (December 2025), led by Andreessen Horowitz
  and CIV
- Small early team
