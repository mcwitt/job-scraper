# ServiceNow

## Overview

Enterprise cloud platform for digital workflows,
IT service management, and business process
automation. Founded in 2004 by Fred Luddy.
Headquartered in Santa Clara, CA.

## Technical Focus

- AI and machine learning for workflow automation
  (Now Assist, agentic AI)
- Large-scale cloud platform engineering
- Natural language processing and generative AI
- Enterprise integration and API platforms
- Low-code/no-code application development
- Security operations and IT operations management

## Scale & Stage

Public (NOW). ~$10B annual revenue. ~28,000 employees.
One of the largest enterprise SaaS companies globally.
Market cap ~$200B+.

## Hiring

Engineering roles span platform, AI/ML, product, and
infrastructure. Heavy investment in AI — agentic AI,
LLMs, and intelligent automation. Sales and consulting
orgs are also large. Distributed workforce with
offices in Santa Clara, San Diego, Seattle, and
globally.

## Recent Context

Rapidly expanding AI capabilities with Now Assist
(generative AI across the platform) and agentic AI
workflows. Positioning as the "AI platform for
business transformation." Strong enterprise customer
base across Fortune 500. Pursuing AI-driven
productivity gains across all product lines.
