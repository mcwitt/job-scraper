# Woven by Toyota

## Overview

Toyota Motor Corporation's software and mobility technology
subsidiary, building the vehicle operating system and
automated driving platform for Toyota's global fleet.
Originally formed as Woven Planet Holdings in 2021,
became a wholly owned Toyota subsidiary in 2023.
Headquartered in Nihonbashi, Tokyo, Japan.

## Technical Focus

- Arene: vehicle operating system and software platform
- Automated driving and advanced driver assistance (ADAS)
- Woven City: real-world testbed for mobility, robotics,
  and smart infrastructure (Susono, Shizuoka, Japan)
- Vehicle safety and connected vehicle technologies

## Scale & Stage

Wholly owned subsidiary of Toyota Motor Corporation
(public, TYO: 7203). Approximately 2,000+ employees
across offices in Tokyo, Ann Arbor, London, and
other locations worldwide.

## Hiring

Software-focused engineering culture within a large
automotive parent. Roles span vehicle OS development,
autonomous driving, robotics, and infrastructure
software. International team with a mix of automotive
and tech-industry talent. High bar for systems and
embedded software engineering.

## Recent Context

Deployed Arene software platform in Toyota's latest-
generation RAV4 (fiscal year 2025 launch). Woven City
officially opened in September 2025, welcoming its
first ~360 residents as a living laboratory for
mobility and robotics technologies. Next major Arene
deployment targets Toyota's next-gen BEV lineup in 2026.
