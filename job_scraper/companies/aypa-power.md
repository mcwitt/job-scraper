# Aypa Power

## Overview

Aypa Power develops, owns, and operates utility-scale
energy storage and hybrid renewable energy projects
across North America. Headquartered in Austin, TX, the
company was founded in 2017 and is a portfolio company
of Blackstone. Aypa has 30 projects in operation or
under construction with a development pipeline exceeding
22 GW.

## Technical Focus

- Utility-scale battery energy storage systems (BESS)
- Hybrid solar-plus-storage project development
- Grid reliability and renewable integration
- Large-scale project financing and construction
- Energy market optimization and dispatch

## Scale & Stage

Private, wholly owned by Blackstone. Approximately 190
employees. Secured $1.5B warehouse revolving credit
facility (Feb 2026) — the largest warehouse financing
for a storage-focused IPP. Also closed $535M for a
California solar-storage project (May 2025), $398M for
Arizona BESS (2024), and CA$700M for Ontario BESS
projects (Mar 2026). Founded 2017.

## Hiring

Aypa hires across energy storage development, project
finance, engineering, construction management, and grid
operations. The company values experience in power
markets, utility-scale renewables, and large
infrastructure project delivery. As a Blackstone
portfolio company with aggressive growth targets, roles
carry significant deal flow and project responsibility.

## Recent Context

Aypa closed a landmark $1.5B construction warehouse
facility in February 2026, enabling projects through
2028. In March 2026, announced CA$700M financing with
Six Nations of the Grand River for Ontario BESS projects.
The company continues to scale rapidly across U.S. and
Canadian markets with a 22 GW+ pipeline.
