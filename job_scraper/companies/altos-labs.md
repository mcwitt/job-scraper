# Altos Labs

## Overview

Biotechnology company focused on cellular rejuvenation
programming to restore cell health and reverse disease.
Founded in 2021, headquartered in Redwood City, CA.
Backed by Jeff Bezos and Yuri Milner. Operates research
institutes in the Bay Area, San Diego, and Cambridge, UK.

## Technical Focus

- Cellular reprogramming and rejuvenation biology
- Partial epigenetic reprogramming in vivo
- Aging reversal and longevity research
- Translational medicine and clinical development
- Multi-omics and computational biology

## Scale & Stage

Private. Launched with ~$3B in funding, one of the
largest biotech launches ever. Approximately 300-500
employees across three global research institutes.

## Hiring

Research-heavy organization with world-class scientists
(multiple Nobel laureates as advisors). Roles span
computational biology, molecular biology, translational
research, and clinical development. Academic-caliber
science with industry resources. Collaborative culture
across Bay Area, San Diego, and UK sites.

## Recent Context

Appointed Dr Joan Mannick as CMO, signaling a shift
toward clinical programs. Published mouse lifespan
extension results via targeted partial reprogramming.
Began early human safety testing (Aug 2025). Testing
reprogramming therapies in ex vivo organ models.
Actively expanding headcount into 2026.
