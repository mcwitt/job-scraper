# BlackRock

## Overview

World's largest asset manager, with ~$11.5T in assets
under management. Founded in 1988 by Larry Fink and
seven partners. Headquartered in New York City with
70+ offices in 30+ countries. Publicly traded (NYSE: BLK).

## Technical Focus

- Aladdin — end-to-end investment management platform
  (risk analytics, portfolio management, trading, ops)
- AI/ML for investment signals, risk modeling, and
  portfolio construction
- Large-scale data engineering and real-time analytics
- Cloud migration and distributed systems (hybrid cloud)
- API platform engineering and microservices
- eFront — alternative investments technology platform

## Scale & Stage

Public (NYSE: BLK). ~22,000 employees globally.
Revenue driven by management fees, technology services
(Aladdin licensing), and advisory. One of the Big Four
index fund managers alongside Vanguard, Fidelity, and
State Street. iShares is the world's largest ETF provider.

## Hiring

Large engineering org centered on Aladdin platform
development. Roles span platform engineering, data
engineering, ML/AI, and fintech product development.
Hiring bar is solid — expect distributed systems and
financial domain knowledge. 4 days/week in-office hybrid
model. Compensation competitive with large tech firms.
Title structure uses Associate / VP / Director levels.

## Recent Context

Expanding Aladdin's AI capabilities with generative AI
features for portfolio analytics and client reporting.
Growing technology services revenue as external Aladdin
clients increase. Acquired eFront (alternatives) and
Global Infrastructure Partners. Continued push into
private markets and digital assets / tokenization.
