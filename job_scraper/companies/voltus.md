# Voltus

## Overview

Voltus is the leading distributed energy resource (DER) technology platform
and virtual power plant operator. The company connects commercial, industrial,
residential, and transportation energy users to electricity markets, enabling
them to earn revenue by reducing or shifting electricity use during grid
stress, high prices, or high emissions periods.

## Technical Focus

- Virtual power plant (VPP) platform and AI-powered optimization
- Demand response aggregation across wholesale electricity markets
- DER integration (flexible load, distributed generation, storage, EVs)
- Real-time grid dispatch and carbon reporting
- Thermal energy storage monetization

## Scale & Stage

Private. Raised ~$121M through Series C. ~275 employees. Manages 8.1 GW
of flexible capacity across the U.S. and Canada, delivering $240M to
customers in 2025.

## Hiring

Voltus hires across software engineering, energy market operations, and
business development. Engineering roles focus on platform scalability,
real-time data pipelines, and market integration. The team combines deep
energy industry expertise with modern software practices.

Given rapid growth in capacity and market expansion, hiring tends toward
candidates who can operate at the intersection of energy markets and
technology. Experience with electricity market structures (ISO/RTO),
real-time systems, or energy analytics is valued.

## Recent Context

Voltus delivered record results in 2025, dispatched every day of the year
across U.S. and Canadian markets. In early 2026, the company partnered with
Octopus Energy to add residential flexibility across major power markets and
with CenTrio to monetize district energy infrastructure as grid resources.
