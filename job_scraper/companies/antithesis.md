# Antithesis

## Overview

Autonomous software testing platform that finds and
reproduces bugs through deterministic simulation.
Founded by Will Wilson and the team behind
FoundationDB (acquired by Apple, 2015). HQ in
Vienna, VA; offices in SF, NYC, London.

## Technical Focus

- Deterministic hypervisor that replays entire system
  executions bit-for-bit
- Property-based testing at the full-system level
- Automatic bug reproduction with minimal test cases
- Targets distributed systems, databases, blockchains,
  and financial infrastructure
