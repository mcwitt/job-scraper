# Weights & Biases

## Overview

AI developer platform for experiment tracking, model training
management, and evaluation. System of record for ML workflows at
1,400+ organizations including Nvidia, OpenAI, and AstraZeneca.
Founded in 2017 by Lukas Biewald, Chris Van Pelt, and Shawn Lewis.
Acquired by CoreWeave in May 2025 for $1.7B.

## Technical Focus

- Experiment tracking and model versioning
- Training run visualization and debugging
- Model evaluation and monitoring in production
- Dataset management and artifact tracking
- AI application evaluation tooling

## Scale & Stage

Acquired by CoreWeave (May 2025) for $1.7B, a premium over its
prior $1.25B valuation. Previously raised $250M over 5 rounds.
Last independent round was $50M in August 2023 led by Daniel Gross
and Nat Friedman. Had filed for IPO before the acquisition.

## Hiring

Now a CoreWeave subsidiary. Historically hired ML infrastructure
engineers, full-stack developers, and developer experience roles.
Core product team focused on building tools that ML practitioners
actually want to use -- strong developer empathy valued.

Post-acquisition, expanding product scope with CoreWeave's cloud
infrastructure. Launched new AI cloud software products in June
2025 combining W&B's developer tools with CoreWeave's GPU cloud.

## Recent Context

CoreWeave completed acquisition in May 2025, integrating W&B's
developer platform with CoreWeave's AI cloud infrastructure.
Announced new products in June 2025 to help customers develop,
deploy, and iterate on AI models and agents faster. Operates as
a product line within CoreWeave rather than a standalone company.
