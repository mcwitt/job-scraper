# Sealed

## Overview

Sealed designs, manages, and finances home weatherization
and electrification projects, partnering with utilities and
certified contractors to deliver upgrades like heat pumps,
insulation, air sealing, and smart home technology. Founded
in 2012 by Lauren Salz (CEO) and Andy Frank, Sealed is
headquartered in New York, NY.

## Technical Focus

- Home energy assessment and project scoping platform
- Contractor management and workflow orchestration
- Utility rebate processing and compliance automation
- Financing and pay-for-performance energy models
- Smart home technology integration
- Data-driven energy savings measurement

## Scale & Stage

Private, raised ~$110M total over 7 rounds including a
$45.5M extended Series B (2022, led by Fifth Wall Climate
Tech). Most recent funding in Jan 2024. Operates in CA, CT,
IL, MD, NJ, NY, PA, and WI. Team size estimated at 50-100
employees.

## Hiring

Sealed hires software engineers, product managers, energy
consultants, and operations roles. The platform side needs
engineers comfortable with contractor-facing tools, rebate
workflows, and energy modeling. As a climate tech company
in the residential electrification space, domain interest
in decarbonization and home energy is a plus.

## Recent Context

Sealed raised $30M in 2024 to streamline the rebate process
for contractors performing electrification and weatherization
work. The company is well-positioned to benefit from IRA
incentives driving residential energy upgrades, though the
political landscape around clean energy tax credits remains
in flux heading into 2026.
