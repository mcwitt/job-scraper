# Character AI

## Overview

Consumer AI company building interactive, conversational AI characters
for entertainment, creativity, and social connection. Platform enables
users to create and chat with millions of AI-powered characters.
Founded by former Google Brain researchers Noam Shazeer and Daniel De
Freitas.

## Technical Focus

- Large language model training and fine-tuning for persona consistency
- Natural language processing and generative AI for interactive dialogue
- Safety, alignment, and content moderation at consumer scale
- Backend infrastructure for real-time conversational AI serving
- Evaluation methodologies for LLM safety and alignment

## Scale & Stage

Private, Series A ($150M raised in 2023). Valuation declined from $2.5B
peak to ~$1B in 2025 amid high operating costs. ~240 employees. Revenue
~$32M in 2025 (doubled from prior year), primarily from subscriptions.
Co-founders returned to Google in late 2024; new CEO Karandeep Anand
(ex-Meta, ex-Brex) hired June 2025.

## Hiring

Smaller engineering team relative to user base (~20M MAUs), meaning
individual contributors have outsized impact. Hiring focuses on ML
research, backend engineering, trust & safety, and product. Expects
strong fundamentals in ML and distributed systems.

Post-founder departure, the company is rebuilding its technical
leadership. Candidates comfortable operating in a turnaround
environment with evolving strategic direction will be best positioned.

## Recent Context

After co-founders returned to Google, Character AI brought in new
leadership and began exploring strategic options including potential
sale or additional fundraising. Implemented new safety measures in late
2025, including restrictions on open-ended chat for users under 18.
