# Symbolica AI

## Overview

AI research lab applying category theory and type theory
to build structured symbolic reasoning models. Founded
2022 by George Morgan (ex-Tesla Autopilot) and Taylor
Wrobel. Headquartered in San Francisco.

## Technical Focus

- Category-theoretic neural architectures
- Symbolic reasoning embedded in model architecture
- Program synthesis, execution, and search
- Alternatives to transformer/LLM paradigm
- Mathematical foundations (category theory, type theory)

## Scale & Stage

Private. Raised ~$33M total (seed + Series A) led by
Khosla Ventures, with General Catalyst, Day One Ventures,
and Abstract Ventures. Team of ~20-30 ML engineers,
researchers, and mathematicians. Stephen Wolfram advises.

## Hiring

Hires ML engineers, ML researchers, and mathematicians
with strong formal foundations. Team drawn from Tesla,
Neuralink, and ClearML. Deep math background (category
theory, type theory, abstract algebra) is a differentiator.
Small team culture with research-lab intensity.

## Recent Context

Launched in April 2024 with $33M in funding and a bold
thesis: structured symbolic models can surpass deep
learning on reasoning tasks. First product, a coding
assistant, was slated for early 2025. Competing against
well-funded LLM incumbents with a fundamentally different
architectural approach.
