# Peak Energy

## Overview

First American company advancing sodium-ion battery systems
for grid-scale renewable energy storage. Founded 2023 by
Landon Mossburg (ex-Tesla Energy, Northvolt) and Cameron
Dales (ex-Enovix, Lockheed Martin). HQ in Burlingame, CA
with offices in Denver, CO.

## Technical Focus

- Sodium-ion (NFPP) battery cell chemistry
- Passive cooling design (no fans, pumps, or vents)
- Grid-scale energy storage systems (ESS)
- Battery management and embedded software
- Power electronics and thermal engineering
- Domestic U.S. cell manufacturing

## Scale & Stage

Private, Series A ($65M total -- $10M seed in 2023, $55M
Series A in 2024). Led by Xora Innovation (Temasek),
Eclipse, TDK Ventures. ~40-100 employees. Targeting 20 GWh
annual capacity and 1,200+ U.S. jobs by 2030.

## Hiring

Hardware-heavy engineering culture. Roles span electrical,
mechanical, embedded software, test, supply chain,
manufacturing, and sales. Team drawn from Tesla, Northvolt,
Apple, Enovix, Zipline, Solid Power, Fluence, Powin.

## Recent Context

Delivered first grid-scale sodium-ion battery in U.S.
(3.5 MWh, July 2025). Signed 4.75 GWh contract with
Jupiter Power (potentially $500M+, 2027-2030). Deal with
RWE Americas for MISO region deployment (March 2026).
Claims 25%+ cost reduction vs lithium-ion.
