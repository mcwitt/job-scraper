# LanceDB

## Overview

Open-source vector database for AI, built on the Lance
columnar data format. Founded in 2022 by Chang She
(ex-pandas core developer, former VP Eng at Tubi) and
Lei Xu (former ML infra lead at Cruise, HDFS core
contributor). YC W22 batch. Headquartered in San
Francisco.

## Technical Focus

- Lance columnar format (Rust-based, optimized for
  multimodal AI data)
- Serverless/embedded vector search for RAG and agents
- Hybrid search (vector + full-text + metadata)
- Multimodal data management (text, images, audio, video)
- Multimodal Lakehouse platform (search, EDA, feature
  engineering, training)
- Integrations with LangChain, LlamaIndex, and other
  AI frameworks

## Scale & Stage

Private. Series A ($30M, June 2025) led by Theory
Ventures with CRV, YC, Databricks Ventures. Total
raised ~$38.5M. Approximately 30-35 employees. Core
team includes alumni of Cloudera, Cruise, and Tubi.

## Hiring

Small engineering-heavy team. Expect strong systems
programming skills (Rust, Python). Deep knowledge of
data formats, storage engines, or vector search likely
valued. Open-source-first culture with community-driven
development. Competitive for a seed/Series A startup.

## Recent Context

Raised $30M Series A (June 2025) to build out the
Multimodal Lakehouse product suite. Released Lance SDK
v1.0.0 (December 2025) with community-driven release
strategy. Launched Lance community governance with
dedicated Discord and GitHub organization. Competing
in the vector database space alongside Pinecone,
Weaviate, Qdrant, and Chroma.
