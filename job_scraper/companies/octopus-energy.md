# Octopus Energy

## Overview

Octopus Energy is a UK-based energy technology company and
the UK's largest domestic electricity and gas supplier,
serving 7.7 million households (~25% market share). Founded
in 2015 by Greg Jackson (CEO), headquartered in London.
Operates in the UK, US, Germany, France, Italy, Spain,
Australia, Japan, and New Zealand.

## Technical Focus

- Kraken: proprietary energy platform managing billing,
  grid analytics, and real-time energy balancing
- Machine learning for battery trading and smart energy
  allocation
- Smart grid and demand response technology
- Heat pump and EV charging deployment at scale
- Full-stack software (Python, React) for energy retail
- Renewable energy generation and virtual power plants

## Scale & Stage

Private, valued at ~$12B as of 2026. Raised $1.96B over
5 rounds (latest: Series F, Dec 2025). Approximately 4,700
employees. Revenue of GBP 15.4B in 2025. Kraken platform
independently valued at $8.65B (Jan 2026) and licensed to
serve 70M+ accounts globally across other utilities.

## Hiring

Octopus hires software engineers, data scientists, product
managers, AI/ML engineers, and low-carbon field technicians.
All employees receive company shares. Strong customer-first
culture: customer ops sits next to the CEO, and leadership
regularly handles customer queries directly. Senior tech
roles (AI, renewables finance) typically GBP 85K+. The
engineering bar is high, with emphasis on Python, React,
and energy domain knowledge.

## Recent Context

In January 2026, Kraken Technologies was de-merged and
independently valued at $8.65B. Octopus finished 2025 with
record GBP 15.4B revenue and continued aggressive global
expansion, particularly in the US market. The company
continues scaling heat pump installations aligned with UK
government decarbonization targets and expanding its EV
leasing and charging infrastructure.
