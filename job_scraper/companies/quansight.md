# Quansight

## Overview

Open source consulting and development company that bridges the PyData
ecosystem with enterprise needs. Quansight Labs contributes to 40+
open source projects including NumPy, SciPy, pandas, Jupyter, Dask,
and conda-forge. Founded in 2017 by Travis Oliphant, grew out of
Anaconda. Also operates Quansight Initiate, an early-stage VC fund
investing in open-source-reliant startups.

## Technical Focus

- Core maintainership of NumPy, SciPy, pandas, and other PyData tools
- Data science and ML consulting for enterprises
- Open source UX, documentation, and accessibility improvements
- AI and voice/language technologies (acquired Cobalt Speech in 2025)
- Community-driven open source development and internships

## Scale & Stage

Private, bootstrapped/minimally funded (~$1.6M raised). ~51 employees.
Based in Austin, Texas. Revenue from consulting contracts and grants
(CZI, NASA). Sold AI consulting division to OpenTeams in May 2025.

## Hiring

Team of open source maintainers, core contributors, and long-time
practitioners in the PyData ecosystem. Hiring developers, community
leads, designers, and technical writers. Expect deep familiarity with
scientific Python and a track record of upstream contributions. Labs
team splits time between community-driven OSS work and client
consulting.

## Recent Context

Sold AI consulting division to OpenTeams in May 2025. Acquired Cobalt
Speech, expanding into voice and language AI. Labs contributed over
44,000 hours to open source projects. Played a major role in NumPy 2.0
release (June 2024). Continues to receive grants from CZI and NASA
for scientific open source maintenance.
