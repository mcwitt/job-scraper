# CircuitHub

## Overview

On-demand electronics manufacturing platform powered by
factory-scale robotics. Delivers 10x improvement in speed
and cost of small-batch electronics production. Founded
2012. HQ in South Deerfield, MA.

## Technical Focus

- Factory automation robotics (The Grid platform)
- Haskell and Elm for core platform software
- Computer vision for manufacturing QA
- Nix-based deployment (AWS and bare metal)
- PostgreSQL backend, full-stack robotics integration

## Scale & Stage

Private. ~$20M total funding from Y Combinator and
Google Ventures (GV). Already profitable with tens of
millions in revenue. ~58 employees.

## Hiring

Roles in Haskell engineering, full-stack robotics,
computer vision, mechanical engineering, and production
operations. South Deerfield, MA (onsite) plus remote UK
roles. Unusual tech stack (Haskell/Elm/Nix) means they
value functional programming expertise.

## Recent Context

Customers include Tesla, NASA, and Zipline. Profitable
and growing without additional VC rounds. The Haskell
stack is a differentiator — one of the largest production
Haskell codebases in industry.
