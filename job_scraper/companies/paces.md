# Paces

## Overview

Paces builds agentic AI software for clean energy
infrastructure development, helping developers site,
permit, and interconnect power projects faster. Founded
in 2022 by James McWalter (CEO) and Charles Bai,
headquartered in New York, NY. Y Combinator alum.

## Technical Focus

- Agentic AI for energy project due diligence
- Permitting prediction and zoning data analysis
- Grid interconnection analysis and optimization
- Environmental and geospatial data platforms
- Project siting for renewables, EV charging, carbon
  sequestration, and data centers
- Automation of early-stage project development

## Scale & Stage

Private, early-stage (Series A). Raised $13.2M total:
$1.9M pre-seed (Resolute Ventures) and $11M Series A
(July 2024, led by Navitas Capital with Suffolk
Technologies, MCJ Collective, Y Combinator). Approximately
40 employees.

## Hiring

Paces hires primarily in engineering and operations. As a
small, fast-moving startup, roles favor generalists who
can work across the stack. Co-founders bring strong AI
backgrounds: James was employee #1 at a Google-acquired
AI startup, Charles led AI efficiency and sustainability
at Meta. Expect a high bar for software engineering with
energy domain interest.

## Recent Context

Paces closed its $11M Series A in mid-2024 and has been
scaling its platform to support AI infrastructure siting
alongside traditional renewables. The company positions
its agentic AI tools as enabling "one-person, billion-
dollar power development companies" by automating the
most time-intensive parts of project development. As of
early 2026, Paces continues to expand its customer base
across renewable energy, EV, and data center developers.
