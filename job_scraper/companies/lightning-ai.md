# Lightning AI

## Overview

AI development platform company, creators of PyTorch Lightning
(now Lightning). Founded by William Falcon. Building tools for
ML model training, deployment, and collaboration. Based in
New York.

## Technical Focus

- PyTorch Lightning framework (training abstraction)
- Lightning Studios (cloud dev environments for AI)
- Model training orchestration and scaling
- ML deployment and serving tools
- Developer experience for ML workflows

## Scale & Stage

Private. Venture-backed. ~100 employees. Revenue from cloud
platform (Lightning Studios) and enterprise offerings.
Open-source framework has broad adoption in ML community.

## Hiring

Small team hiring across platform engineering, ML
infrastructure, and developer tools. Experience with PyTorch,
distributed training, or ML tooling is relevant. Open-source
contributions are valued. Startup environment with broad
responsibilities.

## Recent Context

Transitioning from open-source framework company to cloud
platform business with Lightning Studios. Competing in the ML
developer tools space alongside Weights & Biases, Hugging Face,
and cloud provider ML platforms.
