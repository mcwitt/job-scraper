# Deepgram

## Overview

Voice AI platform providing real-time APIs for
speech-to-text (STT), text-to-speech (TTS), and
autonomous voice agents. Founded in 2015 by Scott
Stephenson, Adam Sypniewski, and Noah Shutty, all
former particle physicists from the University of
Michigan. Headquartered in San Francisco. YC W16 alum.

## Technical Focus

- End-to-end deep learning speech recognition models
  (Nova-3, Flux conversational speech recognition)
- Real-time speech-to-text and text-to-speech APIs
- Voice Agent API (unified speech-to-speech with LLM
  orchestration)
- Multilingual support (36+ languages)
- On-prem and cloud deployment options

## Scale & Stage

Private, unicorn. Raised $130M Series C (Jan 2026) at
$1.3B valuation, led by AVP ($215M+ total). Investors
include Madrona, Tiger, Wing, In-Q-Tel, Alkeon, YC,
Twilio, and SAP. Cash-flow positive as of 2025.
~200-260 employees. 200K+ developers, 1,300+
organizations on the platform.

## Hiring

Remote-first with a San Francisco hub. Engineering
culture emphasizes ownership and cross-team
collaboration. Strong ML research focus -- high bar for
speech/audio ML, systems engineering, and infrastructure
roles. Actively expanding headcount post-Series C.

## Recent Context

Launched Flux, the first conversational speech
recognition model purpose-built for voice agents (Oct
2025). Expanded Nova-3 with 10 new languages and
multilingual keyterm prompting. Acquired OfOne, an AI
voice platform for restaurant drive-throughs. Signed
strategic collaboration with AWS. Key customers include
Twilio, Cloudflare, Sierra, and Vapi.
