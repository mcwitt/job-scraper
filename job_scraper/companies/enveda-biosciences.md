# Enveda Biosciences

## Overview

Enveda Biosciences is a drug discovery company that
decodes the chemistry of medicinal plants using genomics
and AI to find novel drug candidates. Founded in 2019 by
Viswa Colluru (CEO), headquartered in Boulder, CO with
operations in Hyderabad, India. Enveda maps plant
biosynthetic gene clusters to discover natural product
small molecules, then optimizes them for pharmaceutical
development.

## Technical Focus

- Natural products chemistry and plant genomics
- AI/ML for molecule discovery and optimization
- Medicinal chemistry and analog synthesis
- Analytical chemistry (LC-MS, NMR)
- In vivo pharmacology and preclinical biology
- Software engineering for cheminformatics pipelines

## Scale & Stage

Private, Series B. Raised ~$85M including a $51M Series B
in 2023 led by Dimension Capital and GV (Google Ventures).
Team of roughly 100–150 employees split between Boulder,
CO and Hyderabad, India.

## Hiring

Enveda hires across natural products and medicinal
chemistry, analytical chemistry, in vivo pharmacology,
biology, and software engineering. Many scientific roles
are based in Hyderabad; US roles tend to be more
computational or leadership-focused. Strong chemistry or
biology background is required for most roles.

## Recent Context

Enveda has advanced multiple natural product-derived
candidates toward clinical development, with a focus on
fibrosis and inflammation. The company continues to
expand its biosynthetic gene cluster database and AI
platform for discovering novel scaffolds from plant
kingdoms previously untapped by pharma.
