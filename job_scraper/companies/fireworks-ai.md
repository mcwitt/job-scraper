# Fireworks AI

## Overview

AI inference platform focused on fast, cost-efficient serving of
generative models. Founded by ex-Meta AI infrastructure engineers.
Based in the Bay Area.

## Technical Focus

- Optimized model inference and serving
- Custom inference engine (FireAttention)
- Model fine-tuning platform
- Multi-model serving and routing
- GPU utilization optimization

## Scale & Stage

Private. Series B funding. ~100 employees. Revenue from
inference API and enterprise deployments. Competing with
Together AI, Anyscale, and cloud provider inference offerings.

## Hiring

Small engineering-focused team. Looking for systems engineers
with experience in ML inference, GPU programming (CUDA),
compiler optimization, or high-performance computing. Deep
infra experience valued over ML research background.

## Recent Context

Growing as an inference provider for companies that want fast,
cheap model serving without managing their own GPU
infrastructure. Expanding model support and fine-tuning
capabilities. Positioned in the competitive model-serving
middleware layer.
