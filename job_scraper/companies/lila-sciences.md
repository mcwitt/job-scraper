# Lila Sciences

## Overview

AI-driven scientific discovery company using artificial
intelligence to accelerate research and development across
multiple scientific domains. Early-stage, relatively stealth.
Based in San Francisco.

## Technical Focus

- AI for scientific discovery and hypothesis generation
- Multi-domain R&D acceleration
- Large-scale data analysis for scientific research
- ML applied to experimental design
- Automated scientific workflows

## Scale & Stage

Early-stage startup. Venture-backed. Small team. Limited public
information — operating in relative stealth. Pre-revenue,
focused on building platform and initial scientific programs.

## Hiring

Small team with high bar. Looking for ML engineers and
scientists comfortable with ambiguity and cross-disciplinary
work. Ability to apply AI methods to novel scientific problems
is key. Early-stage startup dynamics — broad roles, fast
iteration.

## Recent Context

Part of the growing wave of AI-for-science startups applying
foundation model capabilities to scientific research. Specific
programs and progress largely undisclosed.
