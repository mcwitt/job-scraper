# Mainspring Energy

## Overview

Mainspring Energy manufactures linear generators that convert fuel directly
into electricity through a low-temperature, flameless thermochemical
reaction. Headquartered in Menlo Park, CA, the modular generators provide
clean, reliable distributed power and can run on natural gas, biogas,
hydrogen, or ammonia.

## Technical Focus

- Linear generator design (flameless, low-temperature fuel-to-electricity)
- Multi-fuel flexibility (natural gas, biogas, hydrogen, ammonia)
- Power electronics and grid interconnection
- Distributed energy system controls and monitoring
- Manufacturing scale-up for modular power units

## Scale & Stage

Private, raised approximately $840M total including a $258M Series F
(April 2025) led by General Catalyst with Amazon Climate Pledge Fund and
DCVC. Around 500 employees. Actively deploying units across commercial
and industrial sites. Qualifies for 30% Federal ITC. Founded 2010.

## Hiring

Mainspring hires across mechanical engineering, power electronics,
manufacturing, controls software, and field operations. The linear
generator is a novel power conversion technology, so candidates with
backgrounds in thermodynamics, combustion systems, or power generation
are well-positioned. Software roles span embedded controls, cloud
monitoring, and fleet management.

The company has matured significantly with ~500 employees and commercial
deployments, but retains a hardware-startup intensity given the novel
technology. Board additions of the former Cummins CEO and former Ixia CEO
signal a push toward industrial-scale operations and channel distribution.

## Recent Context

In April 2025, Mainspring raised $258M in Series F financing and added
energy industry leaders to its board. National Grid Ventures announced the
world's first 100% hydrogen-fueled commercial linear generator installation
at Northport Power Plant, expected operational by September 2026. CalBio
expanded Mainspring deployments to 5.3 MW across five California dairy biogas
sites.
