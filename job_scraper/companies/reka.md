# Reka

## Overview

AI research company specializing in multimodal AI models that can
interpret, search, and reason across video, image, and text content.
Also builds agentic AI for complex research and information synthesis.
Small team with roots in top AI labs.

## Technical Focus

- Multimodal foundation models (vision, video, text)
- Reka Vision: video and image understanding at scale
- Reka Research: agentic AI for web browsing and document analysis
- Cross-modal reasoning and retrieval
- Enterprise multimodal AI deployment

## Scale & Stage

Private, raised $110M in July 2025 at $1B valuation (unicorn status).
Total funding ~$168M. Investors include NVIDIA and Snowflake. ~50
employees, up from ~20 the prior year. Revenue ~$11M in 2025.

## Hiring

Very small team, so each hire has significant impact. Likely prioritizes
candidates with strong research backgrounds in multimodal ML, computer
vision, and video understanding. Enterprise customers like Shutterstock
and Turing Video suggest need for production-minded engineers.

Given the small headcount and unicorn valuation, expect a high bar and
selective hiring process. Candidates with experience at major AI labs or
with published multimodal research will have an advantage.

## Recent Context

Reached unicorn status in July 2025 with $110M raise backed by NVIDIA
and Snowflake. Growing rapidly from ~20 to ~50 employees. Focused on
