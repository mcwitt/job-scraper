# BioRender

## Overview

BioRender is a scientific illustration and communication
platform used by researchers to create professional
figures, graphical abstracts, and presentations. Founded
in 2018 by Shiz Aoki, Ryan Marien, and Jordan Doerksen,
headquartered in Toronto, ON, Canada. BioRender has
become the de facto standard tool for life science
visualization, used by researchers at thousands of
institutions worldwide.

## Technical Focus

- Web-based scientific illustration editor (SVG/canvas)
- Curated library of 50,000+ life science icons and
  templates
- AI-assisted figure generation and layout tools
- Enterprise collaboration and publishing workflows
- Data visualization integrations for scientific figures
- API and integrations with lab data platforms

## Scale & Stage

Private, growth stage. Raised ~$55M including a Series B
in 2022. Over 2 million users across 200+ countries.
Team of approximately 200–300 employees. Revenue driven
by individual and institutional subscriptions.

## Hiring

BioRender hires across sales (account executives, BDRs),
product management, engineering (full-stack, ML/AI),
marketing (data analytics, product marketing, growth),
and enterprise customer success. The company is scaling
its go-to-market and product teams as it expands from
individual researchers to enterprise and publisher
customers.

## Recent Context

BioRender is investing heavily in AI-powered figure
generation and enterprise features for publishers and
pharma companies. The company has seen rapid adoption
among academic and biopharma researchers and is expanding
its platform from static figures to interactive and
dynamic scientific content.
