# Atomic AI

## Overview

Biotech startup applying deep learning to RNA biology. Focused
on RNA structure prediction and RNA-targeted drug discovery.
Based in San Francisco.

## Technical Focus

- RNA 3D structure prediction using deep learning
- RNA-targeted small molecule drug discovery
- Molecular modeling and computational chemistry
- Biological sequence modeling
- Wet lab validation integrated with ML predictions

## Scale & Stage

Early-stage startup. Small team (<50 employees). Venture-backed.
Pre-revenue, focused on building platform and pipeline.

## Hiring

Small team means high individual impact. Looking for ML
engineers with interest in structural biology, or computational
biologists with strong software skills. Domain experience in
RNA or molecular modeling is a differentiator but not always
required. Expect startup pace and ambiguity.

## Recent Context

Developing AI platform for RNA structure and drug design,
riding the wave of AlphaFold-style breakthroughs applied to
RNA (a less-studied but therapeutically important target class).
