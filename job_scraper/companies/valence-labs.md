# Valence Labs

## Overview

ML research lab focused on advancing deep learning for
drug discovery. Originally founded in 2017 as Valence
Discovery by Prudencio Tossou, Sebastien Giguere, Therence
Bois, and Daniel Cohen in Montreal. Acquired by Recursion
Pharmaceuticals (NASDAQ: RXRX) in May 2023 for $47.5M
and relaunched as Valence Labs. Operates as a semi-
autonomous research arm of Recursion.

## Technical Focus

- Foundation models for molecular property prediction
- Generative models for molecular design
- Quantum mechanics datasets (1.5B+ geometries curated)
- Virtual cell modeling for drug discovery
- Open-source ML tools and benchmark datasets

## Scale & Stage

Subsidiary of Recursion (public, NASDAQ: RXRX). ~40
employees across Montreal and London offices. Funded
through Recursion's balance sheet. Deep ties to Mila
(world's largest deep learning research institute) and
academic advisor network including Yoshua Bengio.

## Hiring

Research-heavy culture with strong academic ties. Roles
include ML research scientists, ML engineers, and research
interns (Valence Labs Scholar Program with $1M in academic
scholarships). Publishes at top ML venues (NeurIPS, ICML).
Expects strong ML fundamentals; domain knowledge in
chemistry or biology is a plus. Montreal office co-located
at Mila. Recently expanded London office with 10+ new
positions.

## Recent Context

Published research on virtual cell modeling at Recursion
(2025). Curated 40+ quantum mechanics datasets. Continues
open-science mission with public code and data releases.
Operates with significant research autonomy despite being
part of Recursion, maintaining its own brand and publication
pipeline. Recursion parent underwent Exscientia merger and
CEO transition (Najat Khan) in early 2026.
