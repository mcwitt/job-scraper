# SSI (Safe Superintelligence Inc)

## Overview

AI research company focused exclusively on building safe
superintelligence. Founded by Ilya Sutskever (former OpenAI chief
scientist), Daniel Gross (former Apple AI head), and Daniel Levy. The
company has a singular mission: develop superintelligent AI safely,
without the distraction of shipping commercial products.

## Technical Focus

- Superintelligence research and development
- AI safety and alignment (core to the mission, not an afterthought)
- Large-scale model training
- Fundamental AI research

## Scale & Stage

Private, valued at ~$32B after raising $2B in April 2025 (up from $5B
in September 2024). Access to Google TPU chips through Alphabet
partnership. Key investors include Greenoaks Capital, Alphabet, NVIDIA,
Andreessen Horowitz, Lightspeed, and DST Global. Small team by design;
no commercial products.

## Hiring

Extremely selective. The company is research-focused with no commercial
product pressure, meaning it hires only researchers and engineers
directly contributing to the superintelligence mission. Expect world-
class technical bar requiring deep expertise in ML research, safety, or
large-scale systems.

Given the singular focus and massive funding relative to headcount,
SSI can offer exceptional compensation. Candidates should be motivated
by the safety-first superintelligence mission rather than product
building or commercial applications.

## Recent Context

Raised $2B at $32B valuation in early 2025, a 6x increase from its
September 2024 valuation. In July 2025, Ilya Sutskever took over as CEO
after Meta recruited Daniel Gross. Secured access to Google TPU chips
through an Alphabet partnership.
