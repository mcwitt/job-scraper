# CoreWeave

## Overview

GPU cloud infrastructure provider specializing in large-scale
NVIDIA GPU clusters for AI/ML workloads. Pivoted from crypto
mining to AI cloud. Headquartered in Livingston, NJ with data
centers across the US and expanding internationally.

## Technical Focus

- Large-scale GPU cluster deployment and management
- Kubernetes-based cloud orchestration
- High-performance networking (InfiniBand, RoCE)
- AI/ML infrastructure and model serving
- Data center design and operations

## Scale & Stage

Private. Valued at $19B+. ~1000+ employees. Massive capital
expenditure on GPU infrastructure, backed by significant debt
and equity financing. Revenue from GPU cloud contracts with AI
labs and enterprises. IPO expected.

## Hiring

Rapid hiring across infrastructure, DevOps, networking, and
platform engineering. Looking for experience with large-scale
systems, Kubernetes, and data center operations. Less research-
focused than AI labs — this is infrastructure at scale. Fast
growth means less process and more building.

## Recent Context

One of the largest buyers of NVIDIA GPUs outside hyperscalers.
Signing multi-billion-dollar contracts with AI companies for
compute capacity. Building new data centers rapidly. Key
infrastructure partner for companies that need GPU access
without building their own data centers.
