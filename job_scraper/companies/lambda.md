# Lambda

## Overview

GPU cloud provider offering on-demand and reserved NVIDIA GPU clusters for
AI training and inference. Started as a deep learning workstation company
and evolved into a full cloud infrastructure provider. Partners with Nvidia
and Microsoft for large-scale AI compute delivery.

## Technical Focus

- GPU cloud clusters (H100, B200) for training and inference
- Managed Slurm for distributed training workloads
- Cloud filesystem and S3-compatible storage
- Data center infrastructure ("AI factories") at gigawatt scale

## Scale & Stage

Private, Series E. Raised $1.5B Series E in November 2025 (led by TWG
Global) at $4B valuation. Over $2.4B total funding. ~700 employees.
Multi-billion dollar Microsoft partnership for AI infrastructure. Exploring
IPO in 2026.

## Hiring

Infrastructure and operations-heavy organization focused on data center
engineering, GPU cluster management, and cloud platform development.
Experience with large-scale GPU deployments, networking (InfiniBand/RoCE),
and distributed storage systems is highly valued.

Also hiring across product, sales, and cloud engineering as the company
scales from mid-size clusters to gigawatt-scale AI factories. The IPO
trajectory and Microsoft partnership are driving growth across all functions.

## Recent Context

Raised over $2B in 2025 alone and is exploring an IPO targeting at least
$350M. Building gigawatt-scale AI factories starting with a Kansas City
facility (early 2026) and expanding through an EdgeConneX partnership in
Chicago and Atlanta. Signed a multi-billion dollar deal with Microsoft to
supply AI infrastructure using tens of thousands of Nvidia GPUs.
