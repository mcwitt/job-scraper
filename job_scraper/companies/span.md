# Span

## Overview

Span builds smart electrical panels that replace traditional home breaker
panels with intelligent, app-controlled hardware enabling whole-home energy
monitoring, circuit-level control, and seamless integration with solar, EVs,
and batteries. Headquartered in San Francisco, founded by former Tesla Head
of Products Arch Rao.

## Technical Focus

- Smart electrical panel hardware (embedded systems, power electronics)
- Home energy management software and mobile apps
- Solar, battery, and EV charger integration
- Circuit-level energy monitoring and load management
- IoT connectivity and cloud platform

## Scale & Stage

Private, raised approximately $490M total including a $176M Series C and
$75M strategic investment from Eaton (March 2026). Around 340 employees.
Founded 2018. Products sold through electricians, homebuilders, and now
Eaton's distribution network.

## Hiring

Span hires across hardware engineering (power electronics, embedded
systems), software (mobile, cloud, firmware), and go-to-market roles.
The Tesla DNA in the founding team sets a high bar for product quality and
engineering rigor. Experience with consumer hardware, energy systems, or
IoT platforms is valued.

The company operates at the intersection of hardware and software, so
full-stack thinking is important. As Span scales distribution through the
Eaton partnership, roles in manufacturing, supply chain, and channel
operations are growing alongside core engineering.

## Recent Context

In March 2026, Eaton invested $75M in Span and announced a strategic
partnership to co-brand smart panels through Eaton's distribution network,
with joint solutions expected in Q2 2026. Span continues to expand its
installer network and push into new construction markets alongside its
retrofit business.
