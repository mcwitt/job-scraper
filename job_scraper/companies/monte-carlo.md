# Monte Carlo

## Overview

Monte Carlo provides a data observability platform
that monitors data pipelines for freshness, volume,
schema, and distribution anomalies, helping teams
detect and resolve data quality issues. Founded in
2019 by Barr Moses (CEO), Lior Gavish (CTO), Itay
Bleier, and Jordan Van Horn, the company is
headquartered in San Francisco, CA.

## Technical Focus

- Automated data quality monitoring and anomaly
  detection
- End-to-end data lineage and impact analysis
- Schema change tracking and alerting
- Integration with warehouses (Snowflake, BigQuery,
  Databricks, Redshift)
- AI agent observability across data + AI stacks

## Scale & Stage

Private, Series E ($135M in Oct 2025) led by Accel,
ICONIQ Growth, and Redpoint at a $1.6B valuation.
Total raised ~$236M. Approximately 130 employees.
Founded 2019.

## Hiring

Monte Carlo hires software engineers, data engineers,
and go-to-market roles. The engineering team builds
observability infrastructure that operates across
diverse warehouse and pipeline environments. Based
in San Francisco with remote roles available.

## Recent Context

In October 2025, Monte Carlo raised a $135M Series E
at a $1.6B unicorn valuation. In September 2025, the
company launched Agent Observability for end-to-end
visibility across data and AI stacks. In August 2025,
Monte Carlo unveiled native Salesforce integrations
for AI-ready customer data. The company also launched
AI-powered observability agents in April 2025 to
accelerate monitoring and troubleshooting.
