# Profluent Bio

## Overview

AI-first protein design company building generative models
for programmable biology. Founded in 2022 by Ali Madani and
Alexander Meeske. Headquartered in Berkeley/Emeryville, CA.
Created OpenCRISPR-1, the first AI-generated gene editing
system, published in Nature (2024).

## Technical Focus

- Large language models for protein generation
- AI-designed CRISPR gene editors (OpenCRISPR)
- Protein Atlas: 115B+ unique protein sequences
- Scaling laws for protein design models
- Antibody design (OpenAntibodies)
- Wet lab validation of AI-generated proteins

## Scale & Stage

Private, Series B. Raised $106M Series B (Nov 2025) co-led
by Altimeter Capital and Bezos Expeditions, bringing total
funding to ~$150M. Approximately 50 employees.

## Hiring

Small, research-intensive team at the intersection of ML
and biology. Roles span ML research, protein engineering,
and computational biology. High bar for both AI and bio
expertise. Fast-moving startup culture with significant
individual impact.

## Recent Context

Published scaling laws for protein design in NeurIPS 2025
spotlight paper. OpenCRISPR-1 shows 95% reduction in
off-target editing vs SpCas9 with comparable on-target
activity. Open-sourced gene editor used by thousands of
researchers and pharma companies worldwide. Closed $106M
Series B in late 2025 to scale frontier AI models.
