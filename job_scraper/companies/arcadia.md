# Arcadia

## Overview

Arcadia operates a utility data platform that connects to over 9,500 utility
providers across 52 countries, providing standardized energy data access for
enterprises, cleantech companies, and sustainability teams. Headquartered in
Washington, DC, the company also runs community solar programs that expand
access to clean energy.

## Technical Focus

- Utility data API platform (Arc) with integrations to 9,500+ providers
- AI-powered data cleaning, normalization, and gap-filling
- Energy procurement and sustainability reporting tools
- Community solar program management
- Enterprise energy management and analytics

## Scale & Stage

Private, raised approximately $575M over 16 funding rounds including a
recent $50M growth round from Macquarie Asset Management plus a $30M credit
facility from J.P. Morgan. Over 1,100 employees. Manages data from 3M+
utility accounts. Founded 2014.

## Hiring

Arcadia hires across software engineering, data engineering, product, and
sales. The platform is data-intensive, so experience with large-scale data
pipelines, API design, and ML/AI for data quality is relevant. The company
also hires for community solar operations, energy market expertise, and
enterprise sales.

With 1,100+ employees Arcadia operates at significant scale for a climate
tech company. The engineering culture emphasizes building reliable, scalable
data infrastructure. Enterprise customers include major corporations
managing energy across thousands of facilities.

## Recent Context

In early 2025, Arcadia launched AI-powered enterprise solutions for energy
complexity management and partnered with Cority to automate utility data
for ESG compliance. The company secured $50M in growth financing from
Macquarie to scale operations and expand community solar programs
nationwide.
