# Nevoya

## Overview
Nevoya is an AI-powered electric trucking carrier operating
battery-electric trucks for medium-distance freight (≤250
miles). Founded 2024 by Sami Khan (CEO, former PE banker),
John Verdon (former Motive/Waymo), and Tom Atwood (CTO,
supply chain data scientist). HQ in Santa Monica, CA.

## Technical Focus
- Proprietary AI transportation management system (TMS)
  for route optimization, vehicle utilization, and energy
  consumption
- Predictive routing and real-time fleet optimization
- Electric fleet management and charging logistics
- Achieved cost parity with diesel trucks (2025)

## Scale & Stage
- Private; early seed stage
- $12.3M raised across 2 rounds:
  - $3M seed (November 2024): Third Sphere, Never Lift,
    RedBlue Capital
  - $9.3M seed (July 2025): led by Lowercarbon Capital;
    Floating Point, LMNT Ventures
- Serving 10 Fortune 500 companies in California
- Plans to scale to 100 electric trucks and expand to
  Texas and Arizona within 12 months

## Hiring
