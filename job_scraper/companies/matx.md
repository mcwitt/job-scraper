# MatX

## Overview

AI chip startup building purpose-built accelerators
optimized for large language models. Founded 2022 by
Reiner Pope (led AI software for Google TPUs) and Mike
Gunter (lead TPU hardware designer). HQ in Mountain
View, CA.

## Technical Focus

- LLM-optimized ASIC design (MatX One chip)
- Splittable systolic array architecture
- SRAM-first low-latency design with HBM for long context
- Chip design, verification, and compiler toolchains
- ML performance optimization and inference

## Scale & Stage

Private. $500M Series B (Feb 2026) led by Jane Street
and Situational Awareness LP, with Marvell, Spark
Capital, NFDG, and Stripe co-founders. Previous $100M
Series A led by Spark Capital. ~100 employees.

## Hiring

Aggressively scaling across silicon engineering, ML
research, compiler/software, and verification. All
roles in Mountain View. Roles include AI tooling
engineers, ML researchers, ASIC/SoC verification
engineers, and software engineers.

## Recent Context

$500M Series B is one of the largest raises for an AI
chip startup. MatX One chip targeting tape-out in 2026,
shipping 2027. Competing directly with Nvidia by
specializing exclusively in LLM workloads rather than
general-purpose GPU compute.
