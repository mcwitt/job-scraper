# Hex

## Overview

Hex builds an AI-powered data workspace that combines
SQL, Python, R, and no-code tools in collaborative
notebooks for data science and analytics. Founded in
2019 by Barry McCardel (CEO), Caitlin Colgrove (CTO),
and Glen Takahashi, all former Palantir engineers,
the company is headquartered in San Francisco, CA.
Customers include Anthropic, Reddit, Figma, Rivian,
Cisco, and the NBA.

## Technical Focus

- Collaborative data notebooks (SQL, Python, R)
- AI-assisted data analysis and code generation
- Interactive dashboards and data apps
- Integration with Snowflake, Databricks, BigQuery
- Version control and review workflows for analytics

## Scale & Stage

Private, Series C ($70M in May 2025) led by Avra,
with a16z, Amplify, Redpoint, Sequoia, and Snowflake
Ventures. Total raised ~$172M across 5 rounds.
Approximately 220 employees as of early 2026.
Founded 2019.

## Hiring

Hex hires software engineers, ML engineers, and
product designers. The team builds a browser-based
analytics IDE with real-time collaboration and AI
features. San Francisco headquarters with remote
roles available.

## Recent Context

In May 2025, Hex raised a $70M Series C to expand
its AI-powered analytics capabilities. The company
has invested heavily in AI features that assist
analysts with code generation, data exploration,
and visualization. Hex continues to grow its
enterprise customer base as organizations look for
modern alternatives to traditional BI tools.
