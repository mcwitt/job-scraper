# Verse

## Overview

Verse builds enterprise SaaS software that helps large organizations
understand, procure, and manage clean power. The company's AI-enabled
platform, Aria, allows corporate energy buyers to evaluate clean energy
goals, model procurement options, and develop least-cost clean energy
portfolios.

## Technical Focus

- AI-powered energy procurement and portfolio optimization (Aria platform)
- Power Purchase Agreement (PPA) market intelligence and forecasting
- Clean energy cost-benefit modeling and scenario analysis
- Carbon emissions tracking and reduction planning

## Scale & Stage

Private, early-stage. ~46 employees. Headquartered in San Francisco.
Raised $20.5M Series A led by GV (Google Ventures) with participation
from Coatue, CIV, and MCJ Collective (May 2024). Revenue of ~$9.2M
(2025).

## Hiring

Verse is a small, early-stage team where engineers have significant
ownership and direct product impact. The company hires across software
engineering, data science, and energy domain roles. Given the AI-first
product approach, candidates with ML/AI experience combined with energy
market knowledge are particularly valued.

As a GV-backed startup with fewer than 50 people, Verse offers a
high-growth environment with startup equity upside. The company's
focus on enterprise sales means engineering work involves complex data
pipelines, optimization algorithms, and polished customer-facing tools.

## Recent Context

In September 2025, Verse was recognized by Verdantix as a market leader
in PPA Market Intelligence and Forecasting. The company serves
commercial and industrial electricity buyers seeking to reduce both
costs and emissions, a market that continues to grow as corporate clean
energy commitments accelerate.
