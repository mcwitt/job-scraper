# InstaDeep

## Overview

AI research and engineering company specializing in
decision-making AI for enterprise applications.
Founded in 2014 in Tunis by Karim Beguir and Zohra
Slim. Acquired by BioNTech in 2023 for ~$680M, now
operating as a global subsidiary. Headquartered in
London with offices in Paris, Tunis, Lagos, Dubai,
and Cape Town.

## Technical Focus

- Reinforcement learning for decision-making systems
- AI for drug discovery and biological sequence design
- DeepChain platform for protein and DNA modeling
- Generative AI models for molecular biology
- Applications in logistics, chip design, and energy

## Scale & Stage

Wholly owned subsidiary of BioNTech (acquired 2023
for ~$680M in cash, shares, and milestones).
Approximately 430 employees across 4 continents.
Backed by BioNTech's resources including the Kyber
AI supercluster coming online in Paris.

## Hiring

Research-heavy team hiring AI/ML researchers,
reinforcement learning engineers, and computational
biologists. Presence across EMEA and growing. Values
strong publication record and applied ML experience.
Unique position combining cutting-edge AI research
with pharma-scale resources from BioNTech.

## Recent Context

Released foundational generative AI models for
proteins and DNA on the DeepChain platform. Made
first NeurIPS oral presentation in 2025 on
inference-time strategies for RL. Building the
Kyber supercluster in Paris for training proprietary
foundation models.
