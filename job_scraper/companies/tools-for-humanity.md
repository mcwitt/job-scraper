# Tools for Humanity

## Overview

Building human-verification infrastructure for the age
of AI. Co-founded 2019 by Sam Altman (chairman) and
Alex Blania (CEO). HQ in San Francisco; offices in
Munich, London, Tokyo, Buenos Aires, Seoul, Singapore.
Formerly known as the Worldcoin project.

## Technical Focus

- Orb biometric device — iris-scanning hardware for
  proof-of-personhood
- World ID — privacy-preserving digital identity
- World App — "super app" with crypto pay, encrypted
  chat, and token transfers
- World Chain — dedicated L2 blockchain
- Orb Mini — handheld self-service verification device

## Scale & Stage

- Private; last reported valuation ~$3B
- Raised ~$250M+ total (including $115M Series C, 2023)
- Investors: a16z, Bain Capital, Blockchain Capital
- ~33M World App users, ~15M verified (Sep 2025)
- 60+ open roles across engineering, ops, legal, data
