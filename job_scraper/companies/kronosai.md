# KronosAI

## Overview

AI startup building foundation models for multiphysics simulation.
Platform combines frontier AI models with GPU-optimized solvers to
enable engineers to run high-fidelity simulations through natural
language interfaces, compressing design cycles from weeks to minutes.

## Technical Focus

- AI-powered multiphysics simulation (electromagnetics, acoustics, mechanics)
- Foundation models that transfer between physics domains without retraining
- GPU-optimized solvers and AI surrogates for inverse design
- 3D full-wave solver for photonic design space exploration
- 100% cloud-based infrastructure for parallel simulations

## Scale & Stage

Private, seed stage. Based in Palo Alto, CA. 2-10 employees. Funding
amount undisclosed. Early-stage company targeting industries including
semiconductors, oil & gas, healthcare, aerodynamics, and geophysics.

## Hiring

Very early-stage startup — expect to wear many hats and work directly
with founders. Hiring for generative AI engineering roles. Given the
small team size and technical depth of the product, candidates likely
need strong backgrounds in ML/AI, physics simulation, or scientific
computing.

As a seed-stage company, roles will involve significant greenfield
development and research. High tolerance for ambiguity and strong
self-direction expected.

## Recent Context

Building out their "Orb" AI platform combining frontier models ("The
Brain") with GPU-optimized solvers ("The Muscle") for autonomous
multiphysics simulation. Targeting industries where traditional
simulation methods are bottlenecked by manual PhD-level setup work.
