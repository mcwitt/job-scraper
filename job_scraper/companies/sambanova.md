# SambaNova

## Overview

AI hardware and systems company building purpose-built processors and
a full-stack platform for AI inference. Positions itself as a direct
alternative to Nvidia GPUs for enterprise generative AI deployments,
with a focus on agentic AI workloads.

## Technical Focus

- Custom AI accelerator chips (SN40, SN50)
- Reconfigurable dataflow architecture
- Three-tier memory hierarchy supporting 10T+ parameter models
- Full-stack AI platform (hardware + software)
- Enterprise inference optimization

## Scale & Stage

Private. Total funding ~$1.5B including $350M Series E (Feb 2026) led
by Vista Equity Partners with Intel Capital participation. ~400
employees. Record bookings and revenue through 2025.

## Hiring

Engineering-heavy organization with deep expertise in chip architecture,
compiler design, and AI systems. Looks for candidates with experience
in custom silicon, ML frameworks, and systems software. Stanford
connections are strong — founded by Stanford professors Kunle Olukotun
and Chris Re.

The Intel collaboration creates additional hiring needs around systems
integration and co-design. Roles span chip design, platform software,
ML engineering, and enterprise deployment.

## Recent Context

Announced SN50 chip (5x compute over SN40, up to 3.2 petaFLOPS at FP8)
with SoftBank as first customer, shipping later in 2026. Signed
multi-year Intel collaboration for GPU-alternative AI inference systems.
Abandoned earlier talks to acquire Intel, raising funding instead.
