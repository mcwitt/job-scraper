# Grid United

## Overview
Grid United develops next-generation interregional electric
transmission infrastructure across the US. Founded ~2021 by
energy veteran Michael Skelly, headquartered in Houston, TX.
Mission is to connect the fragmented Eastern, Western, and
ERCOT interconnections with long-distance, high-capacity
transmission lines.

## Technical Focus
- High-voltage DC (HVDC) and AC interregional transmission
- Long-distance linear infrastructure development
- Grid resilience, reliability, and congestion reduction
- Permitting, environmental review, and landowner relations
  for large-scale linear projects

## Scale & Stage
- Private; ~60+ employees
- Funding details not public
- Notable project: North Plains Connector (3,000 MW east-west
  line), in partnership with ALLETE and Portland General
  Electric (PGE joined 2024)

## Hiring
- Teams span development, engineering, environmental affairs,

- Houston-based with field roles across project corridors
- Small, experienced team; hires seasoned infrastructure and
