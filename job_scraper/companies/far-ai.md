# FAR AI

## Overview

AI alignment research nonprofit focused on ensuring
advanced AI systems are safe and beneficial. Founded in
2022 by Adam Gleave (CEO) and Karl Berzins. Gleave holds
a PhD from UC Berkeley under Stuart Russell and previously
worked at Google DeepMind. Headquartered in Berkeley,
California, where FAR AI operates a collaborative
co-working space for alignment researchers.

## Technical Focus

- AI robustness and adversarial testing
- Alignment of AI systems with human values
- Model evaluation methodology and safety benchmarks
- AI control research
- AI policy and technical governance

## Scale & Stage

501(c)(3) nonprofit, incorporated October 2022. ~20-50
employees. Funded by philanthropic grants. Operates
research programs and hosts conferences connecting
policymakers with AI technical experts.

## Hiring

Research-oriented roles in ML safety, alignment, and
evaluation. Values strong technical backgrounds in ML
with interest in safety. Berkeley location attracts
talent from UC Berkeley's AI ecosystem. Culture blends
academic research with applied safety engineering.

## Recent Context

Hosting London Alignment Workshop (March 2026) and
Technical Innovations in AI Policy Conference in Berkeley
(April 2026). Organizing ControlConf in Seoul (July 2026),
a conference dedicated to the emerging field of AI control.
Actively expanding programs equipping researchers and
organizations with tools for AI safety challenges.
