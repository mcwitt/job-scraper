# AMD

## Overview

Global semiconductor company designing CPUs, GPUs,
FPGAs, and adaptive computing products. Founded in
1969 by Jerry Sanders and colleagues from Fairchild
Semiconductor. Headquartered in Santa Clara, CA.
Led by CEO Lisa Su since 2014, who grew market cap
from ~$3B to over $200B.

## Technical Focus

- x86 CPUs (Ryzen consumer, EPYC server/HPC)
- AI accelerators (Instinct MI series GPUs)
- Discrete and integrated GPUs (Radeon)
- FPGAs and adaptive SoCs (from Xilinx acquisition)
- ROCm open software stack for GPU computing

## Scale & Stage

Public (NASDAQ: AMD). ~$26B annual revenue (2025).
Q3 2025 revenue $9.2B, up 36% YoY. Approximately
43,000 employees. Market cap over $200B. Targeting
>35% revenue CAGR and >35% non-GAAP operating
margin long-term.

## Hiring

Massive hiring across hardware design, AI/ML
software, GPU architecture, and data center
solutions. Roles span chip design, compiler and
runtime engineering, ML frameworks, and customer
solutions. Growing AI software ecosystem team to
compete with Nvidia's CUDA dominance.

## Recent Context

Announced strategic partnership with OpenAI to
deploy 6 GW of AMD GPUs, starting with MI450 in
H2 2026. MI350 series is fastest-ramping product
in company history. Oracle to offer first public
AMD Helios AI supercluster (50K GPUs) in Q3 2026.
AI GPU revenue projected $10-12B for 2026.
