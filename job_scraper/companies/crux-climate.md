# Crux Climate

## Overview
Crux is a capital markets platform for clean energy and
manufacturing finance, specializing in transferable tax
credits enabled by the Inflation Reduction Act. Founded
January 2023 by Alfred Johnson and Allen Kramer (previously
co-founded Mobilize). HQ not publicly listed; operates
remotely.

## Technical Focus
- Marketplace software for tax credit transaction matching
  between clean energy developers and buyers
- Price discovery and project diligence tools
- Financial reporting and compliance automation
- Transaction management and deal flow infrastructure

## Scale & Stage
- Private; Series B
- $77M+ raised:
  - Seed: $4.25M
  - Series A: $18.2M (January 2024, led by a16z)
  - Series B: $50M (April 2025, led by Lowercarbon Capital)
- ~95+ employees
- ~$20B in transferable tax credit volume managed (2025,
  2x YoY growth)
