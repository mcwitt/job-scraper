# Snorkel AI

## Overview

Enterprise AI data development platform. Originated as a
research project in the Stanford AI Lab in 2015 under
professor Chris Re. Co-founded by Alex Ratner (CEO),
Chris Re, Braden Hancock, Henry Ehrenberg, and Paroma
Varma. Spun out and incorporated in 2019. Headquartered
in Redwood City, California.

## Technical Focus

- Data-centric AI and programmatic data labeling
- Data programming (weak supervision via labeling
  functions)
- LLM fine-tuning and alignment data curation
- AI model evaluation and expert data-as-a-service
- Enterprise AI deployment for regulated industries
- Agentic AI environments and evaluation frameworks

## Scale & Stage

Private, unicorn. Series D ($100M, May 2025) led by
Addition with Prosperity 7, Greylock, and Lightspeed.
Total raised ~$238M at $1.3B valuation. Strategic
investors include Accenture, BNY, and QBE Ventures.
~200-300 employees. Revenue ~$148M in 2025.

## Hiring

Engineering roles span AI/ML, platform infrastructure,
and solutions engineering. Hiring senior engineers,
engineering managers, and frontier AI solutions
engineers. Culture emphasizes mentorship and open
communication. Hybrid schedule with no-meeting Tuesdays
and Thursdays. Quarterly hackathons.

## Recent Context
