# Terray Therapeutics

## Overview

Biotech startup integrating ultra-miniaturized high-throughput
chemistry with AI for small molecule drug discovery. Founded
in 2018 by Jacob Berlin (formerly an NIH-funded professor at
City of Hope) in Monrovia, California (northeast LA area).
The company's EMMI (Experimentation Meets Machine Intelligence)
platform combines proprietary nanoscale experimental hardware
with chemistry foundation models.

## Technical Focus

- Ultra-miniaturized wet lab hardware for massive-scale
  chemical experiments (billions of measurements)
- Chemistry foundation models trained on proprietary data
- Generative AI for de novo small molecule design
- Molecular potency prediction without pose estimation
- Integration of NVIDIA GPU acceleration for model training
- Automated high-throughput screening pipelines

## Scale & Stage

Private. Raised $226M total: $60M Series A (2022), $120M
Series B (2024, led by NVentures/NVIDIA and Bedford Ridge).
Strategic investment from NVIDIA. Approximately 120 employees.
50,000 sq ft headquarters in Monrovia, CA.

## Hiring

Roles span ML/AI, computational chemistry, wet lab science,
and platform engineering. The company emphasizes tight
integration between experiment and computation, so candidates
who bridge wet lab and ML are valued. Startup environment
with a scientific founder-CEO.

## Recent Context

Announced EMMI platform (Nov 2025) for de novo drug design.
Achieved first discovery milestone in multi-target BMS
collaboration. Aiming to advance first AI-discovered
candidate into the clinic by 2027. Active partnership
with NVIDIA for generative AI drug design.
