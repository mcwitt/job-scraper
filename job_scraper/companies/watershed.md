# Watershed

## Overview

Watershed is an enterprise sustainability platform that helps
companies measure, report, and reduce their greenhouse gas
emissions. Founded in 2019 by Taylor Francis, Avi Itskovich,
and Christian Anderson (all former Stripe). Headquartered in
San Francisco, with offices in New York and London.

## Technical Focus

- Carbon accounting and emissions measurement software
- AI-powered scope 3 / supply chain emissions analysis
- Sustainability reporting and regulatory disclosure tools
- Carbon removal and clean energy marketplace
- Open emissions factor databases (Open CEDA)

## Scale & Stage

Private, Series C. Raised $185M total from Sequoia Capital,
Kleiner Perkins, Greenoaks, and others. Valued at $1.8B.
~587 employees. Serves 800+ global businesses including 90
Fortune 500 companies and 5 of the 6 largest US banks.

## Hiring

Watershed hires across engineering, climate science, product,
and go-to-market. Engineering roles focus on data pipelines,
AI/ML for emissions modeling, and full-stack product work.
The team combines software engineers with climate scientists.
Dual US/Europe presence with remote flexibility. High bar
for candidates who combine technical depth with climate
domain interest.

## Recent Context

Launched Product Footprints (AI-powered scope 3 tool) in
September 2025 and Open CEDA (free global emissions factor
database covering 148 countries) in May 2025. Opened 2026
carbon removal RFP inviting project developers to supply
credits to its corporate buyer network. Acquired VitalMetrics
carbon data company to strengthen its data foundation. Now
the most valuable climate software company globally at $1.8B.
