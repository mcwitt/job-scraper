# d-Matrix

## Overview

AI chip startup developing inference accelerators using in-memory
compute architecture. Embeds processing directly into memory to
eliminate the memory wall bottleneck that limits GPU inference
performance, targeting 10x faster performance at 3x lower cost.

## Technical Focus

- In-memory compute architecture for AI inference
- Corsair inference accelerator cards (standard PCIe form factor)
- JetStream networking accelerators
- Aviator software stack
- Data center inference platform deployment

## Scale & Stage

Private. Series C of $275M (Nov 2025) at $2B valuation, total raised
~$450M. Backed by Microsoft, Temasek, and sovereign wealth funds from
Qatar and Singapore. Founded 2019, headquartered in Santa Clara.

## Hiring

Building out silicon, software, and systems teams for production
deployment. Looks for candidates with experience in chip architecture,
memory systems, ML compilers, and data center infrastructure. The
in-memory compute approach requires expertise that spans traditional
boundaries between memory and compute design.

Strong investor backing and enterprise partnerships (including
Microsoft) signal a company transitioning from R&D to production
scale, creating roles across engineering, deployment, and customer
support.

## Recent Context

Became a unicorn in 2025 with the $275M Series C. Claims 10x faster
performance, 3x lower cost, and 3-5x better energy efficiency versus
GPU-based inference. Planning large-scale data center deployments and
global expansion with the new funding.
