# TensorWave

## Overview

AMD-powered GPU cloud infrastructure for AI training and
inference. Founded December 2023 by Darrick Horton (CEO),
Jeff Tatarchuk (CGO), and Piotr Tomasik (President/COO).
Headquartered in Las Vegas, Nevada.

## Technical Focus

- AMD Instinct MI325X GPU clusters for AI workloads
- Large-scale AI training infrastructure
- GPU cloud compute (alternative to Nvidia-centric clouds)
- High-performance computing (HPC) services
- Telecom AI applications

## Scale & Stage

Private, Series A. Raised $143M total: $43M seed (Oct
2024, largest in Nevada history) plus $100M Series A
(May 2025) co-led by Magnetar and AMD Ventures, with
Prosperity7 (Aramco). Deploying 8,000+ MI325X GPUs.
On track for $100M+ run-rate revenue (20x YoY growth).

## Hiring

Engineering-heavy team building cloud infrastructure
at scale. Roles likely span GPU cluster management,
systems engineering, ML ops, and platform development.
Fast-growing startup culture — went from founding to
$100M revenue run-rate in under two years.

## Recent Context

Closed $100M Series A in May 2025 to scale AMD GPU
deployments. Joined the global telecom AI push in 2025.
Positioned as the leading AMD-first cloud alternative
to Nvidia-centric providers like CoreWeave and Lambda.
Rapid growth driven by GPU supply shortage and AMD's
improving AI software ecosystem.
