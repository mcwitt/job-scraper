# Intersect Power

## Overview

Intersect Power develops utility-scale clean energy
infrastructure including solar, battery storage, green
hydrogen, and co-located data centers. Founded in 2016 by
Sheldon Kimber (CEO), who previously worked in gas-fired
power development at Calpine and in investment banking at
Goldman Sachs. Headquartered in San Francisco, CA (140 New
Montgomery St, opened 2025).

## Technical Focus

- Utility-scale solar project development
- Battery energy storage systems
- Green hydrogen production
- Co-located data center and power generation
- Carbon capture infrastructure
- Large-scale power purchase agreements

## Scale & Stage

Being acquired by Alphabet for $4.75B cash plus debt
assumption (announced Dec 2025, expected to close H1 2026).
Previously raised ~$10.8B total including ~$800M round in
Dec 2024 led by TPG Rise Climate and Google. Manages over
10.8 GW of projects operating, under construction, or in
development. Approximately 360 employees. Key investors
include TPG Rise Climate, Climate Adaptive Infrastructure,
and Greenbelt Capital Partners.

## Hiring

Intersect hires across project development, engineering,
finance, and operations. The company values people who
"turn complexity into execution when the stakes matter
most." Offers unlimited PTO, two company-wide vacation
weeks, and up to 12 weeks paid parental leave (18 for
birth parents). No return-to-office mandate; voluntary
relocation program. Glassdoor rating of 3.1/5. Post-
acquisition, operations will remain under the Intersect
brand led by Kimber.

## Recent Context

Alphabet announced acquisition of Intersect for $4.75B in
Dec 2025, driven by need for clean energy to power AI data
centers. A co-located data center and power site is under
construction in Haskell County, TX. Intersect's existing
operating assets in TX and CA are excluded from the deal
and will continue independently. Kimber was honored by
Goldman Sachs for climate entrepreneurship in Oct 2024.
Breaking ground on ~$9B in new projects through 2025-2026.
