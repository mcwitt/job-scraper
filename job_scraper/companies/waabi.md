# Waabi

## Overview

Autonomous driving company building end-to-end AI for
trucks and robotaxis. Founded 2021 by Raquel Urtasun
(former chief scientist at Uber ATG, U of T CS professor).
HQ in Toronto with offices in San Francisco, Dallas, and
Pittsburgh.

## Technical Focus

- End-to-end AI (single model for perception, navigation,
  and control)
- Lidar + camera sensor fusion
- Large-scale simulation for training and testing
- L4 autonomous trucking
- Robotaxi deployment (partnership with Uber)

## Scale & Stage

Private, Series C ($750M from Khosla Ventures and G2
Venture Partners + $250M from Uber, Jan 2026). Total
funding ~$1B+. Valued at ~$3B. ~260 employees. Investors
include NVIDIA NVentures, Volvo Group Venture Capital,
Porsche, BlackRock, Radical Ventures.

## Hiring

Roles span ML/research scientists, software engineers,
systems engineers, and vehicle operators. US salary ranges
$109K-$269K for engineering. Offices in Toronto, SF,
Dallas, Pittsburgh.

## Recent Context

$1B raise in Jan 2026, largest in Canadian history.
Exclusive deal with Uber to deploy 25,000+ robotaxis.
Autonomous trucks integrated with Volvo fleet in Texas,
Norway, Sweden (with safety drivers). CNBC Disruptor 50
in 2025.
