# Renew Home

## Overview

Renew Home operates North America's largest residential
virtual power plant (VPP) platform, managing home energy use
through smart devices like Nest thermostats. Formed in 2024
from the merger of Google Nest Renew and OhmConnect. CEO is
Ben Brown. Headquartered in Oakland, CA; the company is fully
remote with an annual offsite.

## Technical Focus

- Residential virtual power plant orchestration at scale
- AI-powered home energy management and optimization
- Smart thermostat integration (Nest ecosystem)
- Demand response and grid flexibility services
- Google Cloud partnership for VPP infrastructure

## Scale & Stage

Private. Backed by Sidewalk Infrastructure Partners (SIP),
which invested $100M and holds majority ownership. Google
retains a minority stake. Manages 5M+ households and ~3 GW
of residential capacity, targeting 50 GW by 2030.

## Hiring

Fully remote company with unlimited PTO, parental leave,
and home office stipend. Roles span software engineering,
energy markets, data science, and product. Mission-driven
culture focused on expanding clean energy access and making
it more affordable. Values curiosity, solution-orientation,
and cross-functional collaboration.

## Recent Context

Launched Renew Home VPP platform in March 2025, enabling
energy providers to deploy residential demand flexibility
at scale. Announced partnership with NRG Energy in late 2025
to build a ~1 GW AI-powered VPP in Texas by distributing
hundreds of thousands of smart thermostats over the next
decade, powered by Google Cloud technology.
