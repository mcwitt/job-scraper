# YouTube

## Overview

World's largest video-sharing platform, a subsidiary of Google
(Alphabet). Over 2 billion logged-in users monthly and 200 billion
daily Shorts views as of 2026. Headquartered in San Bruno, CA.
CEO Neal Mohan.

## Technical Focus

- Recommendation and ranking systems (separate engines for long-form,
  Shorts, and search)
- Large-scale video processing, transcoding, and serving infrastructure
- Content moderation and trust & safety at scale (ML classifiers,
  policy enforcement)
- Creator tools and monetization platform
- Ads infrastructure and real-time bidding systems
- Living room / TV experience and multi-device playback

## Scale & Stage

Subsidiary of Alphabet (public, GOOGL). ~7,000+ employees.
YouTube ad revenue was $36B+ in 2024. YouTube Premium, TV, and
Music are growing subscription revenue lines.

## Hiring

YouTube roles are posted on Google's careers page with company
field set to "YouTube." Engineering roles typically require strong
systems, ML, or infrastructure backgrounds. Hiring bar is the
standard Google interview process. Roles span recommendations,
infrastructure, ads, trust & safety, frontend/mobile, and creator
platform engineering.

## Recent Context

Shorts expanded to 3-minute limit in 2025. Recommendation engine
for Shorts now fully separated from long-form, ranking on
satisfaction surveys, swipe-through rate, loop rate, and shares
rather than raw watch time. Continued investment in living room
experience and AI-powered content features.
