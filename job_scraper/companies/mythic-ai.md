# Mythic AI

## Overview

Semiconductor company developing analog compute-in-memory AI chips that
perform inference directly in flash memory arrays. Founded in 2012 in
Austin, TX. Mythic's analog processing units (APUs) represent data as
continuous electrical signals rather than discrete digital values, enabling
dramatically lower power consumption for AI workloads.

## Technical Focus

- Analog compute-in-memory chip architecture
- Energy-efficient AI inference (claims 100x perf/watt vs GPUs)
- Edge AI hardware for latency-sensitive and power-constrained deployments
- Hybrid digital-analog processing for neural network acceleration

## Scale & Stage

Private, late-stage. Raised $125M in December 2025 led by DCVC ($266M
total). ~65 employees. Strategic investors include Honda Motors and Lockheed
Martin, alongside NEA, Softbank KR, Future Ventures, and others.

## Hiring

Hardware-focused organization with deep expertise in analog circuit design,
semiconductor architecture, and embedded AI. Hiring primarily chip
designers, verification engineers, and ML engineers with experience in
efficient inference, model optimization for custom silicon, or analog/mixed-
signal design.

Small team building novel silicon from the ground up. Expect a high
technical bar in hardware design and a strong emphasis on power efficiency
as the core differentiator.

## Recent Context

Raised $125M oversubscribed round in December 2025 to scale production and
challenge Nvidia's dominance in AI compute. Positioning the APU as a
solution to AI's energy consumption problem at both data center and edge
scale. The round attracted significant strategic interest from automotive
(Honda) and defense (Lockheed Martin) sectors.
