# Lemurian Labs

## Overview

AI infrastructure company building hardware-agnostic compiler software
for AI workloads. Originally started as an AI chip company with a
logarithmic number scheme for efficient inference, then pivoted to
solving the AI software portability problem across different hardware
platforms.

## Technical Focus

- Tachyon compiler: write-once, run-anywhere for AI models
- Cross-platform AI model portability (Nvidia, AMD, Intel)
- Inference cost optimization (60-80% cost reduction claims)
- Hardware-agnostic AI software stack
- Compiler and runtime optimization for diverse accelerators

## Scale & Stage

Private. Series A of $28M (Dec 2025) co-led by Pebblebed Ventures and
Hexagon, with Oval Park Capital. Earlier stage — Tachyon compiler
entering beta in summer 2026. Small but growing engineering team.

## Hiring

Compiler and systems engineering talent is core. Backgrounds in LLVM,
MLIR, ML compilers (XLA, TVM, Triton), GPU programming, and
performance optimization are directly relevant. The pivot from hardware
to software suggests a pragmatic engineering culture.

Expanding engineering team with new funding. Roles likely span compiler
development, runtime systems, ML framework integration, and
