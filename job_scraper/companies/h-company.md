# H Company

## Overview

Paris-based AI lab building enterprise agentic AI models and agents for
automating complex workflows. Founded in 2023 by former DeepMind
researchers including Laurent Sifre, with a focus on autonomous AI
agents that can operate in real-world environments.

## Technical Focus

- Agentic AI models for enterprise workflow automation
- Vision-language models for web and browser automation
- Open-source browser agents (Surfer-H-CLI, Holo-1)
- Runner H: agentic API platform combining LLM and VLM
- Enterprise deployment with security focus (Mithril Security acquisition)

## Scale & Stage

Private, seed stage. Raised $220M seed round in May 2024, the largest
AI seed round in Europe at the time. ~50-100 employees. Paris
headquartered. Investors include prominent European and global VCs.

## Hiring

Small team of primarily senior researchers and engineers with roots in
DeepMind and other top AI labs. High research bar with emphasis on
agentic AI, vision-language models, and autonomous systems. The company
competes with US labs for top European AI talent.

Leadership change in June 2025 brought in Gautier Cloix (ex-Palantir
France) as CEO, signaling a pivot toward enterprise deployment with
"forward deployed engineers" in the Palantir mold. Candidates with both
research depth and production engineering skills are likely preferred.

## Recent Context

Underwent significant leadership and strategic changes in 2025: new CEO
from Palantir France in June, acquired Mithril Security in May, and
open-sourced the Holo-1 3B-parameter model and Surfer-H-CLI browser
agent in mid-2025. Achieved 92.2% on the WebVoyager benchmark with
their browser automation stack.
