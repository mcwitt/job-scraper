# Electric Hydrogen

## Overview

Electric Hydrogen builds large-scale PEM electrolyzers for producing green
hydrogen from water and renewable electricity. Founded in 2020 and
headquartered in Devens, MA, the company designs high-efficiency, low-cost
electrolyzer systems at the 100 MW scale for industrial decarbonization.

## Technical Focus

- Proton exchange membrane (PEM) electrolysis
- 100 MW-scale electrolyzer system design (HYPRPlant)
- Electrolyzer stack manufacturing
- Power electronics and balance of plant
- Industrial hydrogen integration (e-fuels, refining, ammonia)

## Scale & Stage

Private, reached unicorn status in 2023. Raised approximately $800M total
including a $380M Series C. Operates a manufacturing facility in Devens, MA.
Around 360 employees. Investors include Fifth Wall, Fortescue, Energy Impact
Partners, and Amazon. Founded 2020.

## Hiring

Electric Hydrogen hires heavily in electrochemical engineering, power
electronics, manufacturing, and systems integration. The company values
candidates who can operate at the intersection of deep technical work and
manufacturing scale-up. Hardware experience with fuel cells, electrolyzers,
or related electrochemical systems is strongly preferred.

The culture skews toward urgency and execution given the rapid growth
trajectory. Roles span R&D, factory operations, project engineering, and
software for system controls. Prior startup or scale-up experience is a plus
given the pace of commercialization.

## Recent Context

In 2025, Electric Hydrogen secured contracts to supply electrolyzers to
Infinium and HIF Global for e-fuels projects. Despite broader green hydrogen
market headwinds, the company continues pressing forward with manufacturing
scale-up at its Devens facility and has emerged as one of the leading U.S.
electrolyzer manufacturers.
