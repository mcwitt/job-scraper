# Nosis Bio

## Overview

AI-enabled biotechnology company focused on designing and
developing targeted RNA medicines for challenging cell
types. Founded in 2021 by Chad Miller, Jim Martineau, and
Seth Myers. Headquartered in Berkeley, California. Uses
deep learning and novel multiplexed biochemistry to design
molecules that turn therapies into cell-targeted treatments.

## Technical Focus

- Connexa platform: AI-driven drug delivery design
- Cell-specific internalizing receptor target discovery
- Physics-aware ML and generative AI for drug design
- High-throughput in vivo data characterization
- Targeted RNA therapeutics (gene therapy delivery)

## Scale & Stage

Private, early stage. Funded by KdT Ventures, AlleyCorp,
Madrona Venture Group, and AWS Startups. Received $2M
from Cystic Fibrosis Foundation for gene therapy delivery
research. ~12 employees. Lead fibrosis program entered
IND-enabling studies in 2025. Founded 2021.

## Hiring

Small team hiring across computational biology, ML/AI,
and wet-lab biology. Roles emphasize intersection of
machine learning and drug delivery science. Values
candidates who can bridge computational and experimental
work. Early-stage startup culture with high individual
impact.

## Recent Context

Strategic collaboration with Johnson & Johnson combining
J&J clinical development expertise with Nosis' Connexa
platform. Research collaboration and option agreement
with Daiichi Sankyo for cell-targeted RNA medicines.
Lead fibrosis program anticipating first-in-human trial
in 2026. Presented at BIO International Convention 2025.
