# Amazon

## Overview

Global technology and e-commerce giant operating the world's
largest online marketplace, AWS cloud platform, and consumer
devices (Alexa, Kindle, Fire). Founded in 1994 by Jeff Bezos
in Seattle, WA. Now led by CEO Andy Jassy. Headquartered in
Seattle and Arlington, VA (HQ2).

## Technical Focus

- Cloud computing: AWS (29% global cloud market share),
  including compute, storage, databases, and AI services
- AI/ML: Amazon Bedrock, Nova foundation models, custom
  Trainium and Inferentia chips (Annapurna Labs)
- Robotics: 1M+ warehouse robots deployed; Vulcan, Blue
  Jay, and Project Eluna AI coordination systems
- E-commerce: search, recommendations, supply chain
  optimization, and logistics at massive scale
- Voice AI and smart devices (Alexa ecosystem)

## Scale & Stage

Public (NASDAQ: AMZN). ~1.56M employees. FY2025 revenue
$717B (up ~14% YoY). AWS generated $35.6B in Q4 2025
alone and accounts for the majority of operating profit.
Planning ~$200B capex in 2026, nearly all for AWS and
AI infrastructure.

## Hiring

Massive engineering org spanning AWS, retail, robotics,
devices, and research. Bar raiser interview process:
4-6 rounds including system design and behavioral loops
grounded in Leadership Principles (16 tenets). Strong
emphasis on ownership, bias for action, and customer
obsession. Language-agnostic but Java, Python, and
TypeScript dominate internally.

## Recent Context

Launched Amazon Nova 2 Sonic speech-to-speech model and
reinforcement fine-tuning for Bedrock at re:Invent 2025.
Expanding Annapurna Labs custom silicon hiring for ML
accelerator and compiler roles. Ramping AI infrastructure
capacity -- added ~4 GW of compute in 2025 with plans
to double by end of 2027.
