# PhysicsX

## Overview

AI company that accelerates engineering simulation and design by combining
deep learning with multiphysics modeling. Founded in 2019 by former Formula
1 engineers, the platform dramatically reduces physical prototyping needs
across semiconductors, aerospace, automotive, renewables, and materials
processing.

## Technical Focus

- AI-driven multiphysics simulation (fluid dynamics, thermal, structural)
- Physics foundation models for engineering design
- Generative design and optimization
- Simulation data management and model training pipelines
- Agentic AI applications for engineering workflows

## Scale & Stage

Private. Raised $155M+ Series B (June 2025) with NVIDIA NVentures
extending the round, pushing valuation near unicorn status. NVIDIA
retains option to invest another $80M in the next raise. Previously
raised $32M coming out of stealth in 2023. Customers include Rio Tinto,
Siemens, and Applied Materials.

## Hiring

Technical bar emphasizes deep expertise in physics simulation,
numerical methods, and ML. Roles span simulation engineering, ML
research, and platform development. The company values candidates who
can bridge physics domain knowledge with modern AI techniques.

Formula 1 engineering DNA means a culture of precision, speed, and
iterative optimization. Growing rapidly post-Series B with strong
enterprise customer base providing real-world engineering problems at
scale.
