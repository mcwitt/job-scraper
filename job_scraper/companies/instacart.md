# Instacart

## Overview

Online grocery delivery and retail technology platform
operating across the U.S. and Canada. Connects customers
with personal shoppers who pick, pack, and deliver from
local retailers. Also provides white-label e-commerce
(Storefront Pro) and in-store tech (Caper smart carts)
to grocers. Founded 2012 by Apoorva Mehta, Max Mullen,
and Brandon Leonardo. HQ in San Francisco.

## Technical Focus

- AI-powered smart carts (Caper) with cameras, sensors,
  and on-device item recognition
- Storefront Pro white-label e-commerce platform powering
  380+ retailer sites
- Retail media and ads platform with low-latency serving
  and AI-driven targeting
- Data Hub clean room for CPG first-party data analytics
- AI-assisted engineering tooling (Cursor, internal Ava)
- Large-scale logistics and real-time fulfillment systems

## Scale & Stage

Public (Nasdaq: CART) since September 2023 IPO ($660M
raised). ~3,300 corporate employees plus 600K+ gig
shoppers. FY 2024 revenue ~$3.3B (+11% YoY); trailing
twelve-month revenue ~$3.7B. Market cap ~$9.6B as of
February 2026. CEO Chris Rogers (since August 2025).

## Hiring

Remote-first engineering culture; employees choose where
they work with periodic in-person gatherings. Teams span
infrastructure, retailer storefronts, ads, data science,
and Caper hardware/software. Strong focus on AI adoption
across engineering workflows.

## Recent Context

Launched Data Hub clean room product at CES 2026. Expanded
Allegiance Retail Services partnership for omnichannel
tech across 125+ stores (January 2026). Partnered with
Fareway for Storefront Pro e-commerce (March 2026). Added
non-grocery delivery with Lush Cosmetics and 1-800-Flowers
(February 2026). Beat Q4 2025 revenue expectations with
optimistic 2026 guidance.
