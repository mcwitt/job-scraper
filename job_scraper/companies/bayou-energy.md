# Bayou Energy

## Overview

Bayou Energy builds API-based software that gives clean energy companies
instant access to customer utility billing and usage data. The platform
automates interactions with utility web portals and provides real-time
energy consumption data, enabling solar, EV, heat pump, and battery
companies to streamline customer onboarding and billing.

## Technical Focus

- Utility data API platform (billing, usage, real-time consumption)
- Automated utility portal integration across U.S. utilities
- Fintech tools for clean energy billing
- Customer onboarding automation for energy startups

## Scale & Stage

Private, early stage. Raised $1.2M pre-seed from CoFound Partners, Leap
Forward Ventures, Stepchange, Surface Ventures, and Very Serious Ventures.
Founded in 2022. Small team distributed across Seattle, NYC, and Pittsburgh.

## Hiring

As an early-stage startup, Bayou Energy likely hires generalist engineers
comfortable building across the full stack, with emphasis on API design,
data integration, and working with messy real-world utility data sources.

Early-stage culture means wearing many hats. Candidates who thrive in
ambiguous environments and can ship quickly with minimal process will fit
best. Domain knowledge of utility systems or energy data is a plus but
not required.

## Recent Context

Bayou Energy is focused on scaling its utility data API platform to serve
the growing clean energy deployment market. The company targets startups
deploying distributed energy resources that need reliable access to
customer utility data for billing and analytics.
