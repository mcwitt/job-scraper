# Isomorphic Labs

## Overview

AI-driven drug discovery company founded by Demis Hassabis in
2021 as an Alphabet subsidiary. Applying deep learning methods
derived from AlphaFold to pharmaceutical drug design. Based in
London.

## Technical Focus

- AI for molecular design and drug discovery
- Protein-ligand interaction prediction
- Molecular dynamics and simulation
- AlphaFold-derived structural biology methods
- Computational chemistry and pharmacology

## Scale & Stage

Alphabet subsidiary. Early stage (~200 employees). Funded by
Alphabet with partnerships with major pharma companies (Eli
Lilly, Novartis). Pre-revenue, building drug discovery pipeline.

## Hiring

Hiring ML researchers, computational chemists, and software
engineers. Domain expertise in structural biology, drug design,
or molecular modeling is a strong differentiator. Engineering
roles support the ML and science platform. Compensation
competitive given Alphabet backing.

Good fit for researchers interested in applying AI to drug
discovery with access to world-class ML infrastructure and
scientific talent.

## Recent Context

Signed multi-billion-dollar partnerships with pharma companies.
Leveraging AlphaFold breakthroughs (Nobel Prize 2024) for
practical drug design. Building out pipeline with multiple
therapeutic targets.
