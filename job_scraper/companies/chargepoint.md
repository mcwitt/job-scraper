# ChargePoint

## Overview

ChargePoint designs, develops, and operates electric vehicle charging
infrastructure and cloud-based software for managing EV charging
networks. The company provides networked Level 2 and DC fast charging
solutions for commercial, fleet, and residential applications across
North America and Europe.

## Technical Focus

- Cloud-based charging management platform (real-time monitoring, pricing, analytics)
- Networked Level 2 and DC fast charging hardware
- Fleet management and energy management software
- API integrations for fleet operators, property managers, and utilities

## Scale & Stage

Public (NYSE: CHPT). ~1,500 employees. FY2026 Q3 revenue of $105.7M
(up 6% YoY), with subscription revenue growing 15% YoY to $42M. Raised
$554M in total funding prior to IPO. Reduced total outstanding debt by
$172M (over 50%) in late 2025.

## Hiring

ChargePoint hires across software engineering, hardware engineering,
cloud infrastructure, and product roles. As a public company navigating
a cost-conscious phase, hiring is selective and focused on roles that
directly support platform growth, subscription revenue, and operational
efficiency.

Strong candidates typically have experience in cloud platforms, IoT
systems, energy management, or embedded systems. The company values
hands-on engineers who can work across the stack, given the intersection
of hardware, firmware, and cloud software in their product.

## Recent Context

ChargePoint launched its next-generation ChargePoint Platform for
real-time insights and station management. The company was awarded its
third consecutive Sourcewell cooperative purchasing contract for public
agencies. Focus has shifted toward profitability and subscription
revenue growth after significant debt reduction in late 2025.
