# Oumi

## Overview

Open-source AI platform company building the "Linux of AI" --
a complete, unconditionally open platform for foundation model
development. Founded in 2024 by Emmanouil (Manos) Koukoumidis
(ex-Google Cloud AI, led Cloud PaLM efforts) and Oussama
Elachqar (ex-Apple ML). Headquartered in Bellevue, WA.

## Technical Focus

- End-to-end open-source foundation model platform
- Data preparation, training, evaluation, and deployment
- Text and multimodal model support at any scale
- Open weights, open data, open code, open collaboration

## Scale & Stage

Private, Seed stage. Raised $10M Seed in January 2025
led by Venrock and Obvious Ventures, with participation
from Plug & Play and Ascend. Small core team (~10-20)
of ML engineers and researchers from Google Gemini,
Google AI Safety, and Apple Intelligence.

## Hiring

Research-oriented team with deep industry ML experience.
Hiring ML engineers and researchers comfortable with
open-source development and large-scale model training.
Expect a strong research background bar given the
founders' pedigree at Google and Apple.

## Recent Context

Came out of stealth in January 2025 with backing from
major universities and research institutions. Positioned
as an alternative to closed AI development platforms,
emphasizing fully open collaboration across academia,
industry, and independent researchers.
