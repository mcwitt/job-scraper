# Amperon

## Overview

Amperon provides AI-powered energy demand and price forecasting for
utilities, grid operators, energy traders, and renewable energy
developers. The platform delivers real-time and mid-term forecasts
across electricity demand, pricing, wind, and solar generation in
markets globally.

## Technical Focus

- AI/ML-based electricity demand forecasting
- Weather-informed energy price forecasting (ERCOT, CAISO, etc.)
- Wind and solar generation forecasting
- Grid analytics and data platform (API-first)

## Scale & Stage

Private, growth-stage. Raised $35.7M total funding. Recent investors
include Samsung Ventures (January 2026), National Grid Partners, and
Acario (Tokyo Gas venture arm) in 2025. Active forecasts in 27
countries across North America, Europe, Australia, and the Middle East.

## Hiring

Amperon hires data scientists, ML engineers, software engineers, and
energy market analysts. The company's core product is forecasting
models, so strong candidates have experience in time-series prediction,
weather data, energy markets, or building production ML systems.

The team is relatively small with global ambitions, so engineers
tend to work across the full stack of data ingestion, model training,
and API delivery. Strategic investments from Samsung Ventures, National
Grid Partners, and Acario signal growing enterprise traction and
provide domain expertise connections.

## Recent Context

Amperon launched AI-powered real-time price forecasts for ERCOT in May
2025, weather-informed mid-term energy forecasts in September 2025, and
advanced wind forecasting in early 2025. Samsung Ventures invested in
January 2026, reflecting the company's expanding global footprint
across 27 countries.
