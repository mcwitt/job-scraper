# Zillow

## Overview

Online real estate marketplace for buying, selling,
renting, and financing homes. Founded in 2006 by Rich
Barton and Lloyd Frink. Headquartered in Seattle, WA.

## Technical Focus

- AI/ML for home valuation (Zestimate), pricing, and
  recommendations
- Natural language processing and conversational AI
- Large-scale data infrastructure (Spark, Kafka,
  Airflow)
- Full-stack product engineering (mobile and web)
- Microservices architecture at scale
- Computer vision for property imagery
- Mortgage and fintech platform engineering

## Scale & Stage

Public (Z/ZG). ~$2B annual revenue. ~6,500 employees.
Operates Zillow, Trulia, StreetEasy, and HotPads
brands. "Cloud HQ" distributed-first work model.

## Hiring

Engineering roles span software development, data
science, ML, and product design. Strong applied AI
focus — NLP, computer vision, language models. Large
mortgage/fintech engineering org. Distributed-first
with offices in Seattle, San Francisco, New York, and
international teams in Bengaluru and Mexico City.

## Recent Context

Operating under distributed "Cloud HQ" model built
on trust and flexibility. Investing in AI-powered
consumer experiences including conversational search
and enhanced Zestimate models. StreetEasy continues
as dedicated NYC real estate platform.
