# Anduril Industries

## Overview

Defense technology company building autonomous systems and
software for national security. Founded in 2017 by Palmer Luckey
(Oculus founder) and Brian Schimpf. Headquartered in Costa Mesa,
CA with offices across the US.

## Technical Focus

- Lattice: sensor fusion and command-and-control platform
- Autonomous drones and counter-UAS systems
- Computer vision and object detection at the edge
- Hardware-software integration for defense platforms
- Mesh networking and edge compute

## Scale & Stage

Private. Valued at $14B+. ~3000+ employees. Series F funding.
Revenue from US DoD and allied government contracts. Rapid
growth trajectory with expanding production capabilities.

## Hiring

Hiring aggressively across software, hardware, and systems
engineering. Security clearance required or obtainable for many
roles (US citizenship typically required). Engineering bar is
high — emphasis on systems programming, real-time systems,
embedded, and computer vision. Culture is fast-paced startup
despite defense context.

Strong fit for engineers motivated by defense/national security
mission who want startup speed with government-scale problems.

## Recent Context

Expanding production of autonomous systems. Growing
international partnerships with allied nations. Building
large-scale manufacturing capabilities. Competing with legacy
defense primes (Lockheed, Raytheon) on software-defined systems.
