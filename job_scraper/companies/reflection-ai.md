# Reflection AI

## Overview

AI company building open-weight foundation models and
autonomous coding agents, with the mission to build
"open superintelligence." Founded in March 2024 by Misha
Laskin (CEO, former reward modeling lead for DeepMind
Gemini) and Ioannis Antonoglou (CTO, co-creator of
AlphaGo). Headquartered in New York City with an office
in London.

## Technical Focus

- Open-weight frontier language models
- Reinforcement learning at scale for reasoning
- Autonomous coding agents (Asimov product)
- Multi-agent architectures for code comprehension
- Pre-training and post-training optimization

## Scale & Stage

Private, Series B. Raised ~$2.1B total: $25M seed and
$105M Series A (Mar 2025), $2B Series B at $8B valuation
(Oct 2025, led by NVIDIA at $800M). Reportedly raising
another $2B+ at ~$20B valuation (Mar 2026). Investors
include Sequoia Capital, Lightspeed, and Eric Schmidt.
~80-100 employees.

## Hiring

Research-heavy team recruited from DeepMind, OpenAI,
Google Brain, Meta FAIR, Character.AI, and Anthropic.
Roles skew toward deep RL and systems ML research,
infrastructure engineering, and product engineering.
Very high bar -- expects frontier lab pedigree or
equivalent demonstrated capability.

## Recent Context

Launched Asimov autonomous coding agent (Jul 2025),
which uses multi-agent retrieval over full codebases.
Asimov remains waitlist-only as of early 2026.
Open-weight frontier model has not yet been publicly
released. Revenue reportedly ~$20M in 2025. Positioned
as America's open-source answer to DeepSeek.
