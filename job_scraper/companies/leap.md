# Leap

## Overview

Leap is the leading platform for building and scaling virtual power plants
(VPPs). The company provides automated access to demand response and grid
services revenue for providers of battery storage, EV chargers, smart
building technologies, and other distributed energy resources (DERs),
connecting them to wholesale electricity markets.

## Technical Focus

- DER-to-market integration platform
- Virtual power plant orchestration and dispatch
- Wholesale electricity market APIs (CAISO, PJM, ERCOT, others)
- Real-time grid services and demand response automation
- Partner SDK/API for DER technology providers

## Scale & Stage

Private. Raised $59.6M through Series B (Nov 2023). ~120 employees,
headquartered in Sacramento, CA. Manages 367 MW of nominated capacity
across 400,000+ connected DERs with 100+ technology partners.

## Hiring

Leap hires software engineers, energy market analysts, and business
development professionals. Engineering roles focus on platform reliability,
market integration, and scaling the DER aggregation infrastructure.
Experience with energy markets, real-time systems, or API platform
development is valued.

The company operates at the intersection of energy and software, so
candidates who understand both electricity market mechanics and modern
software architecture tend to do well. Growth has been rapid (100% YoY
capacity increase), so the team values people who can build and iterate
quickly.

## Recent Context

In 2025, Leap achieved 100% year-over-year capacity growth, far outpacing
the industry average of 13.7%. The company expanded into PJM (the largest
U.S. wholesale electricity market) and broadened its California offerings.
Leap also partnered with Enel to connect more commercial and industrial
DERs to utility demand response programs.
