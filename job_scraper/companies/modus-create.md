# Modus Create

## Overview

Digital consulting and product development firm. Acquired
Tweag, a Haskell and Nix consulting company, in 2023.
Founded in 2011 by Jay Garcia and Pat Sheridan.
Headquartered in Reston, VA; fully remote workforce
distributed across 25+ countries.

## Technical Focus

- Haskell, Nix, and functional programming (via Tweag)
- GHC compiler contributions and Nix ecosystem work
- Cloud-native architecture and DevOps consulting
- Full-stack product development and digital transformation
- Open-source tooling and developer experience

## Scale & Stage

Private. ~400 employees globally. Bootstrapped, profitable
consulting business. Tweag team integrated as a practice
area focused on scientific computing and FP consulting.

## Hiring

Remote-first. Engineering roles span web/mobile consulting
and deep FP/Nix work (Tweag-heritage roles). Tweag roles
tend to look for Haskell, Nix, or formal methods experience
and often involve open-source contributions. Consulting
culture with client-facing project work.

## Recent Context

Continues Tweag's open-source contributions to GHC, Nix,
and Bazel. Active in Haskell and Nix communities. Tweag
brand still used for technical content and recruiting in
the FP space.
