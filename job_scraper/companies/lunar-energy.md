# Lunar Energy

## Overview

Lunar Energy designs integrated home battery systems and
virtual power plant (VPP) software to transition homes to
100% clean energy. Founded in 2020 by Kunal Girotra, a
former Tesla Energy executive. Headquartered in Mountain
View, California, with an office in London, UK. The Lunar
System pairs solar with an all-in-one battery, inverter,
and mobile app for home energy management.

## Technical Focus

- Residential battery and inverter hardware design
- GridShare VPP software platform (AI-powered)
- Distributed energy resource management
- Firmware and embedded systems engineering
- Grid services and demand response orchestration
- Home energy monitoring and mobile apps

## Scale & Stage

Private, Series D. Raised over $500M total, including a
$130M Series C (Activate Capital) and $102M Series D
(B Capital, Prelude Ventures) announced February 2026.
Other investors include Sunrun, SK Group, DCVC, Piva
Capital, Itochu, and Q Capital Partners. Approximately
250 employees, mostly hardware, firmware, and software
engineers. 650 MW of devices under management globally.

## Hiring

Lunar hires hardware, firmware, and software engineers
who are hands-on with both simulation and real hardware,
carrying products from concept through high-volume
production. The team has deep energy product experience.
Culture is collaborative and mission-driven, with a
focus on shipping real hardware at scale. Roles span
power electronics, embedded systems, cloud/platform
software, and VPP/grid integration engineering.

## Recent Context

In February 2026, Lunar raised $232M across Series C and
D rounds to scale manufacturing from current levels to
20,000 units by end of 2026 and 100,000 by 2028. The
company targets 50,000 home installations by end of 2026.
Sunrun uses Lunar's GridShare platform for distributed
power assets across 12+ US markets. Lunar claims to
operate the country's largest VPP, and is expanding its
AI-powered platform to utilities in Europe and Asia.
