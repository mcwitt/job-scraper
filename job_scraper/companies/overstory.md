# Overstory

## Overview

Overstory builds an AI-powered vegetation intelligence
platform that combines high-resolution satellite imagery
with proprietary models to help utilities prevent
wildfires, reduce outages, and manage grid risk.
Co-founded in 2018 by Indra den Bakker and Anniek
Schouten, the company is headquartered in Amsterdam with
operations across four continents.

## Technical Focus

- AI-driven vegetation risk assessment from satellite
  and aerial imagery
- Wildfire Intelligence with proprietary Fuel Detection
  Model
- Tree-level risk identification across utility networks
- Remote sensing and computer vision at scale
- Grid resilience analytics for utility corridors

## Scale & Stage

Private, raised $63M+ total including a $43M Series B
(Nov 2025, led by Blume Equity with Energy Impact
Partners). Approximately 130 employees across North
America, Europe, and Oceania. Partners with 40+ leading
utilities worldwide, including 4 of the top 10 U.S.
utilities. Founded 2018.

## Hiring

Overstory hires across machine learning, remote sensing,
geospatial engineering, and utility solutions. The
product requires deep expertise in satellite imagery
processing, computer vision, and wildfire science.
Experience with utility operations or vegetation
management is a plus. Distributed team with a strong
technical culture.

## Recent Context

Overstory closed a $43M Series B in November 2025 to
scale wildfire prevention and grid resilience globally.
The company launched its Wildfire Intelligence product
with a proprietary Fuel Detection Model and expanded to
serve six of the ten largest utilities in the Americas.
Wildfire carbon emissions have climbed 60%, driving
urgency for Overstory's offerings.
