# Unify

## Overview

AI-first go-to-market platform that combines intent
signals with outbound engagement automation. Helps
sales and growth teams identify buyers and reach them
at the right time. Co-founded by Austin Hughes (CEO)
and Connor Heggie (CTO) in early 2023. HQ in San
Francisco with a secondary office in New York.

## Technical Focus

- Intent signal detection (web traffic, product usage,
  job changes)
- Multi-channel outbound sequencing with AI
  personalization
- Workflow orchestration ("Plays") combining data
  signals and engagement actions
- AI agents for lead research and message generation
- B2B contact data enrichment and pipeline analytics
- Temporal-based workflow infrastructure

## Scale & Stage

Private, Series B. Raised $40M Series B led by Battery
Ventures (mid-2025), $12M Series A (Oct 2024), $6.6M
seed from OpenAI Startup Fund, Thrive Capital, and
Emergence Capital. ~$58.6M total raised. ~140
employees. Revenue grew 39x YoY seed-to-A, then 8x
YoY A-to-B.

## Hiring

Engineering-heavy hiring with emphasis on staff and
senior-level roles. Backend, platform, SRE, AI, and
growth engineering positions. Also hiring across sales,
customer success, and product. Small-but-senior team
culture with high leverage per engineer. Notable
customers include Cursor, Perplexity, and Together AI.
