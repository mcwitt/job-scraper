# Prefect

## Overview

Prefect builds an open-source workflow orchestration
framework for Python, along with Prefect Cloud, a
managed platform for scheduling, monitoring, and
automating data pipelines. Founded in 2018 by Jeremiah
Lowin, the company is headquartered in Washington, DC.
Prefect Cloud automates over 200 million data tasks
monthly for customers including Progressive Insurance
and Cash App.

## Technical Focus

- Python-native workflow orchestration framework
- Event-driven and scheduled task execution
- Hybrid execution model (cloud + self-hosted agents)
- Observability and alerting for data pipelines
- Infrastructure-as-code deployment patterns

## Scale & Stage

Private, Series B ($32M in Jun 2021) led by Tiger
Global, with Bessemer Venture Partners and others.
Total raised ~$47M across multiple rounds.
Approximately 137 employees. Founded 2018.

## Hiring

Prefect hires software engineers, developer advocates,
and platform engineers. The team builds both the
open-source Prefect framework and the managed cloud
product. Remote-friendly organization with a strong
open-source community focus.

## Recent Context

Prefect competes in the data orchestration space
alongside Dagster and Apache Airflow. The company
has been investing in AI workflow support and
event-driven orchestration capabilities. Prefect 3.x
continues to evolve the framework with improved
developer ergonomics and cloud-native features.
